What are Bookmarks?

Discord's bookmarks feature is here to transform how you manage your conversations via chat, direct messages (DM), and group chats. Available exclusively for Nitro users, this tool allows you to:

  • Easily save important messages for later reference
  • Organize your Discord content more efficiently
  • Manage your saved items in real-time, with the ability to save or unsave messages as needed
NOTE: Bookmarks have a storage limit of 200 messages.

Never miss crucial information again! With bookmarks, you can keep track of important conversations and come back to them whenever you need.

Bookmark a Message

NOTE: As this feature is experimental, the steps may vary slightly. 

Bookmarking messages allows you to bookmark important information for easy access later. Whether you're using Discord on desktop, browser, or mobile, here's how you can bookmark messages:

Desktop/Browser

1. On desktop or browser, navigate to the message you would like to save. 

2. Right-click the message and select the Bookmark Message option in the dropdown menu, then press Bookmark. 

3. Once bookmarked, you will see a notification badge letting you know that the message has been saved for later. 

4. To view your bookmarked messages, press the Inbox icon [] in the upper-right corner of the app and select the Bookmarks tab to find your messages. 

On desktop/browser: To convert a bookmarked message into a reminder or unsave it, press the Inbox icon [] > Bookmarks tab. Then select the desired option in the upper right-corner of the bookmarked message.

Mobile

1. On mobile, navigate to the message you would like to save. 

2. Long-press the message and tap Bookmark Message in the menu. 

3. Once bookmarked, you will see a notification badge letting you know that the message has been saved for later. 

 

4. To view your bookmarked messages, tap Notifications [] located in the bottom hub and select the Bookmarks icon [] in the upper-right corner of the app. This will take you to the Bookmarks tab.    

 

 

On mobile: To convert a bookmarked message into a reminder or unsave it, navigate to Notifications [] > Bookmarks[]. Then tap the ellipsis [] in the upper-right corner of the message and select the desired option.  

Creating a Reminder

Reminders allow you to set notifications for important messages at a specific time.Here's how to create reminders on both desktop, browser, and mobile:

Desktop/Browser

1. On desktop or browser, select the Inbox icon [] in the upper-right corner of the app.

2. Hover over the message you would like to set as a reminder and press the Create Reminder icon [] in the upper-right corner of the message.

3. In the Remind Me menu, you can choose how long you’d like to wait before being reminded. 

Choosing the Custom option in the Remind Me menu allows you to set a specific date and time for your reminder.

4. To view your reminders, press the Inbox icon [] in the upper-right corner of the app and select the Bookmarks tab. Here, you’ll find your added reminders. Each reminder will display your chosen Due in timeframe above the message. 

On desktop/browser: To edit, complete, or remove the reminder, right-click the message you have set as a reminder within Inbox > Bookmarks. From there, choose your desired action. 

Mobile 

1. On mobile, navigate to the message you would like to set as a reminder. 

2. Long-press the message and tap Create Reminder in the menu. 

3. Choose when you would like to be reminded in the Remind Me… menu. 

 

4. Once set, you will see a notification badge letting you know that the message has been set as a reminder. 

5. To view your reminders, tap Notifications [] located in the bottom hub and select the Bookmarks icon [] in the upper-right corner of the app.  

   

On mobile: To edit or remove the reminder, tap the ellipsis [] in the upper-right corner of the reminder, then select the desired option. Tap the checkmark icon [] to mark it as complete.
