Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Any PlayStation or Xbox console game
Quest Name: Console Gaming Quest

Offer Details: Connect your console to Discord and play any console game for 10 minutes to receive the Warp Helmet avatar decoration. You can learn more about avatar decorations here.  

Note: The Warp Helmet is a limited-time avatar decoration and will only be yours as an avatar decoration until September 9, 2024. After this date, you will no longer have access to this avatar decoration. 

Once the Quest has been completed, participants will also be able to sign up for a chance to join the free VALORANT Console Limited Beta! VALORANT is headed to PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.
For more information, visit the official VALORANT Console Limited Beta website!

Who is Eligible: Available to players on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S where Discord is available. 

Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for this Quest or reward.

Availability Period: The Console Gaming Quest can be accepted on Discord from June 26, 2024 until July 10, 2024 (11:59pm UTC). The Warp Helmet avatar decoration needs to be redeemed by August 9, 2024.

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward. 

Other Terms: 

Limit of one reward redemption per account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

Once you join the Console Gaming Quest, you’ll need to connect your Discord account to one of the following eligible consoles:

  • PlayStation 4 / PlayStation 5
  • Xbox Series X / Xbox Series S 

For guidance on how to connect to your console, you can refer to the Help Center articles below: 

  •  Discord and PlayStation® Network Connection FAQ
  •  Discord and Xbox Connection FAQ

If your console is already connected to your Discord account, you’ll still be able to accept the Quest and can start playing any console game. 

If you’re unsure whether your Discord account is already connected to your console, navigate to User Settings > Connections to review all available connections.

Once you’ve connected Discord to your console, play any console game for 10 minutes to complete the Quest. 

If you are running into any issues with the Quest completion, you can select the Check for Launches button within the Quest banner to check if you’ve launched a game. For additional troubleshooting steps, check out the following Help Center articles: 
- Discord and Xbox Connection FAQ
- Discord and PlayStation® Network Connection FAQ

Redemption Instructions

Once you’ve completed the Quest by connecting Discord to your console and playing a console game, you’ll be able to claim the Warp Helmet avatar decoration. The Warp Helmet avatar decoration needs to be redeemed by August 9, 2024.

Note: If the Quest is not completed by July 10, 2024, the Quest will no longer be available within your Gift Inventory. 

You can use the following methods to claim your Warp Helmet avatar decoration:

Option 1

Head over to your User Settings by selecting the cogwheel [] in the lower right corner. 

Then, navigate to your Gift Inventory and select Claim Reward within the Console Gaming Quest banner.  

Option 2

You can also claim your Warp Helmet avatar decoration by selecting the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar. 

Learn more about avatar decorations and how to apply them here. 

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you’re in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you’re unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for Quests.

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Gift Inventory or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
