Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Zenless Zone Zero
Quest Name: V1.0 Quest

Offer Details: Stream 15 minutes of Zenless Zone Zero to receive a Version 1.0 bundle. This bundle will include the following items: 

  • Denny ×12,000
  • Senior Investigator Log ×3
  • W-Engine Energy Module ×2
  • Bangboo System Widget ×1

NOTE: Polychrome rewards are limited to 300,000 recipients. Only the first 300,000 Proxies to complete the Quest will receive the reward. The time of completion will be determined based on when the bundle code is redeemed after completing the livestream quest. First come, first served.

Who is Eligible: Available to Zenless Zone Zero players on desktop and via Discord, either through  browser or the desktop app.

Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you’re unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for this Quest or reward.

Availability Period: The V1.0 Quest can be accepted on Discord from July 8, 2024 until July 15, 2024 (5:00pm PST). The Version 1.0 bundle needs to be redeemed by August 14, 2024 (3:00am PST).

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward. 

Other Terms: 

Limit of one reward redemption per Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

Once you have accepted the Quest, you must stream the game through the Discord desktop app and have at least one friend actively watching the stream to complete the Quest requirement.

In order to successfully complete the Zenless Zone Zero Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must live stream their Zenless Zone Zero gameplay to a friend via a Discord voice channel for a minimum duration of 15 minutes on the desktop app. 

1. Before you can begin the Quest, you must launch the game first.

2. Head to your Discord desktop app and navigate to a direct message (DM), group chat, or a server of your choice.

3. Start a voice call or join a voice channel and select the Screen Share [] icon located in the voice status panel. Be sure to select the individual application for the game before you Go Live. 

NOTE: You must select the game application to stream! Streaming your device’s screen will not progress your Quest forward. Once you stream, you can check the progress bar on the Gift inventory to ensure progress is being made on the Quest.

4. When you’re ready to stream your gameplay, press Go Live. You can learn more about streaming your game here. 

Redemption Instructions

1. Once you have completed the Quest by streaming 15 minutes of Zenless Zone Zero using Go Live on Discord, you will receive a Gift Code in your Discord Gift Inventory. You can learn more about Go Live and screen sharing here.

2. Then, visit the official  website's code redemption page and log in to your game account. 

3. Enter the redemption code and you'll receive the bundle via in-game mail.  

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you’re in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you're unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for Quests.

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Gift Inventory or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
