As of March 15th, 2024, Discord will no longer be supported on Windows 7, Windows 8, or Windows 8.1.

This change is in line with both Chromium’s deprecation policy as well as Microsoft’s end of support for Windows 7 ESU and Windows 8.1 extended support on January 10, 2024.

To continue receiving updates and support for your Discord client, you must upgrade to at least Windows 10.

You can find more information about upgrading your Windows computer on the following pages from Microsoft:

  • Upgrade to Windows 10 FAQ
  • Upgrade to Windows 11 FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will the Discord client I currently have installed continue to work after March 15th, 2024?
A: Basic functionality may continue to work, however the functionality and stability is not guaranteed as the client will no longer receive updates.

Q: If I cannot upgrade my computer right now, can I use the browser version of Discord?
A: Yes, as long as your browser meets the minimum requirements found here, you can continue to use it.
