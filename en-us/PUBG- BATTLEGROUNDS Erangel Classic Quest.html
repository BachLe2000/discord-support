Promotion Terms

Quest Game: PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS
Quest Name: Erangel Classic Quest

Offer Details: Stream 15 minutes of PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS to receive an OG is Back Pack bundle! This bundle includes the following items: 

  • The Original Is Back T-Shirt
  • Foulmouthed Mask
  • Contraband Coupon x20
  • Hunter's Chest Pack

Who is Eligible: Available on all platforms where the PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS is available. 

Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for this Quest or reward.

Availability Period: The Erangel Classic Quest can be accepted on Discord from May 14, 2024 to May 28, 2024 (5:00PM PT). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward. Reward gift codes expire August 11, 2024.

Other Terms: 

Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

Once you have accepted the Quest, you must stream the game through the Discord desktop app and have at least one friend actively watching the stream to complete the Quest requirement. 

Once you join a Quest, you will need to stream the game on Discord to at least one friend for a short period of time. Each Quest will have its own required time to stream and you can confirm how long you need to stream the game for in the Promotion Terms. 

1. After accepting the quest, launch the partnered game.

2. Then, head to your Discord desktop/browser app and navigate to a server of your choice.

3. Once there, join a voice channel and select the Screen Share [ ] icon located in the voice status panel. Be sure to select the individual application for the partnered game before you Go Live. 

NOTE: You must select the game application to stream! Streaming your device’s screen will not progress your Quest forward. Once you stream, you can check the progress bar on the Gift inventory to ensure progress is being made on the Quest.  

4. When you’re ready to stream your gameplay, press Go Live. You can learn more about streaming your game here. 

Remember that each Quest has specific Quest time requirements to complete. You may have to stream for a certain amount of time and have some friends watch your stream!

Note: we're exploring different ways to complete a Quest. If your Quest instructions say, "Play PUBG:Battlegrounds for 15 minutes", then you do not need to stream the game to a friend.

Redemption Instructions

1. Once you have completed the Quest by streaming 15 minutes of PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS using Go Live on Discord, you will receive a Gift Code in your Discord Gift Inventory. You can learn more about Go Live and screen sharing here.

2. Then, visit the official PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS website, log into your game account, and then redeem your code.

3. While there, enter the redemption code and you'll receive the bundle via in-game mail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for Quests.

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Gift Inventory or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
