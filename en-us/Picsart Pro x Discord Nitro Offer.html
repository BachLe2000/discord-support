How Do I Claim The Picsart Pro/Picsart Gold Promo In Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the Picsart Pro or the Picsart Gold code in the Gift Inventory from October 24, 2023 (time start) to November 23, 2023 (time end)

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active subscriber to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro subscriber).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by November 23, 2023 (time end)
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after November 23, 2023 (time end). 

If you are a Nitro Basic subscriber, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions

1. Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can tap the Take me there button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2. Click on the Claim button for the Picsart Pro/Picsart Gold Gift

Once you press Claim on the Picsart Pro Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before December 23, 2023 (11:59PM PST).

Step By Step Redeem Instructions

Make sure you are logged into the correct Picsart account before officially redeeming this Picsart Pro/Picsart Gold code. Once redeemed, the trial cannot be transferred to another account.

In order to redeem the Picsart Pro/Picsart Gold promotion on your Picsart account, press on Redeem to be taken to the Picsart website or go to this link: USA offer and other countries offer

Once you're on the Picsart redemption page, you'll then need to follow these steps below:

1. Sign into your Picsart Account if you're not already signed-in or create your Picsart account.

 

2. Enter the 12-character code from your Gift Inventory and then press Redeem Offer.

3. Select a subscription plan and enter/choose your payment method.

4. Confirm that you are ready to proceed and all the information is correct by tapping the Start Free Trial button.
