Nameplates are currently in experiment and are only available in the Shop for select users on desktop. However, you can still view others’ nameplates on mobile.

What are nameplates?

Nameplates are unique designs that let you customize how your display name appears on Discord. While avatar decorations and profile effects let you personalize your profile, nameplates let you add more flair to your name so it stands out with style in your DMs, group chats, and servers.

How can I purchase a nameplate?

Desktop/Browser

Option 1

You can head over to the Shop to preview and purchase nameplates.
1. Navigate to your Direct Messages list by selecting the Discord app icon in the upper-left corner.

Direct Messages Icon

2. Then, press the Shop tab [] to the right of the server list to enter the Shop! 

Shop tab

3. Once directed to the Shop, you can preview and buy nameplates which get  added to your collection. 

Example of Nameplates in the Shop

Option 2

1. To access your User Settings, select the cogwheel icon [] in the lower-left corner of the app, then navigate to the Profiles section. 

Cogwheel Icon

2. Once there, you will see the banner to the Shop where you can select Check it out. You can also select Change Nameplate and select the Shop icon [].  

Access Shop via User Settings

3. Once directed to the Shop, you can preview and buy nameplates to add to your collection.

Purchased nameplates are yours to keep and can be used at any time! Items available for purchase in the Shop will change periodically.

How do I apply a nameplate?

Desktop/Browser

1. On the desktop app or web app, head into your User Settings by selecting the cogwheel []. 

Cogwheel Icon

2. Once directed to your User Settings, select the Profiles tab to view your profile settings. 

3. Then, press the Change Nameplate button to open the nameplate selection menu. 

Change Nameplate Button

4. Once you have chosen the nameplate you would like, select Apply.

Selecting a nameplate

5. After you apply your selected nameplate, confirm your changes by selecting Save Changes. 

Save Changes Button

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are nameplates available to everyone?
A: Currently, nameplates are an experimental feature with limited availability to users on desktop. We'll update this article as the feature rolls out to more users.

Q: Can everyone see my nameplate or just me?
A: Everyone can see your nameplate.

Q: Do Nitro subscribers get discounts on nameplates?
A: Yes, Nitro subscribers receive discounts on all items in the Shop, including nameplates.
Q: Are nameplates available to purchase and edit on mobile?
A: Currently, you can only purchase and edit nameplates on desktop. However, nameplates are visible on both desktop and mobile devices.
Q: Can I refund a nameplate I’ve purchased?
A: No, nameplates are non-refundable. You can learn more about our Refund Policy here.

Q: Can I refund Nitro after buying a nameplate?
A: No, if you’ve purchased a nameplate within 5 days of starting or renewing your Nitro subscription, you will not be eligible to refund that Nitro subscription.
Q: Can nameplates be turned off? I don't want to see them.
A: Nameplates cannot be turned off, but you can go to User Settings > Accessibility > Enable Reduced Motion to minimize nameplate animations and effects.
Q: I purchased a nameplate but I don’t see it on my name?
A: Currently, nameplates show when you are active on Discord so ensure you are not in invisible mode or offline.
