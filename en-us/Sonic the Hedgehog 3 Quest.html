Promotion Terms

Quest Video: Sonic the Hedgehog 3
Quest Name: Sonic 3 Quest

Offer Details: Watch Sonic the Hedgehog 3 video in Discord and unlock a Shadow avatar decoration!  

Availability Period: The Sonic 3 Quest can be accepted on Discord desktop app or browser client from December 13, 2024 until December 21, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.

NOTE: The Shadow avatar decoration is a limited-time avatar decoration and will only be yours as an avatar decoration until February 21, 2025 (11:59PM UTC). After this date, you will no longer have access to this avatar decoration.    

Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

To fulfill the Quest requirement, the Sonic the Hedgehog 3 video must be watched through the Discord desktop app or browser client. 

In order to successfully complete Sonic 3 Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must watch the video. 

1. Navigate to Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or hover over the Quest bar to find the Sonic 3 Quest.

Sonic 3 Quest Bar and in Quest Home

2. Press Start Video Quest to launch the pop-up video player and watch the video. 

How to Start Sonic 3 Quest via Quest Bar

NOTE: To play the video, you must use the Discord desktop app or browser client. The video will pause if you switch away from the Discord window. Don't worry if you can't finish the video in one sitting—your Quest progress is saved, so you can return to it later.

Redemption Instructions

Once you have finished watching the Sonic the Hedgehog 3 video, you'll be eligible to earn the Shadow avatar decoration. You can participate in this Quest until December 21, 2024 (11:59 PM UTC) and claim your reward by January 21, 2024.

You can use the following methods to claim your avatar decoration:

Through the Quest Bar or Video Player

To claim your Shadow avatar decoration, simply press the Claim Reward button in either the Quest bar or the pop-up video player.  

  

“Claim Reward” Button: Quest Bar (Left) vs. Pop-up Video Player (Right)

Through the Quest Home

1. To claim your bundle through Quest Home, head over to the Discover tab by selecting the Discover icon []  at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app. 

2. This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here.

3. While here, select Claim Reward for The First Descendent Quest. 

“Claim Reward” Button: Quest Home

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
