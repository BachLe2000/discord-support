Starting from October 7, 2024 (10:00AM PT) to May 31, 2025 (11:59PM PT), YouTube Premium subscribers within the United States can claim Discord Nitro through YouTube's Member Hub.

During this same period, YouTube Premium subscribers in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Germany, Japan, and South Korea can claim their 3-month Discord Nitro offer via email. Learn below about how you can claim Discord Nitro.

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: 3 months of Discord Nitro for YouTube Premium subscribers

Who is Eligible: To claim through YouTube Premium, you must be a current YouTube Premium subscriber in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Germany, Japan, and South Korea. 

  • If you currently have a free YouTube Premium subscription, you will not be eligible to claim this promotion.

To redeem in Discord, you must redeem on the Discord desktop or browser app.

Users who have never had an active Nitro subscription on their Discord accounts are eligible to redeem this Nitro promotion.

Users who have had a Discord Nitro subscription at any point on their accounts and have had a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic subscription in the past 12 months are ineligible to redeem this promotion.

If a user has had a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting subscription active on their account and they’ve never had a Nitro subscription, redeeming this promotion will override that subscription.

We will not be able to refund any leftover subscription time from those, and the user will be forfeiting the remainder of the existing sub when activating the 3 month Nitro trial. 

Requirements:

Redeem to Discord account by June 30, 2025 (11:59PM PT).

Subscription:

After the 3 month free trial period, your Nitro subscription will convert to a monthly recurring subscription, and Discord will bill your payment method per month until you cancel it. You can cancel at any time in your Discord User Settings.

How do I claim the Nitro Promo from YouTube 

As long as you're a YouTube Premium member, you'll be able to claim the Nitro promo from YouTube from October 7, 2024 (10:00AM PT) to May 31, 2025 (11:59PM PT). 

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion from Youtube

In order to claim this promotion link, you'll need to be an active subscriber to YouTube Premium. You will also need to have opted-in to marketing emails from YouTube in order to be sent the code.

In the US, active YouTube Premium subscribers can claim the code from their YouTube Premium Member Hub. All offer claims must be by May 31, 2025 (11:59PM PT) and redeemed by June 30, 2025 (11:59PM PT).

NOTE: If you currently have a YouTube Premium trial subscription, you will not be eligible to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

Within Your Email

To claim your Discord Nitro 3-month trial within your email, you must:  

1. Be a current YouTube Premium subscriber that has opted-in to marketing emails from YouTube to receive this promotion.

2. Press “Get Discord Nitro” in the email sent from YouTube Premium to start the redemption process for your 3-month free trial of Discord Nitro.

Within your YouTube Premium Account (US only)

1. Visit YouTube and log in to your YouTube Premium account.

2. Press on your profile picture in the top right, and select “Your Premium Benefits” from the dropdown menu. This is where you can find all your YouTube Premium membership benefits including the Discord Nitro benefit.

3. Select the Discord Nitro benefit and from here the promo link and details will appear in a pop-up. Press the promo link and you will be automatically redirected to claim your free Nitro. You can also copy and paste the link into your browser. 

4. Select “Redeem Now” to be redirected to redeem your 3-month free trial of Discord Nitro.

How do I redeem the Nitro Trial on my Discord Account? 

Once you have claimed the promo link in your YouTube Premium Member Hub, you'll now need to officially redeem this promo link on your Discord account (or share this code with a friend)! 

Eligibility for Redeeming the Nitro Trial

Users who have never had an active Nitro subscription on their Discord accounts are eligible to redeem this Nitro promotion.

Users who have had a Discord Nitro subscription at any point on their accounts and have had a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic subscription in the past 12 months are ineligible to redeem this promotion.

If a user has had a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting subscription active on their account and they’ve never had a Nitro subscription, redeeming this promotion will override that subscription.

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

If you're eligible and ready to redeem this Nitro subscription on your own Discord account, you can follow these steps below to redeem the promo!

NOTE:
- Before redeeming Nitro, make sure you are logged in your own Discord account.
- If you accidentally redeemed the Nitro promo link on an account that is not yours, we are unable to provide you with another promo link.

Step 1 - Pressing the Promo Link/Copy and Pasting into your Browser Window

  • Start by directly pressing on the promotion link within the YouTube Premium Member Hub or copy and paste the link into your browser window.

Note: If you enter this Nitro promo link from the YouTube premium promotion into the Discord Gift Inventory or as a link within Discord chat, you will see an error when attempting to redeem the promotion. This is the incorrect method of redeeming this promo.

Step 2 - Logging into Discord

  •  If you're not already logged into Discord in your browser, you will then be automatically redirected to either:

Register for a New Account

If you don't have an account, you can directly register a new Discord account

Login to your Account

If you already have a Discord account, you can login to your account using your email and password or using QR code login

Step 3 - Confirming the Correct Account and Payment Method

  • Once you have logged into your Discord account, you'll see a new message asking you to confirm that you are accepting the Nitro subscription on the correct Discord account.
    • You'll see a notice saying: "You are accepting this gift as _________" 
  • If your Discord account does not have your payment information, you'll be asked to fill out your payment details. (Note: You will not be charged until the free trial ends and the subscription renews)
  • When you've filled out all the necessary information and you're ready to redeem this promotion, press the "Accept Gift" button. 
NOTE:
- If you're logged into the incorrect Discord account, press the "Wrong account" button to login to the other account! 

Step 4 - Subscription Now Activated

  • After accepting the promo to your account, you'll now be automatically redirected to your Subscriptions tab in your User Settings to see your newly activated Nitro subscription on your account.

What if I am an Active Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting Subscriber? 

Just to clarify - if you have had a Discord Nitro subscription at any point on your account and have had a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic subscription in the past 12 months, you are ineligible to redeem this promotion.

So long as you have had a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting subscription active on your account and you’ve never had a Nitro subscription, you will be able to redeem this promotion and replace it with the 3 month Nitro subscription.

HOWEVER, please note:
- By choosing to activate this 3 month trial, you will lose any features associated with your current subscriptions, and **you will not receive a refund for any remaining value on your current subscription**.

At the end of the promotion period, you'll be converted to a paying Nitro subscription per month unless you cancel your subscription.

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you've had a Nitro subscription previously on your Discord account in the past 12 months and you're unable to redeem the Nitro subscription link, you can share this link with a friend to use instead.

After you locate the Discord Nitro benefit in the YouTube Premium Member Hub, you can copy the promotion link and send it to a friend. We recommend sending the link in a private direct message.

Payment Info and Subscription Questions

Q: Why do I need to submit my payment info to redeem Nitro?

A: Because Nitro is a subscription plan, at the end of your free 3 month trial, we will start your first billing cycle so you can continue to enjoy Nitro on Discord.

However, you can cancel the Nitro subscription at any time before the end of the 3 months!

Q: Why do I see a temporary hold/charge on my account when this is supposed to be a free promotion?

A: Just to clarify, this Nitro promotion is free, however, there will be a temporary hold placed on the payment method upon activating the promotion. This is to help Discord confirm the payment method works as expected and to prevent the use of fraudulent payment methods. This charge will appear in your Discord transaction history, and it may appear as a hold on your payment method.

We immediately reverse the temporary hold, and you’ll see that reflected in your Discord transaction history. Your bank may take longer to remove the hold from your payment method.

NOTE: Banks will usually remove the temporary hold from your account within a few business days.

If your Discord transaction history does not show the hold released after 5 business days, please let us know at https://dis.gd/contact, and our support team will be able to investigate this issue further.

Q: I see a "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link!

A: If you're seeing an "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error, that means that the Discord account you're trying to redeem this promotion on currently has a Nitro subscription so it is ineligible for this Nitro promotion.

Q: I see a "Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub!" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link!

A: If you're seeing an “Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub” error, that means that you've previously subscribed to Nitro on your Discord account and you won't be able to redeem this 3 month perk! However, this promo link can be gifted to a friend!

Q: I see a "This gift has already been redeemed" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link!

A: If you are seeing a "This gift has been redeemed already" error, that means that the Nitro promo link has already been used and cannot be used again. You may need to double check if you've redeemed this promo link on another Discord account.

In particular, if you redeemed this promo link using your mobile device, double check the browser on your mobile device and see which Discord account you are currently logged into.

Unfortunately, once the promo link has been redeemed, the promo link cannot be re-enabled, and the Nitro cannot be transferred from one account to another.

Q: I see a "Wait a Second...Looks like this code didn't work." error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link!
