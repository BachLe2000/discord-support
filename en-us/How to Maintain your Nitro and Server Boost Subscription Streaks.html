What is a Subscription Streak?

Nitro and Server Boosting Badge streaks

A subscription streak is how long you’ve been subscribed to Nitro or Server Boosting a server. The longer you’ve consistently been subscribed, the bigger your streak will be. Your streak can be reset if you are no longer subscribed to Nitro or if you’re not actively boosting a server with a Server Boost.

How to Maintain Your Nitro Badge Subscription Streak

Example of Nitro Badge and subscription streak

Any user that has a Nitro, Nitro Basic, or Nitro Classic subscription will have a Nitro badge on their profile. In order to maintain your Nitro badge streak, you must remain subscribed to Nitro, Nitro Basic, or Nitro Classic. 

If your subscription has ended, you have 3 days to renew your subscription in order to maintain your streak.

You can view your boosting streak by tapping on your avatar icon to reveal your profile and hovering over your Nitro badge.

How to Maintain Your Server Boost Subscription Streak

Note: If you have been removed or left a server that you were boosting, you will have a 7 day cooldown before you can use your boost again. Learn more about transferring a boost below.

If you are also switching your purchase from desktop to mobile or vice versa, you have 72 hours to manually reactivate your Server Boosts in order to not lose your streak.

Server Boosting Badge Streak through User Profile

Your Server Boosting Badge and streak is based on the length of time that you started boosting a server. 

Note: Regardless of how many servers you boost, the length of your streak is dependent on the oldest applied server boost(s)

You can view your boosting streak by tapping on your avatar icon to reveal your profile and hovering over your Server Boosting badge.

GIF showing how to view your Server Boosting badge streak

You can lose your boosting streak for any of the following reasons:

  • You cancel your Server Boost(s)
  • Your Nitro (with 2 included Server Boosts) or your Server Boost(s) subscription has ended
  • The server you have been boosting has been deleted 
  • You’ve been removed from the server by an admin or moderator
Note: If you stop boosting a server for any of the listed reasons, you have 72 hours to reapply your boosts to a new server in order to maintain your streak. If you do not boost another server within 72 hours, you will lose your streak and our Support Team can not adjust your streak.

As long as you keep boosting your favorite server, that badge will continue to evolve while you reach extra levels.

How do I Transfer my Server Boost to Maintain my Streak?

Note: After using your Server Boost, you’ll need to wait 7 days for the cooldown before transferring it to another server.

If you accidentally boosted the wrong server, or you just want to move your boost, you can easily transfer your Server Boost to another server! Just keep in mind, if you move your boost to another server, the original server might lose a server boost level. Here’s how to transfer your boost.

1. Head into your User Settings by pressing the cogwheel [] icon.

 

Cogwheel Icon on Desktop/Browser

2. Navigate to the Server Boost tab.

3. Here you should see the current servers that your Server Boosts are applied to. Tap on the ellipsis [] to the Server Boost that you want to move, and then tap on Transfer Boost.

4. You can either search for the name of the server you want to transfer the boost to, or you can tap on Transfer here if you see the server in the list. In order for a server to show up in the list, you must be a member of the server.

5. Now just tap on Yes, Transfer Boost to transfer the Server Boost.

6. Finally you’ll see a confirmation message stating it has been transferred successfully.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My streak reset and I believe this is incorrect, what should I do? 

A: Discord cannot restore streaks under any circumstances. If you think your streak was incorrectly reset, first verify that you have an active subscription.

If your subscription expired due to past due payments and you had to restart it, remember to manually reapply your server boosts within 72 hours of reactivating your Nitro subscription.

Q: My streak date appears incorrect. What should I do?

A: The streak calculation differs between products: Nitro subscription streaks are based on the subscription date, while Server Boost streaks start from when you begin boosting a server. Since purchases use Universal Time (UTC), the displayed date may differ by up to 24 hours from your local time.
