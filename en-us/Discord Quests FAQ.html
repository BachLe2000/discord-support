What are Discord Quests? 

Quests are a way for players to discover games and new content while earning rewards for playing, streaming, or watching videos on Discord.

Discord offers two types of Quests:

  • Game Quest: Play or stream a game to unlock a reward.
  • Video Quest: Watch a video to unlock a reward.

Completing a Discord Game Quest

Within each Quest’s Promotion Terms, you will find the steps on how to complete the Quest.

Accepting a Game Quest 

Through the Quest Bar on Desktop 

You may see an alert in your Discord app about a Discord Game Quest. Hover over the Quest bar and select it to expand and learn more about the Game Quest. If you want to participate, you can press Accept Quest. 

“Accept Quest” Button in Quest Bar for Game Quests

Through Quest Home on Desktop

You can also select the Discover icon [] at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app. 

How to Navigate to Discover Page

This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here. 

Quest Home in Discover

In Quests, you can view recent and current Discord Quests. While here, press Accept Quest for active Quests to begin.

“Accept Quest” Button in Quest Home

On Mobile

NOTE: On mobile, accepting, tracking, and viewing Quests is currently in beta and limited to specific Quests.

On mobile, tap the Quest bar to navigate to the Quest tab. Here, you can select Accept Quest for the Quest you wish to participate in.  

 

Mobile: Quest Bar (Left) and Accept Quest Button (Right)

If you dismiss the mobile Quest bar, you can still find the Quest in Quest Home (Settings > Quest) to accept the Quest.

Game Quest Requirements 

Each Game Quest will have its own requirements:

  • Playtime Requirement - To fulfill a Quest, players must engage with the game for a specified duration (e.g., 15 minutes). Ensure you are logged into the Discord desktop app while playing to accurately track your progress.
  • Streaming Requirement - To complete a Quest, players need to stream the game to at least one friend in a voice channel using the Discord desktop app for a minimum duration (e.g., 15 minutes). For more details on how to use Go Live and screen sharing, please refer to the Go Live and Screen Share support article.

Claim Your Game Quest Reward 

Once you have completed the Game Quest, you can claim the reward by pressing the Claim Reward button within any Discord Quest in-app notification.

Users who have completed quests after April 24, 2024 will automatically obtain the Discord Quests Badge, which will be added to their profiles. For more information about profile badges, visit our Profile Badges 101 Help Center article.

Claim Reward Button in Quest Bar

The easiest way to claim your reward is by heading over to Quest Home (Discover > Quests). 

If you’re claiming an in-game item reward, you’ll be given a Discord Quest redemption code that you’ll need to redeem on the partnered game redemption website. You can find a link by heading over to our Active Discord Quests section and referring to the steps mentioned specifically for that Quest! You’ll also receive an email with redemption instructions once you’ve completed the Quest.

If you’re ready to claim a Discord reward, such as an avatar decoration, you can claim your reward by selecting the Claim Reward button.

Completing a Video Quest

Accepting a Video Quest 

Through the Quest Bar on Desktop 

You may see an alert in your Discord app about a Discord Video Quest. Hover over the Quest Bar to expand it and learn more about the Video Quest. If you want to participate, you can press Start Video Quest.

“Start Video Quest” Button in Quest Bar for Video Quests 

Through Quest Home on Desktop

Select the Discover icon [] at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app. This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here.

How to Navigate to Discover Page

In Quests, press Start Video Quest for an active Video Quests to begin.

“Start Video Quest” Button in Quest Home   

Video Quest Requirements 

To complete a Video Quest, players must watch a video through completion in Discord's desktop app or browser app client. 

How to Start a Video Quest in Quest Bar

To play the video, you must use the Discord desktop app or browser client. The video will pause if you switch away from the Discord window. Don't worry if you can't finish the video in one sitting—your Quest progress is saved, so you can return to it later.

Claiming Your Video Quest Reward 

Once you have completed the Video Quest, you can claim the reward in the similar ways to Game Quests:

  • Press the "Claim Reward" button within the pop-up video player or any Discord Quest in-app notification.
  • Go to Quest Home (Discover > Quests).
  • For in-game item rewards, you'll receive a Discord Quest redemption code to use on the partnered game's redemption website.
  • For Discord rewards like avatar decorations, simply select the "Claim Reward" button.

“Claim Reward” Button in Pop-up Video Player

Controlling the Quest Promotions You See

We've built controls into the Quest experience to help you manage the types of Quests you see:

Hide an In-app Promotion of an Individual Quest 

If you want to hide an in-app promotion for a specific Quest or game you’re not interested in, press the ellipsis [] on the Quest in-app promotion to access the menu and then select Hide This to hide this particular in-app promotion.

Once you've done this, you'll no longer receive notifications about this Quest in that particular in-app promotion style. 

If you encounter a different in-app promotion for that same Quest (for example, you may also see the Quest in the Active Now section of the Discord app) you can hide those by following the steps mentioned above. 

If you hide all the in-app promotions of a specific Quest, you can still view and accept the Quest in Quest Home (Discover > Quests).

“Hide This” Option in Quest In-App Promotion

Opt-out of personalized promotion for all Quests 

If you do not want to receive personalized promotions for any Quests based on your activity or data you share with Discord, you can disable your In-game rewards (aka Quests) settings within your Privacy & Safety settings. 

1. Press the cogwheel [] in the bottom left corner of your Discord desktop/browser app to navigate to your User Settings.

2. Then select the Privacy & Safety tab in the left hand panel and scroll down to the How We Use Your Data section.

3. Disable the In-game rewards (aka Quests) setting by selecting the toggle. You can learn more about how data is used for in-game rewards here. 

Although these settings are disabled, you will still be able to participate in Quests if you navigate to Quest Home (Discover > Quests). You may still see Quests promotions, but they won’t be based on your activity on Discord.

In-game Rewards (aka Quests) Option in Privacy and Safety Settings

If you decide later that you want to receive notifications about Discord Quests based on your activity, you will need to make sure to enable the In-game rewards (aka Quests) setting in Privacy & Safety. You can learn more about these settings by visiting Data Privacy Controls or reading our Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Quests will not be available in certain regions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.

Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.

Q: I have completed previous Quests. Why don’t I see the Discord Quests Badge in my profile?

A: If you have completed a Quest before April 24, 2024, the Discord Quests Badge will not appear in your profile. However, if you complete a Quest the Discord Quest after April 24, 2024 the Quests badge will be added to your profile. For more information about profile badges, visit our Profile Badges 101 Help Center article.
