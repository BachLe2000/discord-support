Promotion Terms

Quest Game: VALORANT
Quest Name: Wingman Quest

Offer Details: Play 15 minutes of VALORANT and receive a Wingman avatar decoration. You can learn more about avatar decorations here.

Note: Wingman is a limited-time avatar decoration and will only be yours as an avatar decoration until October 6, 2024. After this date, you will no longer have access to this avatar decoration.   

Who is Eligible: Available to players on desktop, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S where both Discord and VALORANT are available. 

Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for this Quest or reward.

Availability Period: The Wingman Quest can be accepted on Discord from July 26, 2024 until August 6, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). The Wingman avatar decoration needs to be redeemed by October 6, 2024. 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.

Other Terms: 

Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

Desktop/Browser

In order to successfully complete the Wingman Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play VALORANT on desktop or console for a minimum duration of 15 minutes.

If you choose to complete the Quest via console, you will need to connect your Discord account to one of the following eligible consoles:

  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox Series X / Xbox Series S 

If your console is already connected to your Discord account, you’ll still be able to accept the Quest and can start playing VALORANT.
If you are running into any issues with the Quest completion, you can select the Check for Launches button within the Quest banner to check if you’ve launched a game. For additional troubleshooting steps, check out the following Help Center articles:
- Discord and Xbox Connection FAQ
- Discord and PlayStation® Network Connection FAQ  

Mobile

The VALORANT Quest can be accepted on your Discord mobile app (both iOS and Android devices). You can also claim your reward on the mobile app. However, the Quest itself must be completed through desktop or console. 

How to Accept the Quest

1. Open the Discord mobile app

2a. Tap on the VALORANT Quest pop-up located at the bottom of the screen

2b. If you do not have the pop-up, tap on your profile image in the bottom-right corner. Then tap on the cogwheel [] in the top-right corner of your profile and navigate to Quests.

3. Press on Accept Quest

4a. If you are completing this Quest on a Console, you will be prompted to connect your PlayStation or Xbox account if you haven’t already

4b. If you are completing this Quest on a PC, you will need to launch Valorant and Discord on your PC

Redemption Instructions

Desktop/Browser

Once you have completed the Quest by playing 15 minutes of VALORANT on Discord, you’ll be able to claim the Wingman avatar decoration. This avatar decoration needs to be redeemed by September 7, 2024.

Note: If the Quest is not completed by August 6, 2024, the Quest will no longer be available within your Gift Inventory. 

You can use the following methods to claim your Wingman avatar decoration:

Option 1

Head over to your User Settings by selecting the cogwheel [] in the lower left corner. 

Then, navigate to your Gift Inventory and select Claim Reward within the VALORANT Quest banner.  

Option 2

You can also claim your Wingman avatar decoration by pressing the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar. 

Learn more about avatar decorations and how to apply them here. 

Mobile

1. Open the mobile app

2. Tap on your profile image in the bottom right corner

3. Tap on the cogwheel [] in the top-right corner of your profile.

4. Next, tap on Quests and then press on View Reward to accept the reward.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for Quests.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Gift Inventory or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
