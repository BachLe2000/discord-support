Starting from March 19, 2024 (10:00am PST) to April 18, 2024 (11:59pm PST), Discord Nitro members can claim an exclusive Monster Hunter Now x Discord Reward Bundle for Monster Hunter Now. Read more about the promotion below.

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: Exclusive Monster Hunter Now x Discord Reward Bundle for Discord Nitro member

Included in this reward bundle are the following items: 

  1. One (1) Paintball- By using a Paintball, you can mark any monster of your choosing encountered in the field.
  2. One (1) Wander Orb- A sphere housing a mysterious power, increases your access range in the field by 50% for 30 minutes.
  3. One (1) DevilJho Scale (from new rare monster and cannot be purchased) - Monster material from DevilJho (monster with extremely low spawn rate) that can be used to craft the newly introduced DevilJho weapon and gear sets!

Who is Eligible: To claim through Discord, you must be a current Nitro member. 

Requirements:

Claim through Discord Gift Inventory from March 19, 2024 (10:00am PST) to April 18, 2024 (11:59pm PST), and redeem in your Monster Hunter Now account by April 25, 2024 (11:59pm PST).

What is Monster Hunter Now?

Monster Hunter Now is a location-based action RPG mobile game where you team up with other players to hunt monsters in the real world. The core loop is simple – explore your neighborhood to hunt monsters, battle, craft weapons & armor, repeat.

So, get out and hunt together!

How do I claim the Reward Bundle in Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the exclusive reward bundle code in the Gift Inventory from March 19, 2024 (10:00am PST) to April 18, 2024 (11:59pm PST).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active member to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro member).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by April 18, 2024 (11:59pm PST).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after April 18, 2024 (11:59pm PST). 

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting member, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

For both the desktop/browser app and mobile app, you can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select Gift Inventory.

2) Click on the Claim button for the Monster Hunter Now Reward Bundle

Once you press Claim on the Monster Hunter Now Reward Bundle in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before April 25, 2024 (11:59pm PST).

How do I redeem the Reward Bundle on my Monster Hunter Now Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your Monster Hunter Now account.

Eligibility for Redeeming the Reward Bundle

Discord Nitro members are eligible for this offer as long as they have downloaded the Monster Hunter Now app and have created an account with Monster Hunter Now.  

1. Head over to the Monster Hunter Now app and create an account. 

2. If you already have a Monster Hunter Now account or have just created an account, head over to the redemption page: https://store.monsterhunternow.com/offer-redemption.  

3. Once there, log in using the same credentials associated with your Monster Hunter Now account. 

4. Then, enter the offer code. Make sure there are no spaces included. 

5. After successfully redeeming an offer redemption code, a message will display the items added to your item box. 

If items are not added, please try restarting the app. If the message "The code has already been redeemed and the item has been granted" appears, it is likely that the entry has already been accepted, so restart the app and check the item box.

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your Monster Hunter Now account meets the promotion requirements, head over to the Monster Hunter Now Help Center article for further assistance.
