What are avatar decorations?

Avatar decorations let you customize and style your avatar on Discord! You can buy and collect all kinds of decorations from a new Shop. Collected decorations are yours to keep so you can add all kinds of pizazz to your avatar and profile aesthetic whenever you want.

These new avatar decorations are first launching in early access for Nitro members. Stay tuned as we slowly roll out this feature. To learn more about Nitro, check out this article.

How can I collect an avatar decoration? 

Avatar decorations can be found and purchased in the Shop. 

1. To get started, head into your User Settings > Profiles.

2. While there, you will see the banner to the Shop where you can select Go to Shop. You can also select Change Decoration and select the Shop icon.

During early access, you can only access the Shop on the desktop app or your browser.

3. Once redirected to the Shop, you can preview and buy avatar decorations to add to your collection and wear whenever you’d like.

Avatar decorations will be available to purchase for a limited time. Once purchased, you will be able to use your collected avatar decoration whenever you’d like! Available selections will change periodically.

How to apply an avatar decoration to my profile

Desktop

1. On the desktop app or web app, head into your User Settings by selecting the cogwheel []. 

2. Once redirected to your User Settings, select the Profiles tab to view your profile settings. 

3. Then, press the Change Decorations button to open the Edit Avatar Decoration selection menu.  

4. Once you have chosen the avatar decoration you would like, select Apply to add the decoration to your avatar. 

   

5. After you apply an avatar decoration, you will need to confirm your changes by selecting Save Changes. 

Mobile

Avatar decorations are only available for purchase through desktop and browser. However, you will be able to apply and change your avatar decoration across all platforms, including mobile.

1. On your mobile device, head into your User Settings by tapping your avatar. 

2. From here, select Profiles. This will navigate you to the Profiles page where you can tap your avatar to open the Avatar menu.  

3. In the Avatar menu, tap Change Decoration and select the desired avatar decoration you would like to apply. 

4. Once you’ve found an avatar decoration you want to use, tap Save to confirm your changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can currently access the Shop and buy and use avatar decorations?A: At this time, only Nitro members can buy avatar decorations from the Shop and use them as an early access benefit.  

Q: Do I keep the avatar decorations I’ve purchased? A: Yes, any decorations you’ve purchased are yours to keep and use anytime, even without an active Nitro subscription. 

Q: Do I get to keep the DISXCORE bonus decorations I’ve collected?

A: The bonus DISXCORE decorations can be freely collected by Nitro members, but require an active Nitro subscription to use. If you decide to cancel your Nitro subscription, the bonus decorations will remain in your collection but you won’t be able to use them. 

Q: I thought avatar decorations used to be free for Nitro members. Why do I have to buy them now?

A: Previously, avatar decorations would only be freely available for Nitro members to use for a limited time and then they would disappear, which would leave a lot of folks sad. A lot of you also told us our decorations needed more variety.
Now avatar decorations are designed to be yours to keep and use anytime when you buy them. We’ve also been working hard to make avatar decorations better than before, with even more charming and fun designs, higher quality animations and effects, and more styles to choose from.
As a friendly reminder, if you’re a Nitro member, you can collect the 3 bonus DISXCORE avatar decorations from the Shop!

Q: Can I refund an avatar decoration I’ve purchased?

A: No, avatar decorations are non-refundable. You can learn more about our Refund Policy here.
Q: Can I refund Nitro after buying an avatar decoration?

A: No. If you’ve purchased an avatar decoration within 5 days of your Nitro subscription renewing or becoming a new Nitro member, you will not be eligible to refund that Nitro subscription.

Q: How often do you add new decorations? 

A: We don’t have specific details related to when new decorations become available, but our team is hard at work to make sure we continue to bring new and fun styles to your hands.
