Promotion Terms

Quest Game: The First Descendant
Quest Name: Season 1 Invasion Quest
Offer Details: Play 15 minutes of The First Descendant on PC to receive a Hailey avatar decoration, the legendary sniper. You can learn more about avatar decorations here.
Who is Eligible: Available to players on PC where both Discord and The First Descendant are available.
Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions.

NOTE: The Hailey avatar decoration is a limited-time avatar decoration and will only be yours as an avatar decoration until November 5, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). After this date, you will no longer have access to this avatar decoration.

Availability Period: The Season 1 Invasion Quest can be accepted on Discord from August 30, 2024 until September 5, 2024 (11:59PM UTC).
Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.
Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.
For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

How to Complete the Quest

Note: To fulfill the Quest requirement, The First Descendant must be played with your Discord app open simultaneously (on browser or desktop).

In order to successfully complete the Season 1 Invasion Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play The First Descendant for a minimum duration of 15 minutes.

Redemption Instructions

Once you have completed the Quest by streaming 15 minutes of The First Descendant, you’ll be able to claim the Hailey avatar decoration. The Hailey avatar decoration needs to be redeemed by September 5, 2024.

Note: If the Quest is not completed by September 5, 2024, the Quest will no longer be available within your Quest Home (Discover > Quests).

You can use the following methods to claim your Hailey avatar decoration:

Option 1

1. Head over to the Discover tab by selecting the Discover icon [] at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app.

2. This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here.

3. While here, select Claim Reward for the Season 1 Invasion Quest.

Option 2

You can also claim your Hailey avatar decoration by pressing the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar.

Learn more about avatar decorations and how to apply them here.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.

Q: When I press on “Claim Reward” I'm loading FOREVER, why is this?

A: Fear not, you should still see the avatar decoration in your User Settings. Updating your Discord app can also resolve the issue if you're not seeing the reward.
