From November 21, 2022 to January 13, 2023, Discord Nitro subscribers will be able to give a friend 1 trial month of Xbox PC Game Pass!

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: 1 month of Xbox PC Game Pass that a Discord Nitro subscriber can give to a buddy!

Who is Eligible:

  • To claim through Discord, you must be a current Discord Nitro subscriber.
  • To redeem in Xbox, this offer is only for new Xbox PC Game Pass subscribers only.
    • For more details, check out the terms here: https://www.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-game-pass#xgpterms
  • This promo is available in certain regions. Please review Xbox terms for more details. If you have questions, please reach out to the Xbox support team.

Requirements: The receiver of the Xbox PC Game Pass code must redeem in their Xbox account by January 13, 2023.

Subscription:

Unless you cancel, you will be charged the then-current regular membership rate when the promotional period ends.

After the 1 month free trial period, your subscription will convert to a monthly recurring subscription, and Xbox will bill your payment method for $9.99/month (USD) until you cancel it. You can cancel at any time through your Xbox settings.

How do I give this Xbox PC Game Pass code to a friend? 

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to receive the Xbox PC Game Pass code in the Gift Inventory to give to a friend from November 21, 2022 to January 13, 2023. 

Eligibility for the Obtaining this Promotion in Discord

In order to obtain this code, you'll need to be an active subscriber to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro subscriber)!

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro subscriber, the code must be given to your friend by January 13, 2023. 
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim!

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Classic subscriber, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to take part in this promotion!

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2) Click on the See Code button for the Xbox Promotion

Once you press See Code on the Xbox PC Game Pass promotion in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 25 character code for this promotion!
3) Give this Xbox PC Game Pass code to a friend!

To give this code to a friend, copy the 25-character code from the Gift Inventory and then send  this code directly to a friend.

Note: We strongly suggest that you send your gift code to your friend in a private direct message.

How does my friend redeem the Xbox Promo on their Xbox Account? 

Once your friend receives the Xbox PC Game Pass trial code from you, they must officially redeem this code on their Xbox account before January 13, 2023.

Eligibility for Redeeming the Xbox PC Game Pass Promotion

To redeem the 1 month of Xbox PC Game Pass promotion, this offer is only for new Xbox Game Pass subscribers only. For more details, check out the terms here: https://www.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-game-pass#xgpterms

  • The promotion is available in certain regions. Please review Xbox terms for more details. If you have questions, please reach out to the Xbox support team.

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

Note:
- Make sure your friend is logged into the correct Xbox account before officially redeeming this Xbox PC Game Pass promotion!
- Once redeemed, the promotion cannot be transferred to another account.

In order to redeem the Xbox PC Game Pass promotion on a Microsoft account, you'll need to have the 25 character code and follow these steps below:

  1. Head to https://www.xbox.com/en-us/promotions/game-pass-offer/partner-trial! 
  2. Sign into your Microsoft Account (if you're not already signed-In)
  3. Enter the 25-character code and then press Next
  4. Select a payment method or enter your payment info (Credit/Debit Card or PayPal)
  5. Confirm that you are ready to proceed and all the information is correct by pressing Confirm!

Note:
- If you see a "Check the Instructions that came with your code. You might need to redeem it on a different web page" error, that means you do not meet the eligibility requirements to redeem the code!
- If you believe that your Microsoft account meets the requirements, reach out to the Microsoft support team to review your account further at https://support.xbox.com/contact-us !

Q: How long does this promotion for Nitro Subscribers run for?

A: This promotion runs from November 21, 2022 to January 13, 2023. 

After January 13, 2023, Nitro subscribers will no longer be able to receive this promotion.

A: Once a code is claimed, all Xbox codes must be redeemed by January 13, 2023.

If a code has not been redeemed to the Xbox account by January 13, 2023, it will expire and your friend will no longer be able to claim 1 month of Xbox PC Game Pass.
