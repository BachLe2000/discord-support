Starting from November 27, 2023 (10AM PST) to December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST), Discord Nitro members can claim 1 month of Midjourney Basic Plan subscription trial. 

Read more about the promotion below!

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: 1 month of Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription for Discord Nitro members

Who is Eligible: To claim through Discord, you must be a current Nitro member (unable to be redeemed if you have a Nitro trial membership) and are not an active Midjourney subscriber. 

Requirements:

Claim through Discord Gift Inventory from November 27, 2023 (10AM PST) to December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST), and redeem in your Midjourney account by January 3, 2024 (11:59PM PST).

Subscription:

After the 1 month free period, your Midjourney Basic Plan subscription will not automatically renew. Plans may be continued by purchasing a monthly or annual subscription following the free month period. Images generated will be retained even if the subscription is not renewed.

How do I claim the month-long Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription in Discord?

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active member to Discord Nitro (you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro member), not under a Nitro trial membership, and not an active Midjourney subscriber.

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST)
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST)

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting member, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1. Log in to Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app. Press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

For both the desktop/browser app and mobile app, you can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select Gift Inventory.

2. Press on the Claim button to reveal the 1 month Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription gift code

In the Gift Inventory, you’ll find the Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription gift. You can press on the Claim button next to the gift to reveal the unique gift code, which you can immediately redeem by pressing on the Redeem button.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST).

3. After copying the code, press on Redeem to head to the Midjourney website and sign in with your Discord account.

4. After signing in, you’ll be able to redeem the code fully to your Midjourney account by tapping on Confirm.

5. Finally, enter your payment information and then tap on Start trial when completed. Though the checkout page states that this subscription will automatically renew, this is inaccurate. The subscription will not renew automatically and must be reactivated after the end of the month

Eligibility for Redeeming the Midjourney Basic Plan free month 

To redeem the 1 month Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription code, you must not currently have an active Midjourney subscription.

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your Midjourney account meets the promotion requirements, reach out to the Midjourney support team to review your Midjourney account further: billing@midjourney.com

How do I redeem the Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription on my Midjourney Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Discord Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your Midjourney account.

1. Press on Redeem to head to the Midjourney website and sign in with your Discord account.

2. After signing in, you’ll be able to redeem the code fully to your Midjourney account by tapping on Confirm.

3. Finally, enter your payment information and then tap on Start trial when completed.

Promotion Details

Q: How long does this promotion for Discord Nitro members run for?

A: This promotion runs from November 27, 2023 (10AM PST) to December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST).

After January 3, 2024 (11:59PM PST), Nitro members will no longer be able to claim a subscription code.

Q: How long does my Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription code last for in my Discord Gift Inventory?

A: Once a code is claimed in the Gift Inventory in Discord, all Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription codes must be redeemed by December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST).

If a code has not been redeemed to the Midjourney account by December 27, 2023 (11:59PM PST), it will expire and you will no longer be able to claim 1 month of the Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription.

Eligibility for the Promotion

Q: Who can claim this promotion in the Discord Gift Inventory?

A: All current and new Discord Nitro members who are not active Midjourney subscribers are eligible for this perk. 

This does not include Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boost-only members.

Q: Who can redeem this Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription to their Midjourney account?

A: This offer is open to users who do not have an active Midjourney subscription.

Q: Can I redeem the Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription to my Midjourney account in my region? 

A: All users outside of Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Ukraine, Russia and the Crimea, Donetsk, and Luhansk Regions may claim this promotion. These restrictions are placed by Midjourney’s payment processor and may be read in detail here. 

Claiming and Redemption Process

Q: On which Discord platforms can I claim this promotion?

A: You'll be able to claim the promo on the mobile, browser, and desktop version of the Discord app. 

Potential Errors and Other Issues

Q: What if I don't see the 1 Month Midjourney Basic Plan Subscription code in my Discord Gift Inventory? 

A: In order to claim this code through Discord,  you must be a current Nitro member (unable to be redeemed if you have a Nitro trial membership) and not be an active Midjourney subscriber. 

If you are an active Discord Nitro member, make sure to refresh your Discord client to double check if an update was needed.

Q: What if I'm still running into issues after refreshing my Discord client? 

A: If the refresh does not work, please submit a ticket through our support system at: https://dis.gd/contact and our support team will be able to investigate further. Be sure to submit the ticket using the email address associated with your Discord account, and send us a screenshot of the following: Your My Account tab and your Gift Inventory tab.

Q: I'm having trouble redeeming this promotion to my Midjourney account. How do I contact Midjourney support? 

A: If you are having issues redeeming this promotion on your Midjourney account, you can reach out to Midjourney support at billing@midjourney.com

Q: When entering my input, the form says that the subscription will automatically renew at the end of the month. Will the subscription automatically renew?

A: Due to technical limitations, the payment screen will display that the trial renews automatically. This is not the case, and is incorrectly labeled.

Q: Will I retain access to my images if I do not renew my subscription?

A: You will be able to access images that you create independent of subscription renewal.

Q: I’ve claimed my trial. Now how do I generate images with Midjourney?

A: Join the Midjourney server at https://discord.gg/midjourney and follow the quickstart guide https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/quick-start.
