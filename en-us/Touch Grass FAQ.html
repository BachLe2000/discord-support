From April 1, 2025 (9:00AM PDT) to April 7, 2025 (11:59AM PDT) play the new adventure by Discord and earn an avatar decoration. This avatar decoration will be yours until May 1, 2025 (12:00AM PDT).

What this article covers:
  • How to Play
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Play

NOTE: The game is only available to play on the desktop app and browser.

Option 1

1. Tap on the alert in the bottom-right corner and then press on the right arrow icon to access the game.

Example of the alert that will appear in the Discord app

2. Press on Play Game to start the game.

Example of where to locate The Last Meadow under your User Settings

Option 2

1. Open User Settings by pressing the cogwheel [] in the bottom-left corner and select The Last Meadow in the left sidebar. 

2. Tap on Play Game to start the game!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What clients is this game available on?

A: The game is only available on desktop app/browser. Unfortunately, the game cannot be played on the mobile version of the app.

Q: How long does the avatar decoration last? 

A: The avatar decoration is yours until May 1, 2025 (12:00AM PDT).

Q: How long will this game be available? 

A: The game will be available until April 7, 2025 (11:59AM PDT). After this time, the game will be removed and cannot be played in the app.

Q: Will my score be saved if I exit the game? 

A: Yes, when you go back to The Last Meadow tab in your User Settings, you can tap on the Continue Game option to continue your progress.

Example of where to tap to continue your game
