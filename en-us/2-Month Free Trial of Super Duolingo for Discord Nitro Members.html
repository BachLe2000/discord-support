Claim through Discord Gift Inventory from July 2, 2024 (10:00am PST) to August 1, 2024 (11:59pm PST), and redeem in your Duolingo account by August 8, 2024 (5:00pm PST).

Subscription:

What is Duolingo?

Duolingo is a fun, free app for learning 40+ languages, math, and music through quick, bite-sized lessons. Super Duolingo is the fastest, most fun and effective way to learn a language; subscribers can fast track their progress with no ads, no interruptions, and features to help them learn at their own pace. 

How do I claim the Super Duolingo trial in Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the Super Duolingo code in the Gift Inventory from July 2, 2024 (10:00am PST) to August 1, 2024 (11:59pm PST).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active member to Discord Nitro. This applies to monthly or yearly Nitro members, as well as Nitro trial users).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by August 1, 2024 (11:59pm PST).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after August 1, 2024 (11:59pm PST). 

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting member, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

For both the desktop/browser app and mobile app, you can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select Gift Inventory.

2) Click on the Claim button for the Super Duolingo Gift.

Once you press Claim on the Super Duolingo Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before August 8, 2024 (5:00pm PST).

3) Complete the claim offer step on the Duolingo x Discord landing page.

After receiving your unique gift code, you will be redirected to the Duolingo x Discord landing page. Then, select Claim Offer. 

If you’re not redirected to the landing page, you can redeem your unique code on duolingo.com/redeem. 

4) Create or log in to your free Duolingo account.

Then, you can log in to your free Duolingo account. If you do not already have a Duolingo account, you can create an account by selecting Create Account. 

4) Review how your free trial works, select your plan, and complete the billing information page. 

Once you’ve logged into your free Duolingo account, you will be asked to review the free trial, select a plan for after your free trial, and complete your billing information. 

NOTE: If you’re a current Super or Duolingo Max subscriber, you will not be eligible for this promotion.

5) Super Duolingo trial is now activated!

After you have completed the steps above, your Super Duolingo trial will be activated and you can continue your learning journey.

How do I redeem the Super Duolingo Trial on my Duolingo Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code with an eligible Duolingo account.

Eligibility for Redeeming the Super Duolingo Trial

You are eligible for this promotion if:

  • You are a new or existing free Duolingo learner. Current Super or Duolingo Max subscribers are not eligible. 
  • You have redeemed the Duolingo x Discord offer through the landing page or by redeeming your unique code on duolingo.com/redeem. 
  • You have completed the Duolingo account sign-up and added your billing information.

NOTE: The Super Duolingo subscription will automatically renew at the current local price at the end of the free trial, unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the 2-month trial. 
Offer may not be combined with any other coupons, discounts, or promotions. Offer has no cash value. Offer cannot be sold or otherwise transferred. Void where prohibited by law. Users are subject to the Terms and Conditions of Service at www.duolingo.com/terms, which Duolingo may modify at its sole discretion.

You can do so by emailing super-support@duolingo.com with the subject line: “Nitro x Duolingo claim Super Duolingo offer code”.

A: This promotion runs from July 2, 2024 (10:00am PST) to August 1, 2024 (11:59pm PST).

After August 1, 2024 (11:59pm PST), Nitro members will no longer be able to claim a trial code.

Q: How long does my Super Duolingo free trial code last for in my Gift Inventory?

A: Once a code is claimed in the Gift Inventory in Discord, all Super Duolingo trial codes must be redeemed by August 8, 2024, 2024 (5:00pm PST).

If a code has not been redeemed to the Duolingo account by August 8, 2024 (5:00pm PST), it will expire and you will no longer be able to claim 2 months of Super Duolingo.
