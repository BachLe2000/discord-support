Promotion Terms

Quest Game: The Sims 4

Quest Name: Lovestruck Quest

Offer Details: Play 15 minutes of The Sims 4 on PC to receive a Freezer Bunny Lovebug.

Who is Eligible: Available to US users on desktop where The Sims 4 is available.
Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for this Quest or reward.

Availability Period: The Lovestruck Quest can be accepted on Discord from July 25, 2024 until August 1, 2024 (11:59pm UTC).

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.

Other Terms:

Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

How to Complete the Quest

In order to successfully complete the Lovestruck Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play Sims 4 for a minimum duration of 15 minutes.

Note: To fulfill the Quest requirement, Sims 4 must be played with your Discord app open simultaneously (on browser or desktop).

Redemption Instructions

Once you have completed the Quest by playing 15 minutes of Sims 4 on Discord, you’ll be able to claim the Freezer Bunny Lovebug. The Sims 4 avatar decoration needs to be redeemed by September 1, 2024.

Note: If the Quest is not completed by August 1, 2024, the Quest will no longer be available within your Gift Inventory.

You can use the following methods to claim your Freezer Bunny Lovebug avatar decoration:

Option 1

Head over to your User Settings by selecting the cogwheel [] in the lower left corner. Then, navigate to your Gift Inventory and select Claim Reward within the Sims 4 Quest banner.

Option 2

You can also claim your Freezer Bunny Lovebug by pressing the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar.

Learn more about avatar decorations and how to apply them here.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is my Sims 4 game not being detected by Discord?

A: If your Sims 4 game is not being detected by Discord, please try the following steps:

  1. Close your Sims game.
  2. Restart the Discord browser or desktop app.
  3. Relaunch your Sims game.
  4. Re-check the Quest progress bar to see if the game is now detected.

Q: Why is Discord not tracking or updating my progress towards the Lovestruck Quest?

A: To begin tracking progress for the Lovestruck Quest, you must first accept the Quest. If you have already accepted the Quest and are still experiencing issues, please follow these steps:

  1. Close your Sims game.
  2. Restart the Discord browser or desktop app.
  3. Relaunch your Sims game.
  4. Re-check the Quest progress bar to see if the game is now being tracked.

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account.
For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.

Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for Quests.

Q: Why am I unable to see this Quest on the Discord mobile app?

A: This Quest can only be accepted and completed on the Discord browser or Desktop app

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices.

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Gift Inventory or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
