Starting from May 25, 2023 (11:00am EDT) to June 7, 2023 (11:00am EDT), you can claim 1 month of free Discord Nitro from the Epic Games Store! Learn below about how you can claim Discord Nitro.

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: 1 month of free Discord Nitro

Who is Eligible: Users who have not had an active Discord Nitro subscription on their accounts in the past 12 months are eligible.

Users who previously had a Discord Nitro subscription and currently hold a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic subscription that has been active for less than 12 months are ineligible.

If a user currently has a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting only subscription active on their account and you’ve never had a Nitro subscription, redeeming this promotion will override that subscription.

We will not be able to refund any leftover subscription time from those, and the user will be forfeiting the remainder of the existing sub when activating the 1 month Nitro trial.

This promotion is only available in certain regions. If you receive an "Unknown Gift Code" error during the claim/redemption process, you are not eligible to redeem this promotion. You can learn more at this Help Center article.

Requirements: Claim through an email from the Epic Games Store by June 7, 2023 (11:00am EDT), and redeem in Discord account by July 7, 2023 (11:59pm EDT).

Subscription: After the 1 month free trial period, your Nitro subscription will convert to a monthly recurring subscription, and Discord will bill your payment method until you cancel it. You can cancel at any time in your Discord User Settings.

What is Discord Nitro?

Discord Nitro is our subscription plan that gives you access to special perks that’ll make your time on Discord as fun as ever! Personalize and enhance your experience with popular perks like HD streaming (up to 4K and 60fps), 500MB file uploads, profile and avatar customizations, 2 free Server Boosts, and more:

  • Custom Emojis
  • Discord App Color Themes
  • Super Reactions
  • Avatar Decorations
  • Unique Server Profiles
  • Server Boosts
  • More Nitro & Nitro Basic perks

How do I claim the Nitro Promo in the Epic Games Store?

In order to claim this Nitro trial promotion, you must have an Epic Games Store account.

Go to the Epic Game Store app from May 25, 2023 (11:00am EDT) to June 7, 2023 (11:00am EDT) and you should see the Nitro trial promotion on the homepage. Press on the promotion to get started on redeeming Nitro.

Then check your email tied to your Epic Games Store account to find an email from the Epic Games Store about this promotion. Try searching for "Nitro" in your email inbox!

Please make sure the email address associated with your Epic Games Store account is current and correct! Allow for up to 24 hours for email delivery and check your spam folder.

Once you have found the email, press on the button within the email that prompts you to claim the Nitro trial and you'll be automatically redirected to a browser window to redeem the promotion.

The unique promotion link will look like this: https://discord.com/billing/promotions/XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX  

How do I redeem the Nitro Trial on my Discord Account?

Once you have claimed the promo link, you'll now need to officially redeem this promo link on your Discord account (or share this code with a friend). You must redeem this Nitro trial by July 7, 2023 (11:59pm EDT).

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

If you're eligible and ready to redeem this Nitro subscription on your own Discord account, you can follow these steps below to redeem the promo.

NOTE:
- Before redeeming Nitro, make sure you are logged in your own Discord account.
- If you accidentally redeemed the Nitro promo link on an account that is not yours, we are unable to provide you with another promo link.

Step 1 - Pressing the Promo Link/Copy and Pasting into your Browser Window

  • Start by directly pressing the promotion link or right clicking the link to copy and paste the link into your browser window.

 Note: If you're redeeming this Nitro promo link in the Discord Gift Inventory, enter only your unique 24-digit code from the Nitro promotion link in the following format: XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX

Step 2 - Logging into Discord

  •  If you're not already logged into Discord in your browser, you will then be automatically redirected to either:

Register for a New Account

If you don't have an account, you can directly register a new Discord account

Login to your Account

If you already have a Discord account, you can login to your account using your email and password or using QR code login

Step 3 - Confirming the Correct Account and Payment Method

  • Once you have logged into your Discord account, you'll see a new message asking you to confirm that you are accepting the Nitro subscription on the correct Discord account.
    • You'll see a notice saying: "You are accepting this gift as _________" 
  • If your Discord account does not have your payment information, you'll be asked to fill out your payment details. (Note: You will not be charged until the free trial ends and the subscription renews)
  • When you've filled out all the necessary information and you're ready to redeem this promotion, press the "Accept Gift" button. 

Note:
- If you're logged into the incorrect Discord account, press the "Wrong account" button to login to the other account.

Step 4 - Subscription Now Activated

  • After accepting the promo to your account, you'll now be automatically redirected to your Subscriptions tab in your User Settings to see your newly activated Nitro subscription on your account.

What if I am an Active Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting Subscriber? 

Just to clarify - if you previously had a Discord Nitro subscription and currently hold a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic subscription that has been active for less than 12 months, you are ineligible to redeem this promotion.

So long as you currently have a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting only subscription active on your account and you’ve never had a Nitro subscription, you will be able to redeem this promotion and replace it with the 1 month Nitro subscription.

 

HOWEVER, please note:
- By choosing to activate this 1 month trial, you will lose any features associated with your current subscriptions, and **you will not receive a refund for any remaining value on your current subscription**.

At the end of the promotion period, you'll be converted to a paying Nitro subscription per month unless you cancel your subscription.

Payment Info and Subscription Questions

Q: Why do I need to submit my payment info to redeem Nitro?

A: Because Nitro is a subscription plan, at the end of your free 1 month trial, we will start your first billing cycle so you can continue to enjoy Nitro on Discord.

However, you can cancel the Nitro subscription at any time before the end of the 1 month.

Q: Why do I see a temporary hold/charge on my account when this is supposed to be a free promotion?

A: Just to clarify, this Nitro promotion is free, however, there will be a temporary hold placed on the payment method upon activating the promotion. This is to help Discord confirm the payment method works as expected and to prevent the use of fraudulent payment methods. This charge will appear in your Discord transaction history, and it may appear as a hold on your payment method.

We immediately reverse the temporary hold, and you’ll see that reflected in your Discord transaction history. Your bank may take longer to remove the hold from your payment method.

Note: Banks will usually remove the temporary hold from your account within a few business days.

If your Discord transaction history does not show the hold released after 5 business days, please let us know at https://dis.gd/contact, and our support team will be able to investigate this issue further.

Potential Errors and Other Issues

Q: What if I didn't receive an email from Epic Games Store with the Nitro promo link, even if I have an Epic Games Store account?

A: In order to receive an email from Epic Games Store with the Nitro promo link, you must have an Epic Games Store account.

Additionally, make sure that you've opted to receive marketing emails from the Epic Games Store. If you've unsubscribed, you sadly won't be able to see this offer. 

If you believe you are eligible for the promotion, contact Epic Games Store's support team: https://www.epicgames.com/help/en-US/

Q: I see a Sad Trombone error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: Would you happen to be entering this Nitro promo link from the promotion into the Discord Gift Inventory or as a link within Discord chat?

If so, this is the incorrect method for redeeming the Nitro promo link, and you will see an error when attempting to redeem the promotion.

In order to successfully claim this promotion, enter only your unique 24-digit code from the Nitro promotion link in the following format: XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX.

Q: I see a "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you're seeing an "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error, that means that the Discord account you're trying to redeem this promotion on currently has a Nitro subscription so it is ineligible for this Nitro promotion.

Please make sure you're logged into the account that you want to redeem this promotion on. Or you can give the promotion link to a friend who hasn't had Nitro before.

Q: I see a "Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub!" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you're seeing an “Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub” error, that means that you've previously subscribed to Nitro on your Discord account in the past 12 months and you won't be able to redeem this 1 month perk. However, this promo link can be gifted to a friend.

Q: I see a "This gift has already been redeemed" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you are seeing a "This gift has been redeemed already" error, that means that the Nitro promo link has already been used and cannot be used again. You may need to double check if you've redeemed this promo link on another Discord account.

In particular, if you redeemed this promo link using your mobile device, double check the browser on your mobile device and see which Discord account you are currently logged into.

Unfortunately, once the promo link has been redeemed, the promo link cannot be re-enabled, and the Nitro cannot be transferred from one account to another.

Q: I see a "Wait a Second...Looks like this code didn't work." error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you're seeing an “Wait a Second...Looks like this code didn't work.” error, that means that the link is invalid. If you've copy and pasted this link, we recommend you double check the link you were given once more and make sure it exactly matches the link sent to your email.

If you received this link from a friend, double check with your friend on the exact link to make sure that it matches the link in their email.

Please note that our support team is unable to intervene if your friend has given an invalid link and we will not be able to provide a new link.

Q: I see a "Sorry, This Payment Source Cannot Be Used for Redemption" error when trying to redeem my promo link!

A: If you're seeing a "Sorry, This Payment Source Cannot Be Used for Redemption" error, that means that the payment method you've selected to cover the temporary $1 hold cannot be used for that purpose. 

Please keep in mind that prepaid payment methods, such as Gcash, cannot be used to cover temporary holds due to the nature of how prepaid payment methods function.

For more information on temporary holds, please check out this article!

Q: I’m having trouble with my Epic Games Store account.

A: Reach out to Epic Games Store’s support team: https://www.epicgames.com/help/en-US/
