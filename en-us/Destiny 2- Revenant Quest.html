Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Destiny 2: Revenant
Quest Name: Destiny 2: Revenant Quest

Offer Details: Play 15 minutes of Destiny 2: Revenant on PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S to receive the Celestial Ties Shader. 

Who is Eligible: Available to players on PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S where both Discord and Destiny 2: Revenant are available. Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Availability Period: The Destiny 2: Revenant Quest can be accepted on Discord from October 8, 2024 until October 22, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward. Reward gift codes expire December 31, 2024 (UTC -8). 

Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

NOTE: To complete the Quest on PC, you must play Destiny 2: Revenant while running the Discord desktop app simultaneously.
If you decide to complete the Quest on console, the Quest tracking will begin as long as your Discord account is connected via console. For guidance on how to connect and complete the Destiny 2: Revenant Quest on console, you can refer to the following:
  • Discord and PlayStation® Network Connection FAQ
  • Discord and Xbox Connection FAQ

In order to successfully complete the Destiny 2: Revenant Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play Destiny 2: Revenant for a minimum duration of 15 minutes.

Redemption Instructions

1. Once you have completed the Quest by playing 15 minutes of Destiny 2: Revenant, you will receive a reward code in Quest Home (Discovery > Quests). For detailed instructions on how to claim your reward code, please refer to our Discord Quests FAQ.

Once you select Claim Reward, the reward code will also be sent to the email address associated with your Discord account.

2. Then, visit the official Destiny 2: Revenant website's code redemption page and sign into your game account. 

3. Enter the reward code you received.

4. After redeeming, go to the Tower in-game. Find the Special Deliveries terminal between the Cryptarch and Gunsmith to receive your Celestial Ties Shader.

NOTE: On mobile, select the Quest bar. This will bring you to the Quests tab where you can select Claim Reward. 

. 

If you dismiss the mobile Quest bar, you can still find the Quest in Quest Home (Settings > Quests) to claim your reward. For more information about Quests on mobile, see this article.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
