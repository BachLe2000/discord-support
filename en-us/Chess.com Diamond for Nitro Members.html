How do I claim Chess.com Diamond in Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the Chess.com Diamond code in the Gift Inventory from September 4, 2024 (10AM PST) to October 4, 2024 (1PM PST).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active member to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro member).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by October 4, 2024 (1PM PST).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after October 4, 2024 (1PM PST). 

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting member, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

For both the desktop/browser app and mobile app, you can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select Gift Inventory.

2) Click on the Claim button for the Chess.com Diamond Gift

Once you press Claim on the Chess.com Diamond Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12-character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before November 4, 2024 (10AM PST).

How do I redeem the Chess.com Diamond Trial via Chess.com?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your Chess.com account.

Eligibility for Redeeming the Chess.com Diamond Trial

This offer is only available for Chess.com members who are not currently subscribed to any Chess.com Premium Plan during the promotional period. 

Offer not available in the following countries: Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Venezuela, Russia, and Myanmar.

After claiming the Chess.com Diamond offer in your Gift Inventory, you’ll be automatically redirected to Chess.com. If you’re not logged in, you’ll  be prompted to do so. Once logged  your account will be instantly credited with the Chess.com Diamond trial.

NOTE: If you encounter any issues while trying to redeem this promotion and believe your Chess.com account meets the requirements, contact the Chess.com support team for further review: https://support.chess.com.
