How do I claim the Party Animals Exclusive Nitro Bundle in Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member or Nitro Trial user, you'll be able to claim the Party Animals Exclusive Nitro Bundle code in the Gift Inventory from October 31, 2023 [5PM PT] to December 29, 2023 (8PM PT).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active Discord Nitro member or Nitro Trial user, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro member).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by December 29, 2023 (8PM PT).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after December 29, 2023 (8PM PT)

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting member, you'll need to become a Nitro member in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re a Discord Nitro member, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2) Click on the Claim button for the Party Animals Exclusive Nitro Bundle Gift

Once you press Claim on the Party Animals Exclusive Nitro Bundle Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before December 29, 2023 (8PM PT).

How do I redeem the Party Animals Exclusive Nitro Bundle on my Party Animals Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your Party Animals account (or share this code with a friend). Here is how to redeem:

1. Log into your Party Animals account

2. Find the Item Shop located in the bottom left corner of the game and then click on Redeem.

3. Enter your code and then tap on Redeem to fully redeem the code.

Eligibility for Redeeming the Party Animals Exclusive Nitro Bundle

In order to redeem, you will have to have Nitro and own the Party Animals game to be able to redeem the Party Animals Exclusive Nitro Bundle.

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your Party Animals account meets the promotion requirements, reach out to the Party Animals support team to review your Party Animals account further: submit a ticket to https://support.partyanimals.com/ or send an email to support@recreate.games

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you're unable to claim this code on your own Party Animals account, you'll be able to share this code with a friend to use instead.

To share the code, you can copy the 12-character code from the Gift Inventory (in your User Settings in the Discord app) and then share this code to a friend in a Direct Message.
