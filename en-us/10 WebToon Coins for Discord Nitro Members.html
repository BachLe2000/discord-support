What is WEBTOON?

WEBTOON is the largest webcomics community in the world where readers can find new stories or create their own to share. Home to epic sagas, short stories, manga, manhwa, and daily comic strips, access to thousands of creator-owned comics anytime, anywhere across 23 genres, including romance, comedy, action, fantasy, and horror.

Learn more about WEBTOON here!

Want to chat with other readers about your favorite stories, characters, and creators? Join the official WEBTOON server on Discord!

What are WEBTOON Coins?

Coins are currency that are exclusive to the Android and iOS WebToon app. You can use Coins to unlock Fast Pass or completed series available through Daily Pass to read ahead and support your favorite creators. The number of Coins you need to use for each episode may vary depending on the WebToon series.

Coins can also be used to unlock Daily Pass episodes. For each Daily Pass series, you will be able to unlock one Daily Pass episode for free each day. Each unlocked DP episode will be available for 14 days. You can also unlock the episodes using Coins, and these episodes will be available to you for as long as the series is on WEBTOON.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2) Click on the Claim button for the WEBTOON Gift

Once you press Claim on the WEBTOON Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with more details and prompts to begin the redemption process for your gift.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before July 10, 2023 (11:59pm PT).

How do I redeem the WEBTOON Coins offer on my WEBTOON Account?

You must have a WEBTOON account to redeem the 10 free WEBTOON Coins offer from Discord.

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your WEBTOON account (or share this code with a friend).

1) Once you press the Claim button next to the WEBTOON Coins offer in the Gift Inventory, you must follow the prompts to be taken to a special WEBTOON’s redemption page in your browser. Make sure you’re logged into your WEBTOON account!

2) On the WEBTOON  redemption page, input the gift code you have from the Discord Gift Inventory.

3) The 10 free WEBTOON Coins will now be in your WEBTOON account!

If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, reach out to the WEBTOON support team here.

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you're unable to redeem this code on your own WEBTOON account, you'll be able to share this code with a friend to use instead.

To share the code, you can copy the code from the Gift Inventory (in your User Settings in the Discord app) and then share this code to a friend in a Direct Message.
