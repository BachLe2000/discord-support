What Are Leaderboards?

Note: This is currently an experimental feature and is only available to a limited number of League of Legends players on desktop (Windows and Mac OS) and browser.

Leaderboards allow users to share their stats for a game, activity or application, and compete against other members in a server.

Leaderboards via the Desktop App

How to View Leaderboards

For servers in the experiment, a Leaderboard will appear on the Recent Activity section of the Member List. 

League of Legends Displayed as Activity Status in Members List

By hovering over the League of Legends activity in the Members List, you can view the Leaderboard for the server and see where everyone ranks.

Selecting a User in the Leaderboard

How to Join Leaderboards

In order to join Leaderboards, you will need to connect your League of Legends account to your Discord account. If you haven’t connected your League of Legends account already, there are two ways to do this.

Connecting Through the Leaderboard

1. With the Leaderboard opened, tap on Join Leaderboard at the bottom and then follow the prompts to connect your League of Legends account to Discord.

“Join Leaderboard” Button in the Leaderboard

Connecting in User Settings

1. Press on the cogwheel [] in the bottom-left corner to access your User Settings .

2. Select Connections and then choose League of Legends to connect your account.

Connections Tab within User Settings

3. After following the prompts, your League of Legends account will be connected to your Discord account. You can learn more about Connections through the Connections & Linked Roles: Community Members Help Center article. 

How Leaderboard Competition Works

Here's everything you need to know to participate and track your progress:

Viewing the Leaderboard
  • Find the leaderboard in the Recent Activity section of the server's members list.
  • Leaderboards are based on various League of Legends stats, including:
    • Wins
    • Kills
    • Damage taken
  • We may introduce additional stats in future updates.
Rank Updates
  • Your stats and rank updates automatically within 30 minutes after each match.
  • Discord fetches your latest match stats to keep the leaderboard current.
Rewards and Recognition
  • The #1 ranked player receives a special icon next to their name in the server.
  • Leaderboards reset weekly, giving everyone a fresh chance to compete.
Sharing Your Achievements
  • Want to showcase your rank? Simply copy a player's rank from the leaderboard.
  • Paste the rank in any text channel to share with the community.

How to Leave a Leaderboard

You can remove yourself from a server’s leaderboard by tapping on the ellipsis [] and  selecting Leave Leaderboard.

“Leave Leaderboard” Button

How to Customize Your Server’s Leaderboard

Members with the Manage Server permissions enabled can customize how the Leaderboard behaves in the server. 

Manager Server Permission 

1. With the Leaderboard opened, tap on the ellipsis [] and then select Manage Settings. 

Manage Settings Option in Leaderboards

2. There are two ways to customize the leaderboard:

  • Select a different stat as the basis for competition (e.g., kills, deaths, assists, damage taken or dealt, and wins).
  • Disable the role icon in chat.
