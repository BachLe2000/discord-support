Understanding the Nitro Quest Perk

The Nitro Quest Perk is an experimental feature for a limited number of users. We're gradually expanding access to all eligible users.

Earned Quest avatar decorations are now available for use for two months after earning them. However, Nitro subscribers will gain an exclusive perk—they can keep their earned decorations for longer - and in most cases, previously expired decorations will become available again for use!

Requirements to utilize the Nitro Quest Perk:

  • This benefit is exclusively for standard Nitro subscribers (not available for Nitro Basic or Classic).
  • You must have claimed the decoration reward after completing a Quest to be eligible for extended access.
  • Decoration must not have a listed expiry date.

Finding Your Quest Avatar Decorations

Once you've earned a Quest decoration, you can easily access and equip it through your User Settings.

NOTE: For more information about avatar decorations in general, check out our Avatar Decorations article.

Desktop

1. To access your Quest-earned avatar decorations on desktop, head into your User Settings by selecting the cogwheel []. 

The User Settings Icon (Cogwheel) in Discord's Desktop App

2. Once redirected to your User Settings, select the Profiles tab to view your profile settings. 

Accessing Your Profile Through the Profile Tab

3. Then, press the Change Decorations button to open the avatar decoration selection menu.  

4. Under the Your Decorations section, you will find your Quest-earned avatar decorations. 

5. Select Apply once you have chosen the Quest-earned avatar decoration to preview your current avatar decoration. 

Example of a Quest avatar decoration under Your Decoration section

6. After you apply an avatar decoration, you will need to confirm your changes by selecting Save Changes. 

Select "Save Changes" to Apply Your Avatar Decoration

Mobile

1. On your mobile device, head into your User Settings by tapping your avatar. 

The User Settings Icon (Cogwheel) in Discord's Mobile App

2. From here, select Edit Profile. 

This will navigate you to the Profile page where you can tap Avatar Decoration to navigate to the Change Decoration page. You can also tap on your avatar and select the Change Decoration option instead.  

Accessing Your Profile by Pressing “Edit Profile”

3.  In the Change Decoration menu, under the Your Decorations section, you’ll find your Quest-earned avatar decorations. Select the desired avatar decoration you’d like to apply. 

4. Once you’ve found an avatar decoration you want to use, tap Apply then Save in the upper-right corner to confirm your changes. 

  

Example of Setting an Avatar Decoration   

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will I lose access to my decorations if my Nitro subscription expires or I downgrade to Nitro Basic?

A: Yes, you will lose access to these decorations. Extended access to Quest-earned avatar decorations requires an active Nitro subscription. If your subscription expires or you downgrade your subscription to Nitro Basic, you'll lose access to the decorations until you re-subscribe.

Q: Can I regain access to decorations from Quests I completed in the past?

A: Not all past avatar decoration rewards are available - however, Nitro members can access most decorations they previously earned by completing Quests and claiming the rewards.

Q: Do I need to complete the Quest again to get the decoration back?
A: No. If you previously completed the Quest and claimed the reward, you'll automatically regain access to eligible decorations when you subscribe to Nitro.

Q: How do I know how long I can keep a Quest-earned avatar decoration?

A: Most Quest-earned avatar decorations are yours to use with your Discord account. If an avatar decoration has a specific expiry date, you’ll see the expiry date listed under the specific avatar decoration in Profile Settings > Change Decoration.
