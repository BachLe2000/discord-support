How do I claim the YouTube Premium Promo in Discord? 

As long as you're a $9.99 Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the YouTube Premium code in the Gift Inventory from August 9, 2021 (9:00am PT) to October 30, 2021 (11:59pm PT). 

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active subscriber to $9.99 Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro subscriber)!

  • If you are an active $9.99 Discord Nitro subscriber, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by October 30, 2021 (11:59pm PT).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after October 30, 2021 (11:59pm PT). 

Unfortunately, if you are a $4.99 Nitro Classic subscriber, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion!

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to $9.99 Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

 

2) Click on the See Code button for the YouTube Premium Gift

Once you press See Code on the YouTube Premium Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion! 

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before November 30, 2021 (11:59pm PT).

 

[Optional] Check your Email for your YouTube Premium Gift Code

Once you press See Code in the previous step, we’ll also email you with a copy of your code as well! Just search for "YouTube Premium (3 months) Redemption Code" in your email inbox! 

How do I redeem the YouTube Premium Trial on my YouTube Account? 

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially claim this code on your YouTube account (or share this code with a friend)! 

Eligibility for Redeeming the YouTube Premium Trial

To redeem the 3 month YouTube Premium trial code, this offer is only for New YouTube Premium subscribers only.

This offer is not available to current or previous YouTube Premium or YouTube Music Premium customers, or users who have participated in a YouTube Premium, YouTube Music Premium, or Google Play Music trial in the past. Full terms here.

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

Note:
- Make sure you are logged into the correct YouTube account before officially redeeming this YouTube Premium code!
- Once redeemed, the trial cannot be transferred to another account.

In order to redeem the YouTube Premium promotion on your YouTube account, you'll need to copy that code from Discord and head over to https://www.youtube.com/redeem.

Once you're at https://www.youtube.com/redeem, you'll then need to follow these steps below:

  1. Sign into your YouTube Account (if you're not already signed-In)
  2. Enter the 12-character code from your Gift Inventory (or from your email) and then press Next
  3. Press the Try it Free button in the next window that appears (You should see a message for a 3-month free trial.)
  4. Select a payment method or enter your payment info
  5. Confirm that you are ready to proceed and all the information is correct by pressing the Try 3-Month Trial button!

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your YouTube account meets the promotion requirements, reach out to the YouTube support team to review your YouTube account further at this link here!  

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you're unable to claim this code on your own YouTube account, you'll be able to share this code with a friend to use instead!

To share the code, you can either: 

  1. Copy the 12-character code from the Gift Inventory (in your User Settings in the Discord app) and then share this code directly to a friend or...
  2. You can head to your email inbox, search for the email with the subject line "YouTube Premium (3 months) Redemption Code", and then copy this 12-character code to share with your friend.
