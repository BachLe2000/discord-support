What is Picsart Gold?

This means access to: premium editing tools, 20+ million premium stock images, photos, videos, and graphics, cross-platform access on both the mobile app and the web, and more. Learn about Picsart Gold here.

How do I claim the Nitro Promo from my Picsart Gold account?

1) Check Your Email Tied to your Picsart Gold account account or your Picsart Gold account directly

If you’re subscribed to Picsart Gold, start by logging into your email that is tied to your Picsart Gold account, and then check for an email inbox for a new email from the Picsart team.

You can also search for "Nitro" in your email inbox.

You can also log into your Picsart Gold account to find the landing page for the promotion.

2) Press on the red "Claim Nitro Offer" button

Once you have found the email, press on the red "Claim Nitro Offer" button within the email and you'll be automatically redirected to a browser window to redeem the promotion.

The unique promotion link will look like this: https://discord.com/billing/promotions/XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX  

Alternatively, on the promotion’s landing page under your PicsArt Gold account, you can press the “Claim Offer” button to claim the promotion.

Payment Info and Subscription Questions

Q: Why do I need to submit my payment info to redeem Nitro?

A: Because Nitro is a subscription plan, at the end of your free 3 month trial, we will start your first billing cycle so you can continue to enjoy Nitro on Discord.

However, you can cancel the Nitro subscription at any time before the end of the three months.

Q: Why do I see a temporary hold/charge on my account when this is supposed to be a free promotion?

A: Just to clarify, this Nitro promotion is free, however, there will be a temporary hold placed on the payment method upon activating the promotion. This is to help Discord confirm the payment method works as expected and to prevent the use of fraudulent payment methods. This charge will appear in your Discord transaction history, and it may appear as a hold on your payment method.

We immediately reverse the temporary hold, and you’ll see that reflected in your Discord transaction history. Your bank may take longer to remove the hold from your payment method.

Note: Banks will usually remove the temporary hold from your account within a few business days.

If your Discord transaction history does not show the hold released after 5 business days, please let us know at https://dis.gd/contact, and our support team will be able to investigate this issue further.

Potential Errors and Other Issues

Q: What if I didn't receive an email from Picsart with the Nitro promo link, even if I'm a Picsart Gold subscriber?

A: In order to receive an email from Picsart with the Nitro promo link, you must be a current Picsart Gold subscriber. 

Additionally, make sure that you've opted to receive marketing emails from Picsart. If you've unsubscribed, you sadly won't be able to see this offer. 

If you believe you are eligible for the promotion, contact Picsart’s support team: support@picsart.com

Q: I see a Sad Trombone error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: Would you happen to be entering this Nitro promo link from the Picsart premium promotion into the Discord Gift Inventory or as a link within Discord chat?

If so, this is the incorrect method for redeeming the Nitro promo link, and you will see an error when attempting to redeem the promotion.

In order to successfully claim this promotion, try opening up an incognito browser and then copy and pasting the promotion link into your browser's address bar (also known as a location bar or URL bar). 

Q: I see a "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you're seeing an "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error, that means that the Discord account you're trying to redeem this promotion on currently has a Nitro subscription so it is ineligible for this Nitro promotion.

Please make sure you're logged into the account that you want to redeem this promotion on. Or you can give the promotion link to a friend who hasn't had Nitro before.

Q: I see a "Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub!" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you're seeing an “Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub” error, that means that you've previously subscribed to Nitro on your Discord account and you won't be able to redeem this 3 month perk. However, this promo link can be gifted to a friend.

If you have never subscribed to Nitro, please submit a ticket through our support system: https://dis.gd/contact and our support team will be able to investigate further.

Be sure to include your full Discord ID and email address tied to your Discord account

Q: I see a "This gift has already been redeemed" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you are seeing a "This gift has been redeemed already" error, that means that the Nitro promo link has already been used and cannot be used again. You may need to double check if you've redeemed this promo link on another Discord account.

In particular, if you redeemed this promo link using your mobile device, double check the browser on your mobile device and see which Discord account you are currently logged into.

Unfortunately, once the promo link has been redeemed, the promo link cannot be re-enabled, and the Nitro cannot be transferred from one account to another.

Q: I see a "Wait a Second...Looks like this code didn't work." error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo link.

A: If you're seeing an “Wait a Second...Looks like this code didn't work.” error, that means that the link is invalid. If you've copy and pasted this link, we recommend you double check the link you were given once more and make sure it exactly matches the link sent to your email.

If you received this link from a friend, double check with your friend on the exact link to make sure that it matches the link in their email.

Please note that our support team is unable to intervene if your friend has given an invalid link and we will not be able to provide a new link.

Q: I’m having trouble with my Picsart Gold account.

A: Reach out to Picsart Gold’s support team: support@picsart.com
