You can cancel any of your Discord subscriptions at any time. Once canceled, your subscription will end at the end of your current billing period. 

Subscriptions need to be canceled on the same device type where the purchase was originally made.
For example:
If you purchased Nitro through the desktop app, you must cancel it through the desktop app or browser.
If you purchased through a mobile device, you can only cancel through a mobile device.

How do I Cancel my Nitro or Nitro Basic subscription?

Desktop/Browser

1. Tap on cogwheel [] next to your Discord username to access your User Settings.

2. Next, navigate to the Subscriptions tab.

3. Now, tap on Cancel within the Nitro or Nitro Basic banner to cancel your subscription. 

4. Finally, follow the prompts to cancel your subscription. 

Mobile

1. Tap on your avatar in the bottom right.

2. Then, tap on the cogwheel [] in the top right.

3. Next, scroll down to Billing Settings and tap on Manage Nitro.

4. Here you can cancel your Nitro or Nitro Basic subscription. Follow the prompts on screen until the cancellation is confirmed.

Once the cancellation has been completed, your Manage Nitro settings will show the date on which your subscription will expire and you will lose your Nitro benefits..

Alternatively, you can also cancel your Discord subscriptions directly on your mobile device settings by following these instructions for Apple iOS subscriptions or Google Play subscriptions

How do I Cancel my Server Boost subscription?

If all of your Server Boosts subscriptions are canceled at any point, the Server Boosting badge will be removed from your profile. You’ll have a grace period of 3 days to resubscribe and reapply your Server Boost to a server.

Desktop/Browser

1. Tap on the cogwheel [] next to your Discord username to access your User Settings.

2. Next, navigate to the Server Boost tab.

3. Here you will see all of your current Server Boosts.

Tap on the 3 dots or vertical ellipsis next to the Server Boost that you want to cancel.Then, tap on Cancel Boost Subscription.

4. A confirmation pop-up will appear and inform you of when the Server Boost will officially be removed and the date that it’ll be removed.

Tap on Next to confirm and follow the prompts to successfully cancel the Server Boost.

Mobile

1. Tap on your avatar in the bottom right.

2. Then tap on the cogwheel [] in the top right.

3. Next, scroll down to Billing Settings and tap on Manage Nitro.

How do I Cancel my Server subscription?

1. Tap on cogwheel [] next to your Discord username to access your User Settings.

2. Then head to the Subscriptions tab.

3. Here you should see a banner for your Server Subscriptions. To cancel, tap on Manage Memberships.

4. Here you will see all of your current Server Subscriptions.

To cancel, tap on the dropdown next to the Server Subscription you want to cancel and then tap on Cancel my Membership.

5. Follow the prompts to fully cancel your Server Subscription.

How do I Cancel my Premium App subscription?

1. Tap on cogwheel [] next to your Discord username to access your User Settings.

2. Then head to the Subscriptions tab.

3. Next, tap on Manage next to your Premium Membership banner.

4. Here you will see all of your current Server Subscriptions.

To cancel, tap on the dropdown next to the Server Subscription you want to cancel and then tap on Cancel my Membership.

5. Follow the prompts to fully cancel your Premium Membership.

How do I Re-subscribe after canceling my subscription?

If you’re trying to re-subscribe on your mobile device, the steps might be a little different. Please head to the Manage Nitro tab under your User Settings in order to re-subscribe your subscription.

If you’ve changed your mind and no longer wish for your Discord subscription to end at the end of your billing period, then you can always reactivate it. Just make sure you do before the billing period ends, otherwise you’ll just have to purchase a new subscription. Here’s how to re-subscribe:

1. Tap on cogwheel [] next to your Discord username to access your User Settings.

2. Next, navigate to the Subscriptions tab.

3. Now, tap on Re-Subscribe within the corresponding banner to uncancel your subscription. 

You’ll be able to tell it was reactivated, because you’ll see the banner no longer has Pending Cancellation listed.

What if I can’t access my Discord and need to cancel?

If your Discord purchase was through an Apple device, please reach out to Apple to cancel your subscription.

If you’re unable to log into your Discord account for any reason and cancel your subscription (for desktop, browser, or even through an Android device) yourself, you can reach out to our Billing Support team under the email address tied to your Discord account and request it to be canceled through here: http://dis.gd/billing. 

If you want to submit a refund request instead, and your purchase is within our Refund Policy, please follow the steps outlined here.
