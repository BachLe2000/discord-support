What’s the Quick Switcher?

The Quick Switcher is a powerful navigation tool in Discord that lets you instantly jump between different parts of the app. Think of it as a search bar that helps you quickly find and switch to any channel, direct message, or server without clicking through menus. It's especially useful for power users who want to navigate Discord efficiently using their keyboard.

How do I use the Quick Switcher?

Navigate effortlessly between channels, DMs, and servers with Quick Switch. Simply press CTRL+K or ⌘+K to open the switcher and instantly jump to your desired destination.

Use these Quick Switch prefixes to find exactly what you need:

Prefix Action
***** Find and switch between servers
@ Access direct messages with users
# Navigate to text channels
! Find and join voice channels

Using the @ Prefix in Quick Switcher

Need to return to the most recent channel? Just hit the RETURN or ENTER key to return to your previous channel.
