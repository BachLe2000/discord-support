What this article covers:
  • Desktop/Browser
  • Mobile
Note: The Discord Support Team cannot change the email address associated with your account. To update your email, you'll need access to the inbox of your current email address.

Desktop/Browser

1. Tap on the cogwheel [] next to your Discord username to access your User Settings.

Example of the cogwheel icon through Discord desktop/browser

2. Make sure you're on the My Account tab.

The My Account tab located in your User Settings

3. Press the Edit button next to your account's current email address.

Example of what the Edit button looks like in your My Account tab

4. Follow the instructions of the in-client pop-up to change the email address then press Done once completed.

Example of what the Enter an email address pop-up looks like

5. Be sure to check the inbox of your updated email address for a verification email!

Mobile

1. Head over to your User Profile by selecting your avatar icon in the bottom-right corner. 

Example of the avatar icon in the bottom-right corner of the mobile app

2.  Tap on the cogwheel [] in the top-right corner.

 

Example of the cogwheel icon to tap to access your User Settings

3. Tap on Account.

Example of the Account tab in the mobile app

4. Tap on your current email address to change it.

Example of the Account tab

4. Follow the on-screen instructions to change your email. Once completed, tap on Change Email.

Example of what it looks like when you go to change your email address

5. Be sure to check the inbox of your updated email address for a verification email.

Note: If your email was changed and you want to undo it for any reason, please check out the My Discord Email was Changed and I Want to Undo It Help Center article.
