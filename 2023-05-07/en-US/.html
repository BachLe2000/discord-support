Speaking of this year’s charity, we’re partnering with Crisis Text Line for Snowsgiving 2022. Crisis Text Line is a not-for-profit that provides 24/7 text-based mental health support and crisis intervention in English and in Spanish.

Charity Partner: Crisis Text Line

Crisis Text Line is a not-for-profit organization that provides free, 24/7, high-quality

text-based mental health support and crisis intervention in English and Spanish. Since its launch in 2013, Crisis Text Line has trained over 55,000 volunteers and engaged in more than 7.5 million crisis conversations.

Supporting Crisis Text Line’s service through donations keeps the service free and helps a texter in need reach a trained volunteer Crisis Counselor. Investing in Crisis Text Line will also expand our additional programs that support research, workforce development, and higher education collaborations to address the largest systematic issues contributing to the current mental health crisis.

Individuals seeking support through Crisis Text Line can text DISCORD to 741741 or 443-SUPPORT in WhatsApp to be connected to a live, trained volunteer Crisis Counselor or text HOLA to 741741 or 442-AYUDAME in WhatsApp for Spanish.

Mental Health support continues to be a focus for us here at Discord and we chose Crisis Text Line as a partner this year as part of our core belief to ensure Discord is a safe, inclusive place where everyone can find community. We established a wonderful partnership with them earlier this year, and because they offer support in various regions in both English and Spanish, we wanted to continue supporting the important work they are doing.

You can learn more about Crisis Text Line at their website.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long is Discord Snowsgiving?

The main Snowsgiving event will be from December 5, 2022 to December 12, 2022. Snowsgiving themes will deactivate on January 3, 2023.

Q: Where can I find the Snowsgiving tab in my app?

A: The Snowsgiving tab is only available on your desktop and browser application. You can check out the steps above for where to find it! However, if you do not have the desktop application and are a mobile user, you can visit the Snowsgiving page here.

Q: I missed claiming a previous day’s reward from the Snowsgiving page, can I still get the rewards?

A: Yes, the Snowsgiving rewards will be available for you from now until Monday, December 12, 2022.

Q: How will I get access to my Snowsgiving prizes? Some of the prizes are available by download only. Is this safe?

A: All the rewards will be provided on the Snowsgiving page from downloadable links. Don't be alarmed by the downloads—these items are being provided directly from Discord.

Q: Why can’t I use or access some of the Snowsgiving rewards (e.g. animated emojis, stickers, etc)?

A: As part of Snowsgiving, we wanted to give users fun in-app rewards ranging from emojis and animated emojis to stickers and profile banners. However, some rewards, like animated emojis, profile banners, and this year’s seasonal sticker pack are intended to be fun extras that can only be used by Nitro subscribers. This is not available for Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, and Server Boost subscribers.

Q: Will I lose access to the Snowsgiving emojis?

A: You will not lose access to the Snowsgiving emojis so long as you download the emojis from the Snowsgiving page here. Otherwise, you will lose access to the emojis as the end of Snowsgiving.
