Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Street Fighter 6
Quest Name: Street Fighter 6 Quest

Offer Details: Play 15 minutes of Street Fighter 6 on PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S to receive a Street Fighter 6 Battle Field avatar decoration. You can learn more about avatar decorations here. 

NOTE: The Street Fighter 6 demo version cannot be used to complete the Quest. You will need to have the complete Street Fighter 6 game to fulfill the Quest.

Who is Eligible: Available to players on PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S where both Discord and Street Fighter 6 are available. Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

NOTE: The Street Fighter 6 Battle Field avatar decoration is a limited-time avatar decoration and will only be yours as an avatar decoration until November 29, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). After this date, you will no longer have access to this avatar decoration.

Availability Period: The Street Fighter 6 Quest can be accepted on Discord from September 25, 2024 until September 29, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.

Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

NOTE: To complete the Quest on PC, you must play Street Fighter 6 while running the Discord desktop app simultaneously.
If you decide to complete the Quest on console, the Quest tracking will begin as long as your Discord account is connected via console. For guidance on how to connect and complete the Street Fighter 6 Quest on console, you can refer to the following:  
  • Discord and PlayStation® Network Connection FAQ
  • Discord and Xbox Connection FAQ

In order to successfully complete the Street Fighter 6 Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play Street Fighter 6 for a minimum duration of 15 minutes.

Redemption Instructions

After completing the Quest by playing 15 minutes of Street Fighter 6 you'll be eligible to earn the Street Fighter 6 Battle Field avatar decoration. You can participate in this Quest until September 29, 2024 (11:59 PM UTC) and claim your reward by October 29, 2024.

You can use the following methods to claim your Street Fighter 6 Battle Field avatar decoration:

Option 1

1. Head over to the Discover tab by selecting the Discover icon []  at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app. 

2. This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here.

3. While here, select Claim Reward for the Street Fighter 6 Quest. 

NOTE: On mobile, select the Quest bar. This will bring you to the Quests tab where you can select Claim Reward. 

 

If you dismiss the mobile Quest bar, you can still find the Quest in Quest Home (Settings > Quests) to claim your reward. For more information about Quests on mobile, see this article.

Option 2

You can also claim your Street Fighter 6 Battle Field avatar decoration by pressing the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar.  

Learn more about avatar decorations and how to apply them here. 

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
