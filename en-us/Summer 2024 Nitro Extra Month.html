Promotion Summary

Offer Details: Get 1 extra month of Discord Nitro when you subscribe to a Nitro subscription (Nitro Monthly or Nitro Yearly) on Discord’s desktop or browser app.

  • Promotion also applies to eligible upgrades from Nitro Basic and Nitro Classic to Nitro.
  • You will receive your extra month at the end of your first month or first year, depending on the Nitro plan you choose.

Note: Nitro Basic and mobile (Apple/Google Play) Nitro subscription sign ups are not eligible for this promotion.

Who is Eligible: 

  • Discord users who have not had a Nitro subscription in the past 10 days prior to the promotion claim date. 
  • Members who have a Nitro Basic or Classic plan that upgrade to Nitro.

Unfortunately, this promotion won’t be available for:

  • Current Nitro members, including those on free trials or discounted plans.
  • Users who had a Nitro subscription within 10 days prior to the date they attempted to claim this promotion. 
  • Sign ups made through the Discord mobile app (iOS and Android).

Requirements: This promotion is only available for purchases on our desktop and browser apps, and only available in certain regions. If you receive a "Sadly something went wrong here. You won't be able to use this code." error during the claim process, you are not eligible for this promotion.

Deadline for Claiming Promo: This promotion must be claimed before July 31, 2024 (10:00 AM PT).

How do I claim my extra month of Nitro?

Note: If you do not see the promotion within your Nitro tab after refreshing your app, this means that you are not eligible for this promotion.

Step 1: Go to the Nitro tab

On the desktop or browser app, press the Direct Messages button in the top left corner and then select the Nitro tab. Alternatively, you can navigate to your User Settings > Nitro.

Note: You cannot claim this promotion through the mobile (iOS and Android) apps. If you are eligible and would like to receive this promotion, please log into your account through the Discord desktop or browser app.

Step 2: Subscribe to Nitro Monthly or Yearly 

Once you are on the Nitro page - simply press Claim Offer and select either the monthly or yearly plan for Nitro.

On the next screen, enter your credit card information and other payment details.

Note: This promo will only allow users to receive 1 extra month of Nitro. If you select the Yearly option - you will still only receive 1 extra month of Nitro, not 1 year.

Step 3: Receive Your Month of Nitro

Upon successful completion of the payment, your account will be upgraded to Nitro!

Your additional month of Nitro will automatically be applied after the end of your first month or year.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't see the promotion details on my Nitro tab. Why?

A: If you don't see any promotion details on your Nitro tab on the desktop or browser (this promotion is not available on the mobile apps), it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria.

Q: If I become a Nitro Yearly member during this promotion, what will I receive?

A: If you become a Nitro Yearly member during this promotion, you will receive 1 extra month of Nitro at the end of your yearly subscription.

Q: If I upgrade my subscription from Nitro Basic or Nitro Classic to Nitro, can I receive this promotion?

A: Yes, this promotion applies for upgrades from Nitro Basic or Classic to Nitro.

Q: How much will I be charged if I upgrade my plan to Nitro?

A: We prorate the amount you'll be charged for upgrading to Nitro, which depends on:
1. Whether you're upgrading from Basic or Classic
2. The amount of your existing subscription plan you haven’t used
3. The remaining amount of your new subscription plan you will use after upgrading
For example, if you've used Nitro Basic for only 10 days this month, you'll only be charged for those 10 days at Basic's price. Then, if you upgrade to Nitro mid-month and have 20 days left, you'll only pay for those 20 days at Nitro's price, rather than for the entire month. You can find more details here.

Q: I claimed and redeemed a different trial for Discord Nitro already. Can I access this promo?

A: Unfortunately, if you are currently on a free trial, discounted plan or have been in the last 10 days before attempting to claim, you are not eligible for this promotion. Have no fear, there will be more of these down the road! 

Q: If I have Nitro credit in my account, can I apply it to this promo?

A: If you have credit in your account from a previous Nitro subscription, this credit will be automatically applied to the purchase total when you check out.

If you have credit in your account that is associated with a different Nitro subscription type (ex: Nitro Classic), it will not apply to this promotion.

Q: I see a notification saying "You're ineligible for this offer". Why?

A: If you encounter this notification, it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria outlined above.

Q: Will my subscription renew automatically after the promotion ends?

A: Yes your subscription will renew automatically after the promotion ends. If you’d like to make changes to your subscription, including cancel, you can find more information here. 

Q: I purchased a Nitro subscription during the promotion period, but I’d like to request a refund. What are the steps?

A: Before requesting a refund, please review our Refund Policy to ensure that your purchase is eligible for a refund.

If you’d like to proceed, please send in a ticket specifying that you’d like a refund for your Nitro purchase to our support team here: dis.gd/billing and our agents will be happy to assist you.

Please note that if your refund is issued, you will lose any promotional value or discounts associated with the purchase.

Q: Can I gift my month of Nitro to someone else?

A: No, this month of Nitro is yours to keep and will be automatically applied to your account. It can’t be gifted or transferred to another user.
