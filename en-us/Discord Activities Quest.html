Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Any Discord Activity
Quest Name: Discord Activities Quest

Offer Details: Play any Activity for 15 minutes on the Discord desktop app to receive a Nitro trial! 

Who is Eligible: Available to eligible US-based users through Discord's desktop app where Discord Activities are available.

Availability Period: The Discord Activities Quest can be accepted on Discord from September 16, 2024 until September 26, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward. 

The Nitro trial must be claimed by October 3, 2024.

Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

NOTE: The Discord Activities Quest is available to a limited number of Discord users within the US at this time. 

In order to successfully complete the Discord Activities Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play an Activity on Discord for a minimum duration of 15 minutes.

Redemption Instructions

Once you have completed the Quest by playing 15 minutes of an Activity, you’ll be able to claim the Nitro trial. 

NOTE: If the Quest is not completed by September 26, 2024, the Quest will no longer be available within your Quest Home (Discover > Quests). 

You can use the following methods to claim your trial:

Option 1

1. Head over to the Discover tab by selecting the Discover icon []  at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app. 

2. This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here.

3. While here, select Claim Reward for the Discord Activities Quest to begin your Nitro trial. 

Option 2

You can also claim your Nitro trial by pressing the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar.  

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: The Quest Home page (Discover > Quests) and this Discord Activities Quest are available to a limited number of eligible Discord users at this time. If you can't access Quest Home or accept the Discord Activity Quest, unfortunately you're not eligible at this time.

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This Quest is available on both the desktop and browser versions of Discord. However, the Discord Activities Quest can only be completed through the desktop app. 

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Do I need to submit my payment info to redeem the Nitro trial?

A: No, you will not need to submit your payment info to redeem this Nitro trial.

Q: Who can redeem this Nitro trial to their Discord account?

A: This Quest is an experimental feature available only to a select number of Discord users. Note: if you have subscribed to Nitro after accepting the Discord Activities Quest, you will no longer be eligible to participate in the Quest or claim the reward.  

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
