What are Discord Quests? 

Quests are a way for players to discover games and earn rewards for playing them on Discord.

How do I complete a Discord Quest?

Here are the steps to complete a Discord Quest: 

1. Accept the Quest 

2. Stream the game to friends on Discord

3. Claim your reward 

Please see below for more detailed information on how to complete each step. 

Accept the Quest 

Quests can only be accepted through the Discord desktop and browser apps.

You may see an alert in your Discord app about a Discord Quest. Hover over the Quest bar and select it to expand and learn more about the Quest. If you want to participate, you can press Accept Quest. 

If you don't see the Quest bar, navigate to your User Settings > Gift Inventory.

While there, you will see the active Discord Quests that are available to join. Press Accept Quest to join the quest.

Stream the Game to Friends on Discord 

To complete a Quest requirement, you must stream the game to friends through the Discord desktop app. 

Once you join a Quest, you will need to stream the game on Discord to at least one friend for a short period of time. Each Quest will have its own required time to stream and you can confirm how long you need to stream the game for in the Promotion Terms. 

1. Before you can begin the Quest, launch the partnered game.

2. Then, head to your Discord desktop/browser app and navigate to a server of your choice.

3. Once there, join a voice channel and select the Screen Share [] icon located in the voice status panel. Be sure to select the individual application for the partnered game before you Go Live. 

NOTE: You must select the game application to stream! Streaming your device’s screen will not progress your Quest forward. Once you stream, you can check the progress bar on the Gift inventory to ensure progress is being made on the Quest.  

4. When you’re ready to stream your gameplay, press Go Live. You can learn more about streaming your game here. 

Remember that each Quest has specific Quest time requirements to complete. You may have to stream for a certain amount of time and have some friends watch your stream!

Claim Your Reward 

Once you have completed the Quest, you can claim the reward by pressing the Claim Reward button within any Discord Quest in-app notification.

The easiest way to claim your reward is by heading over to your User Settings > Gift Inventory.  

When you claim your reward you will be given a Discord Quest redemption code that you will need to redeem on the partnered game redemption website. You can find a link by heading over to our Active Discord Quests section! You will also receive an email with redemption instructions once you’ve completed the Quest. 

Opting-out of Quests Promotions

If you’re not interested in receiving promotions of Discord Quests, you can control these promotions in the following ways: 

Hide an In-app Promotion of an Individual Quest 

If you still want to receive in-app promotions for future Quests, but you want to hide an in-app promotion for a specific Quest or game you’re not interested in, press the ellipsis [] on the Quest in-app promotion to access the menu and then select Hide This to hide this particular in-app promotion. 

Once you've done this, you'll no longer receive notifications about this particular Quest in that particular in-app promotion style. 

If you encounter a different in-app promotion for that same Quest (for example, you may also see the Quest in the Active Now section of the Discord app) you can hide those by following the steps mentioned above.

If you hide all the in-app promotions of a specific Quest, you can still view and complete the Quest in your Gift Inventory. 

Opt-out of personalized promotion for all Quests 

If you do not want to receive personalized promotions for any Quests based on your activity or data you share with Discord, you can disable your In game rewards (aka Quests) settings within your Privacy & Safety settings. 

1. Press the cogwheel [] in the bottom left corner of your Discord desktop/browser app to navigate to your User Settings.

2. Then select the Privacy & Safety tab in the left hand panel and scroll down to the How We Use Your Data section.

3. Disable the In-game rewards (aka Quests) setting by selecting the toggle. You can learn more about how data is used for in-game rewards here. 

Although these settings are disabled, you will still be able to participate in Quests if you navigate to your Gift Inventory. You may see Quests shared by your friends or reflected in their activity status on Discord.  

If you decide later that you want to receive notifications about Discord Quests based on your activity, you will need to make sure to enable the In-game rewards (aka Quests) setting in Privacy & Safety. You can learn more about these settings by visiting Data Privacy Controls or reading our Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.

Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for Quests.

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Gift Inventory or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
