Picsart Gold is a subscription plan for unlimited access to Picsart’s premium tools and content.

This means access to: premium editing tools, 20+ million premium stock images, photos, videos, and graphics, cross-platform access on both the mobile app and the web, and more. Learn about Picsart Gold here.

How do I claim the Picsart Gold Promo in Discord?

As long as you're a $9.99 Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the Picsart Gold code in the Gift Inventory from March 22, 2022 (12:00am PDT) to May 31, 2022 (11:59pm PDT).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

This promotion is only available in certain regions. If you receive an "Unknown Gift Code" error during the claim/redemption process, you are not eligible to redeem this promotion. You can learn more at this Help Center article.

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active subscriber to $9.99 Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro subscriber).

  • If you are an active $9.99 Discord Nitro subscriber, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by May 31, 2022 (11:59pm PDT).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after May 31, 2022 (11:59pm PDT). 

Unfortunately, if you are a $4.99 Nitro Classic subscriber, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to $9.99 Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2) Click on the Claim button for the Picsart Gold Gift

Once you press Claim on the Picsart Gold Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before June 30, 2022 (11:59pm PDT).

How do I redeem the Picsart Gold Trial on my Picsart Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your Picsart account (or share this code with a friend).

Eligibility for Redeeming the Picsart Gold Trial

To redeem the 3 month Picsart Gold trial code, this offer is only for new, first-time Picsart Gold subscribers.

Further terms and conditions to redeem this promotion can be found at the bottom of this article.

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

Note: Make sure you are logged into the correct Picsart account before officially redeeming this Picsart Gold code. Once redeemed, the trial cannot be transferred to another account.

In order to redeem the Picsart Gold promotion on your Picsart account, press on Redeem to be taken to the Picsart website or go to this link: https://picsart.com/discord-trial

Once you're on the Picsart redemption page, you'll then need to follow these steps below:

1) Sign into your Picsart Account if you're not already signed-in or create your Picsart account.

2) Enter the 12-character code from your Gift Inventory and then press Redeem Offer.

3) Select a subscription plan and enter/choose your payment method.

4) Confirm that you are ready to proceed and all the information is correct by pressing the Start Free Trial button.

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your Picsart account meets the promotion requirements, reach out to the Picsart support team to review your Picsart account further: support@picsart.com. 

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you're unable to claim this code on your own Picsart account, you'll be able to share this code with a friend to use instead.

To share the code, you can copy the 12-character code from the Gift Inventory (in your User Settings in the Discord app) and then share this code to a friend in a Direct Message.
