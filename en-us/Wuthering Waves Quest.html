Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Wuthering Waves

Quest Name: V1.2 WUWA Quest

Offer Details: Stream 15 minutes of Wuthering Waves to a friend and receive the V1.2 WUWA Pack gift code. Reward gift codes expire October 9, 2024 (4AM UTC).

Who is Eligible: Available to the following regions US, Canada, UK, JP, NZ, and AUS on PC where both Discord and Wuthering Waves are available. 

Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Availability Period: The V1.2 WUWA Quest can be accepted on Discord from September 5, 2024 until September 9, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.

Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

Once you have accepted the Quest, you must stream the game through the Discord desktop app and have at least one friend actively watching the stream to complete the Quest requirement.

In order to successfully complete the V1.2 WUWA Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must live stream their Wuthering Waves gameplay to a friend via a Discord voice channel for a minimum duration of 15 minutes on the desktop app. 

1. Before you can begin the Quest, launch the game.

2. Then, head to your Discord desktop app and navigate to a direct message (DM), group chat, or a  server of your choice.

3. Once there, start a voice call or join a voice channel and select the Screen Share [] icon located in the voice status panel. Be sure to select the individual application for the game before you Go Live. 

NOTE: You must select the game application to stream! Streaming your device’s screen will not progress your Quest forward. Once you stream, you can check your progress on the Quest bar or via Quest Home (Discovery > Quests) to ensure progress is being made on the Quest.

4. When you’re ready to stream your gameplay, press Go Live. You can learn more about streaming your game here. 

Redemption Instructions

Option 1

1. Once you have completed the Quest by streaming 15 minutes of Wuthering Waves, you will receive a gift code in Quest Home (Discovery > Quests). For detailed instructions on how to redeem your gift code, please refer to our Discord Quests FAQ.

2. Then, select your server and enter the game, and then go to Settings > Other > Redemption Code.

3. Enter the Redemption Code and press Confirm to redeem rewards. 

4. The Redemption Code function will be unlocked once you've reached Union Level 2. This must be completed by October 9, 2024 (4AM UTC).

5. After confirming your rewards, you can then check your in-game mail for the items.

Note: If the Quest is not completed by September 9, 2024, the Quest will no longer be available within Quest Home (Discovery > Quests). 

Option 2

You can also claim your V1.2 WUWA Pack gift code by pressing the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar. 

Once you select Claim Reward, the gift code will also be sent to the email address associated with your Discord account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discovery > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
