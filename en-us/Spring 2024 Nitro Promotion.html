Eligibility for Claiming the Spring Nitro Promo

Wondering whether you’ll be able to claim this promotion? As long as you meet the criteria  below, you should see the promotion in your Nitro tab!

Note: After refreshing your client - if you do not see the promotion within your Nitro tab - this means that you are not eligible for this promotion.

This promotion is currently available for:

  • First time Nitro Monthly or Nitro Yearly members.
  • Users who have had a Nitro membership in the past, but not within the last 7 days.
  • Active Nitro Basic and Classic members who upgrade to Nitro.
  • You can only access this promotion through the Discord desktop or browser app.

Unfortunately, this promotion won’t be available for:

How do I claim my Gyoiko Sakura Avatar Decoration?

Step 1: Go to the Nitro tab

On the desktop or browser app, press the Direct Messages button in the top left corner and then select the Nitro tab. Alternatively, you can navigate to your User Settings > Nitro.

Note: You cannot claim this promotion through the mobile (iOS and Android) apps. If you are eligible and would like to receive this promotion, please log into your account through the Discord desktop or browser app.

Step 2: Subscribe to Nitro Monthly or Yearly

Once you are at the Nitro page - simply select Join Nitro or Upgrade and select either the monthly or yearly plan for Nitro.

On the next screen, enter your credit card information and other payment details.

Step 3: Receive Your Gyoiko Sakura Avatar Decoration

Upon successful completion of the payment, your account will be upgraded to Nitro!

Your Gyoiko Sakura avatar decoration will automatically be available in your inventory.

If you meet the eligibility requirements and didn’t receive your Gyoiko Avatar Decoration when signing up for Nitro by May 1, 2024 (10:00AM PST), please reach out to our Support Team for further assistance.

Step 4: How to Apply Your Avatar Decoration

1. Once you have claimed the Gyoiko Sakura Avatar Decoration, you can head over to your User Settings by pressing the cogwheel [] icon in the bottom left corner. 

2. Then, select Profiles > Change Decoration where you will find all of your decorations. 

3. While in the Change Decoration window, select your Gyoiko Sakura avatar decoration and press Apply. 

4. Be sure to confirm your changes by pressing Save Changes and you should be all set!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't see the promotion details on my Nitro tab. Why?

A: If you don't see any promotion details on your Nitro tab on the desktop or browser (this promotion is not available on the mobile clients), it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria.

Q: I’m a current Nitro member. Am I eligible to get the Gyoiko Sakura avatar decoration?

A: If you are a Nitro member prior to the start of this promotion, excluding Basic and Classic members, you are not eligible to receive the Gyoiko Sakura avatar decoration at this time. We are cooking up more exciting things for current Nitro members - so stay tuned! In the meantime, you can check out all of the current Nitro benefits, including discounts and Nitro exclusive items in the Shop, here.

Q: Where can I find my new avatar decoration and how do I apply it?

A: You can head over to your User Settings > Profiles > Change Decoration to find your claimed avatar decoration. While in the Change Decoration window, select the avatar decoration you would like to use and select Apply > Save Changes.  

You can learn more about avatar decorations here. 

Q: What happens if I don't receive my Gyoiko Avatar Decoration?
A: If you meet the eligibility requirements and didn’t receive your Gyoiko Avatar Decoration when signing up for Nitro by May 1, 2024 (10:00AM PST), please reach out to our Support Team for further assistance.

Q: Do I need Nitro to use the Gyoiko Sakura avatar decoration?

A: Yes, you will need an active Nitro membership to use this avatar decoration as it’s considered Nitro Exclusive.

Q: If I upgrade my subscription from Nitro Basic or Nitro Classic to Nitro, can I receive this promotion?

A: Yes, this promotion applies for upgrades from Nitro Basic or Classic to Nitro.

Q: How much will I be charged if I upgrade my plan to Nitro?

A: The amount you'll be charged for upgrading to Nitro depends on:
1. Whether you're upgrading from Basic or Classic
2. The amount of your existing membership plan you haven’t used
3. The remaining amount of your new membership plan you will use after upgrading

For example, if you've used Nitro Basic for only 10 days this month, you'll only be charged for those 10 days at Basic's price. Then, if you upgrade to Nitro mid-month and have 20 days left, you'll only pay for those 20 days at Nitro's price, rather than for the entire month. You can find more details about this payment breakdown here.

Q: I claimed and redeemed a different free trial for Discord Nitro already. Can I access this promo?

A: Unfortunately, if you are currently on a free trial or have been in the last 7 days before attempting to claim, you are not eligible for this promotion. Have no fear, there will be more of these down the road! 

Q: If I have Nitro credit in my account, can I apply it to this promo?

A: If you have credit in your account from a previous Nitro membership, this credit will be automatically applied to the purchase total when you check out.

If you have credit in your account that is associated with a different Nitro membership type (ex: Nitro Classic), it will not apply to this promotion.

Q: I see a notification saying "You're ineligible for this offer". Why?

A: If you encounter this notification, it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria outlined above.

Q: I purchased a Nitro membership during the promotion period, but I’d like to request a refund. What are the steps?

A: Before requesting a refund, please review our Refund Policy to ensure that your purchase is eligible for a refund.

If you’d like to proceed, please send in a ticket specifying that you’d like a refund for your Nitro purchase to our support team here: dis.gd/billing and our agents will be happy to assist you.

Please note that if your refund is issued, you will lose any promotional value or discounts associated with the purchase.
