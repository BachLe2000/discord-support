Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Minecraft

NOTE: Minecraft Education is not eligible for this promotion. To complete the Minecraft Holiday Quest, the following Minecraft versions can be played: Java, Bedrock, and Windows.

Quest Name: Minecraft Holiday Quest

Offer Details: Play 15 minutes of Minecraft on PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S and receive a Minecraft Skull Cloak.

NOTE: The Minecraft Skull Cloak can only be redeemed through Minecraft: Bedrock.    

Who is Eligible: Available on all platforms where Minecraft is available. 

Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for this Quest or reward.

Availability Period: The Minecraft Holiday Quest can be accepted on Discord from December 6, 2024 to December 20, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward. Reward gift codes expire January 20, 2025.

Other Terms: 

Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

NOTE: To complete the Quest, you must play Minecraft on PC or console. If you decide to complete the Quest on PC, you will need to have the Discord desktop app running simultaneously.
If you decide to complete the Quest on console, the Quest tracking will begin as long as your Discord account is connected via console. For guidance on how to connect and complete the Minecraft Holiday Quest on console, you can refer to the following:
  • Discord and PlayStation® Network Connection FAQ
  • Discord and Xbox Connection FAQ

In order to successfully complete the Minecraft Holiday Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play Minecraft for a minimum duration of 15 minutes.

Redemption Instructions

1. After completing the Quest by playing Minecraft for 15 minutes, you'll receive a reward code in Quest Home (Discover > Quests). This code will also be sent to your Discord account's email address when you claim your reward.

You can participate in this Quest until December 20, 2024 (11:59 PM UTC) and must claim your reward by January 20, 2025.

2. To redeem your Minecraft Holiday Quest reward code, visit Minecraft’s code redemption website and log in to your account.

3. While there, scroll to find the module and paste your reward code to obtain your Minecraft Skull Cloak (redeemable only through Minecraft: Bedrock Edition).

Minecraft Skull Cloak Claim Window

On mobile, select the Quest bar. This will bring you to the Quests tab where you can select Claim Reward. 

   

Mobile: Minecraft Quest Bar and “Claim Reward” Button in Quest Home

If you dismiss the mobile Quest bar, you can still find the Quest in Quest Home (Settings > Quests) to claim your reward. For more information about Quests on mobile, see this article.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. If you are unable to access the Gift Inventory, you are not eligible for Quests.

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Gift Inventory or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
