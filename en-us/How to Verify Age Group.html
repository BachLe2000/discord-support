The age verification features described are an experiment and available only to users in the United Kingdom and Australia.

Why do I need to verify my age group?

Some Discord features or spaces are designed for certain age groups. We're experimenting with new ways to verify your age when you try to access them.

How do I verify my age group?

1. If you have not verified your age group and are included in the experiment, when you access an age-restricted space, you’ll see an age-restricted warning. If age verification is required, press the Verify Age button to begin.

Example of Age- Restricted Channel Warning and Verify Age Button

2. A We need to verify your age window will appear, offering two verification options:

  • Face Scan: Select Face Scan on the verification page. Choose your preferred webcam or camera, follow the on-screen instructions, and press Done when finished.
  • Scan ID: Select Scan ID on the verification page. Scan the displayed QR code with your device, follow the instructions to photograph your ID, and press Done.

Face Scan and Scan ID options in the Age Verification window

After verification, you'll receive system messages about the verification process and a Direct Message from Discord's official profile confirming your verified age group.

Example of direct message (DM) notification from Discord confirming your age verification status

If the verification fails, you can either try again or contact our Trust & Safety team to request a manual review.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data stored when I use Face Scan or ID verification?

A: The information you share to power the age verification method that you choose is only used for the one-time age verification process and is not stored by Discord or its vendor. For Face Scan, the solution our vendor uses operates on-device, which means there is no collection of any biometric information when you scan your face. For ID verification, the scan of your ID is deleted upon verification. 

Q: Do I need to verify my age multiple times to access age-restricted content?

A: No. Age verification is a one-time process. Once your age group is confirmed, you won't need to verify again when you attempt to access an age-restricted feature. However, if you believe your age group is incorrect, you may retry the age verification process again.

Q: What happens if my age group is verified incorrectly or it fails to verify.

A: You have two options:

  1. Retry verification through your chat with Discord system messages where you can select Retry to enter the verification flow again.
  2. You can retry by opting for a manual review here. 

Q: How long does verification take?

A: The verification process typically takes just a few minutes. You'll receive notifications about the status and a DM from Discord's official profile once completed.

Example of DM notification from Discord confirming your age verification status

Q: What happens if I’m banned after age verification?

A: Your account may be banned if your age is below the minimum requirement. If you believe the result is incorrect, you can submit an appeal through the underage appeals process here.
