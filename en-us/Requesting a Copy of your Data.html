Requesting Your Data

To request a copy of your personal data from your account's entire history, go to your User Settings.

Once you submit your request, Discord will begin collecting your data. Here are a few key details you should know:

  • The data request process can take up to 30 days to complete.
  • You must wait for your current request to finish before submitting a new one.
  • Discord will send a download link to the email address linked to your account when you made the request. 
  • Changing your email address later will not redirect the download link.
  • Your request will be canceled if you disable or delete your account before receiving the download link.
NOTE: Your Discord account must be verified with an email address before requesting your data. Having trouble accessing your account? Contact privacy@discord.com for help with your data request.

Desktop/Browser

1. Select the cogwheel [] in the lower-left corner. 

2. Select the Data & Privacy tab.

3. Under Request your data, select the Request Data button.

4. In the Submit Data Request window, select which data you'd like to receive. The data will be sent to your Discord account's email address.

Requesting your data in User Settings on Desktop

Mobile

1. Select your avatar in the lower-right corner.
2. Tap the cogwheel [] in the upper-right corner, then tap Data & Privacy.
3. Under Request your data, tap the Request all of my data button and confirm. Discord will send the data to your account's email address.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to receive my Discord data?    

A: The data request process can take up to 30 days to complete.

Q: Can I submit multiple data requests at once?    

A: No, you must wait for your current request to be completed before submitting a new one.

Q: What happens if I change my email address after submitting a request?

A: The download link will be sent to the email address that was linked to your account at the time of the request. Changing your email address later will not redirect the download link.

Q: What if I delete my account before receiving my data?    

A: Your data request will be canceled if you disable or delete your account before receiving the download link.

Q: Do I need a verified email address to request my data?

A: Yes, your Discord account must be verified with an email address before you can request your data.

Q: Who should I contact if I'm having trouble accessing my account?

A: If you're having trouble accessing your account, you can contact privacy@discord.com for assistance with your data request.    

Q: Can I choose which data to receive?

A: Yes, when submitting a data request on desktop/browser, you can select which specific data you'd like to receive. On mobile, you can only request all of your data.
