How do I claim my Free Month of Nitro?

Step 1: Go to the Nitro tab

On the desktop or browser app, navigate to your User Settings page and locate the Discord Nitro tab. Alternatively, you can press the Home button at the top left of your client and then select the Nitro tab.

Note: You cannot claim this promotion through the mobile (iOS and Android) apps. If you are eligible and would like to receive this promotion, please log into your account through the Discord desktop or browser app.

Step 2: Subscribe to Nitro Monthly or Yearly

Once you are at the Nitro page - simply click Subscribe and select either the monthly or yearly plan for Nitro.

On the next screen, enter your credit card information and other payment details.

Note: This promo will only allow users to receive 1 free month of Nitro. If you select the Yearly option - you will only receive 1 month of free Nitro - not 1 year.

Step 3: Receive Your Free Month

Upon successful completion of the payment, your account will be upgraded to Nitro!

Your free additional month of Nitro will automatically be applied after the end of your first month or year subscription.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't see the promotion details on my Nitro tab. Why?

A: If you don't see any promotion details on your Nitro tab on the desktop or browser (this promotion is not available on the mobile clients), it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria.

Q: If I subscribe to Nitro Yearly during this promotion, what will I receive?

A: If you subscribe to Nitro Yearly during this promotion, you will receive 1 month of Nitro for free at the end of your yearly subscription.

Q: I claimed and redeemed a different free trial for Discord Nitro already. Can I access this promo?

A: Unfortunately, if you are currently on a free trial, you are not eligible for this promotion. Have no fear, there will be more of these down the road! 

Q: If I have Nitro credit in my account, can I apply it to this promo?

A: If you have credit in your account from a previous Nitro subscription, this credit will be automatically applied to the promotion when you check out.

If you have credit in your account that is associated with a different Nitro subscription type (ex: Nitro Classic), it will not apply to this promotion.

Q: I see a notification saying "You're ineligible for this offer". Why?

A: If you encounter this notification, it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria outlined above.

Q: If I receive a Nitro gift and redeem it during the promotion period, will I get another free month?

A: This promotion is for new purchases only, so you will not get the free month of Nitro if you redeem a Nitro gift during this time.  

Q: If I upgrade my subscription from Nitro Basic or Nitro Classic to Nitro, can I receive this promotion?

A: Yes, this promotion applies for upgrades from Nitro Basic or Classic to Nitro

Q: I purchased a Nitro subscription during the promotion period, but I’d like to request a refund. What are the steps?

A: Before requesting a refund, please review our Refund Policy to ensure that your purchase is eligible for a refund.

If you’d like to proceed, please send in a ticket specifying that you’d like a refund for your Nitro purchase to our support team here: dis.gd/billing and our agents will be happy to assist you.

Please note that if your refund is issued, you will lose any promotional value or discounts, including this Black Friday promotion, associated with the purchase.

Q: Can I gift my free month to someone else?

A: No, this free month is yours to keep and will be automatically applied to your account. It can’t be gifted or transferred to another user.
