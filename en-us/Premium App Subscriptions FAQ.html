What are Premium App Subscriptions

Premium App Subscriptions are a monthly subscription to premium features of an app that any individual member or server admin in a server can purchase to get enhanced functionality and more commands for the app. Any Discord user can purchase a user subscription for an app and personally utilize it in any server the user and app are both part of, while a server admin can purchase a guild subscription for an app that can be used by server members across the entire server. At the discretion of the developer of the app, subscriptions can be divided up into tiers and each tier may have unique pricing and features.

Developers of the apps provide the premium subscriptions and features. They are responsible for creating the offerings and their descriptions, and they also provide the subscriptions themselves and any associated content or other features. Please read the tier descriptions carefully to make sure you understand what exactly you are buying.

Because these are a recurring subscription, we’ll charge you monthly until you cancel the subscription (which you can do any time as further described below).

If you are unhappy with your Premium App Subscription, try contacting the developer of the app directly. If that doesn’t resolve your issue, you can request a refund as described below. If you experience technical issues with Discord that prevent you from accessing your Premium App Subscription, reach out to us at dis.gd/billing and choose “Premium Apps” under Type of billing issue. If you experience technical issues with an app, please contact the developer of the app.

How to Purchase a Premium App Subscription?

There are 5 ways to upgrade to the premium version of an app:

  1. App Directory: Start by heading over to the App Directory page for the app that you’d like to subscribe to! You can get to the App Directory page through the server’s Server Settings > App Directory or through the App Directory webpage. Once you’re in the App Directory, search for the app you would like to upgrade, head to the app’s Premium tab, and you’ll see the Premium Feature offerings. Press on the Subscribe button to start the subscription process.
  2. App Profile: On the profile of the app, press the Upgrade button.
  3. Premium Feature command: When you try to run a premium command of an app and you don’t have a Premium App Subscription, you’ll see the Upgrade button in the response.
  4. Members List: If you’re a server admin and you’ve added the app to your server, you can navigate to your server’s members list and find the app in the list. Pressing on the app will reveal the Upgrade button.
  5. Integration Settings: If you are a server admin of the server, you can go to Server Settings and find the Integrations page with all the apps on your server. You’ll see an option to Upgrade each app that has Premium App subscription available.
  6.  

Once you start the upgrade process, a new window will then appear where you can review your Premium App Subscription benefits. Press the Continue button in order to proceed to the payment process. 

In the next window, you will need to input your payment details, and agree to our Terms of Service and Paid Service Terms. Once you have filled in the relevant information, press the Get Premium button to confirm your payment and subscription. 

If the payment was successful, you’ll see a window that confirms your subscription is now activated and you can begin using your Premium App Subscription benefits! 

 

How to Manage/View/Cancel Subscriptions

To view your Premium App subscription on the desktop app, head into the Subscriptions tab within your User Settings. Scroll down to the Premium App subscription section and then press on the Manage Subscriptions button. 

In the Manage Subscriptions tab, you’ll now be able to view your active Premium App subscriptions and manage, view, or cancel the individual subscriptions within this menu. 

Note: Premium Apps are currently available to use within servers. Expanded availability for Premium Apps across Discord may be in the future!
