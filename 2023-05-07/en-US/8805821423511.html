3 months of Xbox PC Game Pass with Discord Nitro - SEA Region

From October 26, 2022 to April 26, 2023, Discord Nitro users based in the Southeast Asia region will be able to claim 3 months of Xbox PC Game Pass!

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: 3 months of free Xbox PC Game pass for Discord Nitro Users

Who is Eligible:

  • To claim through Discord, you must be a current Discord Nitro subscriber.
  • To redeem in Xbox, this offer is only for new Xbox PC Game Pass subscribers only.
    • For more details, check out the terms here: https://www.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-game-pass#xgpterms

Subscription: Unless you cancel, you will be charged the then-current regular membership rate when the promotional period ends.

After the 3 month free trial period, your subscription will convert to a monthly recurring subscription, and Xbox will bill your payment method for $9.99/month (USD) until you cancel it. You can cancel at any time through your Xbox settings.

How do I claim the Xbox Promo in Discord? 

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the Xbox PC Game Pass code in the Gift Inventory from October 26, 2022 to April 26, 2023 (4:59 PM PT). 

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active subscriber to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro subscriber)!

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro subscriber, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by April 26, 2023 (4:59 PM PT). 
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim!

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Classic or a Nitro Basic subscriber, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion!

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2) Click on the See Code button for the Xbox Promotion

Once you press See Code on the Xbox PC Game Pass promotion in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 25 character code for this promotion! 

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before May 25, 2023 (4:59 PM PT)

How do I redeem Xbox PC Game Pass on my Xbox Account? 

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your Xbox account (or share this code with a friend) before May 25, 2023 (4:59 PM PT)! 

Eligibility for Redeeming the Xbox PC Game Pass Promotion

To redeem the 3 months Xbox PC Game Pass promotion, this offer is only for New Xbox Game Pass subscribers only. For more details, check out the terms here: https://www.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-game-pass#xgpterms

  • The promotion is available only for the Southeast Asia region (Malaysia, Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand, and Philippines). Please review Xbox terms for more details. If you have questions, please reach out to the Xbox support team.

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

Note:

- Once redeemed, the promotion cannot be transferred to another account.

Note:

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you're unable to claim this code on your own Xbox account, you'll be able to share this code with a friend to use instead!

To share the code, you can copy the 25-character code from the Gift Inventory (in your User Settings in the Discord app) and then share this code directly to a friend! 

Note: We recommend sending the link to your friend in a private direct message.
