What is Emoji Confetti?

Emoji Confetti is an experimental feature currently available to select desktop users.

Emoji Confetti adds eye-catching flair to your Discord messages. Pick an emoji and watch it burst as confetti on any message—yours or someone else's—to make it stand out with personality and pizzazz.

Example of Emoji Confetti

Why and When should I use Emoji Confetti?

Emoji Confetti turns ordinary messages into extraordinary ones, going beyond standard emoji reactions to create more fun moments and interactions in your conversations.

Here are moments where adding Emoji Confetti to a message can be a game-changer:

  • Celebrations: If someone posted a message of a gaming milestone or accomplishing a personal goal, shower that message with emoji confetti!
  • Special Announcements: Make important messages stand out in busy conversations or dedicated announcement channels.
  • Spreading Joy & Appreciation: Add confetti to messages to brighten someone’s day or show your appreciation.
  • Just for Fun: Sometimes a conversation needs that extra touch of magic - no special occasion required!

How do I apply Emoji Confetti to a message?

1. To apply Emoji Confetti to a message, select the Add Emoji Confetti icon [] when hovering over a posted message or in the lower-right corner of the text box while typing.

Add Emoji Confetti Icon While Hovering a Message or in Text Chat 

NOTE: As part of this experiment, start your Emoji Confetti fun with 2 free Emoji Confetti!

2. To add Emoji Confetti when typing a message, press the emoji icon [] in the Select an emoji to apply to your message banner located above the text box. Then, choose an emoji from the emoji picker. 

Selecting the Emoji Icon in the Banner Above the Text Box

For a posted message, pick an emoji using the Pick Emoji box. Then, choose an emoji from the emoji picker.

Selecting the Pick Emoji Box to and Choosing an Emoji from the Picker

3. Once you've sent your message or applied confetti to an existing message, you're all set! Hover over the emoji on a message with Emoji Confetti applied to cause an explosion of confetti!

Hovering over Messages with Emoji Confetti

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What platforms is Emoji Confetti available on?

A: Currently, Emoji Confetti can only be applied to messages on desktop devices.

Q: Is there a way to disable the Emoji Confetti explosion?

A: The confetti explosion is an opt-in effect that you can choose to trigger. You can disable the Emoji Confetti effect and reduce motion across the app by going to your User Settings > Accessibility > toggle Enable Reduced Motion. You can learn more about reduced motion here.

Q: What’s your refund policy for Emoji Confetti?

A: Emoji Confetti is non-refundable—we cannot provide refunds after purchase or use.

Q: Can I see who applied Emoji Confetti to a message?

A: Yes—simply hover your cursor over the CONFETTI badge on a message to see who applied it.

Example of CONFETTI Badge

Q: Can everyone in the server see Emoji Confetti on a message?
A: Yes, everyone in the server can see Emoji Confetti on a message and trigger the effect. However, Emoji Confetti that you trigger will only appear for you.

Q: Can I remove or change Emoji Confetti after applying it?
A: No, once you’ve applied Emoji Confetti to a message, it cannot be removed or modified.

Q: What happens to Emoji Confetti after the experiment ends?

A: As an experimental feature, Emoji Confetti on messages and any remaining in your inventory may expire in the future.
