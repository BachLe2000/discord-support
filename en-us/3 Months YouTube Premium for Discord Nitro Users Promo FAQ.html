Starting from January 21, 2025 (10:00AM PT) to April 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT), Discord Nitro members can claim 3 months of YouTube Premium. Read more below about the promotion below.

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: 3 months of YouTube Premium for Discord Nitro member

Who is Eligible: To claim through Discord, you must be a current Nitro member (unable to be redeemed if you have a Nitro trial membership

Requirements:

Users located in the United States, Canada, France, Germany, United Kingdom, Japan, and Korea can claim through Discord Gift Inventory from January 21, 2025 (10:00AM PT) to April 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT), and redeem in your YouTube account by May 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT).

Subscription:

After the 3 month free trial period, your YouTube Premium subscription will convert to a monthly or yearly recurring subscription, and YouTube will bill your payment method unless you cancel the subscription.

How do I claim the YouTube Premium in Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the YouTube Premium code in the Gift Inventory from January 21, 2025 (10:00AM PT) to April 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active member to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro member).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by April 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after April 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT).  

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting member, you'll need to become a Nitro member in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re a Discord Nitro member, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

For both the desktop/browser app and mobile app, you can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select Gift Inventory.

2) Click on the Claim button for the YouTube Premium Gift

Once you press Claim on the YouTube Premium Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before May 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT).

How do I redeem the YouTube Premium Trial on my YouTube Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your YouTube account (or share this code with a friend).

Eligibility for Redeeming the YouTube Premium Trial

To redeem the 3 months YouTube Premium trial code, this offer is only for eligible potential YouTube Premium subscribers. Learn more here.

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your YouTube account meets the promotion requirements, reach out to the YouTube support team to review your YouTube account further through here.

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you're unable to claim this code on your own YouTube account, you'll be able to share this code with a friend to use instead.

To share the code, you can copy the 12-character code from the Gift Inventory (in your User Settings in the Discord app) and then share this code to a friend in a Direct Message.

A: Once a code is claimed in the Gift Inventory in Discord, all YouTube Premium codes must be redeemed by May 15, 2025 (11:59PM PT).
