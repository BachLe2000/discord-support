As of March 15, 2024, Discord will no longer be supported on macOS 10.13 and macOS 10.14.

To continue receiving updates and support for your Discord client, you must upgrade to at least macOS 10.15. Check out our OS/system requirements for Discord here.

You can find more information about upgrading your macOS from the following pages:

  • Upgrade macOS on Mac
  • Upgrade to macOS 10.15 Catalina

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Will the Discord client I currently have installed continue to work after March 15, 2024?

A. Basic functionality may continue to work, however the functionality and stability is not guaranteed as the client will no longer receive updates.

Q. If I cannot upgrade my computer right now, can I use the browser version of Discord?

A. Yes, as long as your browser meets the minimum requirements found here, you can continue to use it.
