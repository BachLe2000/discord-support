We’re excited to announce that Discord is partnering with Samsung! From October 26, 2022 to March 31, 2023, Samsung customers in the UK and Ireland can claim 3 months of Discord Nitro through Samsung Boost. 

TL;DR

Offer Details: 3 months of free Discord Nitro through Samsung Boost

Who is Eligible to Redeem Nitro: Users who have not had an active Discord Nitro subscription on their accounts in the past 12 months are eligible.

This promo is only available to customers in the United Kingdom and Ireland who have bought the following Samsung products: Samsung Galaxy S21 Family Device (S21, S21+, S21 Ultra 5G) + Flip/Fold Family +Tabs7 Family, Flip4, Fold4.

Requirements: Claim the promotion link through Samsung Boost starting October 26, 2022 (12:00AM GMT) to March 31, 2023 (12:00AM GMT), and redeem in Discord account by April 30, 2023 (12:00AM GMT).

Subscription: After the 3 month free trial period, your Nitro subscription will convert to a monthly recurring subscription, and Discord will bill your payment method for US$9.99/month until you cancel it. You can cancel at any time in your Discord User Settings.

WHAT IS DISCORD NITRO?

With Nitro, you’ll get many awesome chat perks to enhance your Discord experience! Upgrade your emoji, enjoy bigger file uploads, stand out in your favorite Discords, and more! Check out some of these perks below! 

  • GIF Avatars
  • Custom Discord Tag
  • Global Custom emojis
  • Higher Video quality
  • Server Boosting
  • More!

HOW DO I CLAIM DISCORD NITRO ON MY SAMSUNG DEVICE?

From October 26, 2022 (12:00AM GMT) to March 31, 2023 (12:00AM GMT), go to the Samsung Boost browser page on your eligible Samsung device and choose the Discord promotion.

Once your eligibility is verified, you will be given a unique digital link then prompted to go to Discord to create a Discord account or log into your Discord account.

HOW DO I REDEEM NITRO ON MY DISCORD ACCOUNT? 

Once you have your promotion link and you create/log into your Discord account, you'll now need to officially redeem this gift on your Discord account (or share this Nitro with a friend)! 

Users who have not had an active Nitro subscription on their Discord accounts in the past 12 months are eligible to redeem this Nitro promotion.

NOTE:

- Before redeeming Nitro, make sure you are logged in your own Discord account.

- If you accidentally redeemed the Nitro promo code on an account that is not yours, we are unable to provide you with another promotion link.

Step 1 - Claiming the Promotion

  • You must first claim the promotion link following the steps listed in the section, “How Do I Claim Discord Nitro On My Samsung Device?”

Step 2 - Create your Discord account or Log into Discord

  •  If you're not already logged into Discord on your Samsung device, you will then be automatically redirected to either:

Register for a New Account

If you don't have an account, you can directly register a new Discord account

Login to your Account

If you already have a Discord account, you can login to your account using your email and password or using QR code login

Step 3 - Confirming the Correct Account and Payment Method

  • Once you have logged into your Discord account, you'll see a new message asking you to confirm that you are accepting the Nitro subscription on the correct Discord account.
    • You'll see a notice saying: "You are accepting this gift as _________" 
  • If your Discord account does not have your payment information, you'll be asked to fill out your payment details. (Note: You will not be charged until the free trial ends and the subscription renews)
  • When you've filled out all the necessary information and you're ready to redeem this promotion, press the "Accept Gift" button. 

Note: If you're logged into the incorrect Discord account, press the "Wrong account" button to login to the other account! 

Step 4 - Subscription Now Activated

  • After accepting the promo to your account, you'll now be automatically redirected to your Subscriptions tab in your User Settings to see your newly activated Nitro subscription on your account.

HOW DO I SHARE THIS NITRO PROMO LINK WITH A FRIEND?

If you're unable to redeem the Nitro subscription on your own Discord account, you'll be able to share the unique digital promotion link with a friend to use instead!

You can copy-paste the promotion link and send it directly to a friend in a direct message and then they can follow the steps above to redeem through the promotion link.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • PROMOTION DETAILS

Q: How long does this promotion run for?

A: This promotion runs from October 26, 2022 (12:00AM GMT) to March 31, 2023 (12:00AM GMT).

Q: How long does this Nitro promotion code last for?

A: All Discord Nitro promotional links must be redeemed in your Discord account by April 30, 2023 (12:00AM GMT).

Note: If a promo code has not been redeemed in your Discord account by April 30, 2023 (12:00AM GMT), the code will expire and you will no longer be able to redeem three months of Discord Nitro.

Q: Who can receive the Nitro promo and is this promotion available in my region?

A: This offer is only available to Samsung customers in the United Kingdom and Ireland who have bought the following Samsung devices: Samsung Galaxy S21 Family Device (S21, S21+, S21 Ultra 5G) + Flip/Fold Family +Tabs7 Family, Flip4, Fold4 devices.

Note: Only users who haven’t had a Nitro subscription on their Discord accounts in the past 12 months are able to redeem this Nitro trial. Existing subscribers of Discord Nitro aren't eligible, however, this promo link can be gifted to a friend! 

  • REDEEMING NITRO PROCESS QUESTIONS

Q: I was gifted a Nitro subscription previously, can I redeem this Nitro subscription to my Discord account?

A: Nope! Only users who haven’t had a Nitro subscription on their Discord accounts in the past 12 months are able to redeem this Nitro trial. However, this promotion link can be gifted to a friend! 

Additionally, if you have previously redeemed a 3-months of free Nitro promotion in the past 12 months through other events, you will also not be eligible to redeem the 3 months of free Nitro for this promotion.

Q: How do I share the promo link with a friend?

A: You can directly copy paste the Nitro promotion link from the Samsung Boost page and send it to a friend!

  • PAYMENT INFO AND SUBSCRIPTION QUESTIONS

Q: Why do I need to submit my payment info to redeem Nitro?

A: Because Nitro is a subscription plan, at the end of your free 3 month trial, we will start your first billing cycle so you can continue to enjoy Nitro on Discord.

However, you can cancel the Nitro subscription at any time before the end of the three months!

Q: Why do I see a hold on my account when this is supposed to be a free promotion?

A: Just to clarify, this Nitro promotion is free, however, there will be a temporary hold placed on the payment method upon activating the promotion. This is to help Discord confirm the payment method works as expected and to prevent the use of fraudulent payment methods. This charge will appear in your Discord transaction history, and it may appear as a hold on your payment method.

We immediately reverse the temporary hold, and you’ll see that reflected in your Discord transaction history. Your bank may take longer to remove the hold from your payment method. Note: Banks will usually remove the temporary hold from your account within a few business days.

If your Discord transaction history does not show the hold released after 5 business days, please let us know at https://dis.gd/contact, and our support team will be able to investigate this issue further.

  • POTENTIAL ERRORS AND OTHER ISSUES

Q: I see a "Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub" error when I try to apply my Nitro promotional link! 

If you're seeing an “Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub” error, that means that you've previously subscribed to Nitro on your Discord account in the past 12 months and you won't be able to redeem this 3 month perk! However, this promotion link can be gifted to a friend! 

If you have never subscribed to Nitro, please submit a ticket through our support system: https://dis.gd/contact and our support team will be able to investigate further. (Be sure to include your full Discord ID and email address tied to your Discord account)

Q: I see a "This gift has already been redeemed" error when I try to apply my Nitro promotion link!

A: If you are seeing a "This gift has been redeemed already" error, that means that the Nitro promotion link has already been used and cannot be used again. You may need to double check if you've redeemed this promo on another Discord account.

In particular, if you redeemed this promotion using your mobile device, double check the browser on your mobile device and see which Discord account you are currently logged into. 

Unfortunately, once the promotional link has been redeemed, the promo cannot be re-enabled, and the Nitro cannot be transferred from one account to another.

Q: I see a "Seems like something’s gone wrong" error when I try to apply my Nitro promo link! 

A: If you're seeing an “Seems like something’s gone wrong” error, that means that the link is invalid! If you've copy and pasted this promotional link, we recommend you double check the link you were given once more and make sure it exactly matches the link from the Samsung Boost page.

If you received this promo link from a friend, double check with your friend on the exact link to make sure that it matches the link given! Please note that our support team is unable to intervene if your friend has given an invalid link and we will not be able to provide a new promotion link.
