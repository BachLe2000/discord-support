With Nitro, you’ll get many awesome chat perks to enhance your Discord experience. Upload custom emojis and stickers, enjoy bigger file uploads, stand out in your favorite Discords with unique Server Profiles, and more. Check out some of these perks below.

  • Custom Discord Tag
  • Global Custom emojis
  • Higher Video quality
  • Server Boosting
  • More Nitro & Nitro Classic perks

What is Tower of Fantasy?

Tower of Fantasy is a shared open world MMORPG, set hundreds of years in the future on the distant planet of Aida. It's an anime-infused sci-fi adventure from developer Hotta Studio and publisher Level Infinite!

Tower of Fantasy is available on Mobile and PC starting on August 10th, 2022!

How do I claim the Tower of Fantasy Gift Pack in Discord?

As long as you're a $9.99 Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the Tower of Fantasy Gift Pack key in the Gift Inventory from August 11th, 2022 (2:00am GMT) to November 8th, 2022 (8:00pm GMT)*.

*Limited supply and subject to availability.

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

This promotion is only available in certain regions. If you receive an "Unknown Gift Code" error during the claim/redemption process, you are not eligible to redeem this promotion. 

In order to claim this key, you'll need to be an active subscriber to $9.99 Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro subscriber).

  • If you are an active $9.99 Discord Nitro subscriber, the key must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by November 8th, 2022 (8:00pm GMT).
    • Otherwise, the key will not be available to claim after November 8th, 2022 (8:00pm GMT).

Unfortunately, if you are a $4.99 Nitro Classic subscriber, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to $9.99 Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2) Click on the Claim button for the Tower of Fantasy Gift

Once you press Claim on the Tower of Fantasy Gift in the Gift Inventory, you'll then see a new pop-up with your key for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your key later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the key before November 8th, 2022 (8:00pm GMT).

How do I redeem the Gift Pack in my Tower of Fantasy Account?

Redemption of this promotion is in limited supply and subject to availability.

Once you have claimed the key in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this key on your Tower of Fantasy account.

Eligibility for Redeeming the Tower of Fantasy Gift Pack

You must have an active Tower of Fantasy account in order to redeem this gift pack!

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

Note: Make sure you are logged into the correct Tower of Fantasy account before officially redeeming this gift pack. Once redeemed, the gift pack cannot be transferred to another account.

In order to redeem the gift pack on your Tower of Fantasy account, press on Redeem to get your unique redemption key. Then, proceed to the Tower of Fantasy app or website.

Once you're on the Tower of Fantasy app or website., you'll then need to follow these steps below:

1) Sign into your Tower of Fantasy Account if you're not already signed-in, or create your Tower of Fantasy account. 

You can sign up or log in here: https://www.toweroffantasy-global.com/ 

2) Enter the 10-character key from your Gift Inventory in the "Rewards" section and then press "Claim".

And that's it! Once you redeem your key, you will receive all of the items included in your gift pack. Happy adventuring!

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion in the Tower of Fantasy app or website, reach out to the Tower of Fantasy support team to review your account further: support@toweroffantasy-global.com 

Can I share my key with a Friend?

You cannot share your Tower of Fantasy gift pack key with a friend, as it is against the terms for this promotion.

The CD Key can only be used by the individual who redeemed it. If you are found to have gifted or traded it, Tower of Fantasy will take action that includes but is not limited to removing the items and banning your account.
