WHAT IS THE DISCORD ADMINS SERVER?

This server allows Discord to chat with admins, moderators, content creators, and server owners who manage a server with Community Settings enabled. 

Here you can learn more about features, use best practices, and meet other members who want to improve their communities!

JOIN THE DISCORD ADMINS SERVER

What are the requirements to join this space?

Below are the requirements your server will have to meet. 

  • Have at least 200 members on your server
  • Your server must be at least 3 months old
  • Enable Community Settings on your server

If your server meets the above requirements and you have the Manage Server permission on that server, you will be able to join the Discord Admins server.

You, as an individual, can join as long as you have the "Manage Server" permission natively in Discord. If you would like other admins or moderators from your server to join the community, you will have to temporarily grant them the Manage Server permission in order to see the join button.

To join the Discord Admins server… 

  • Access Discord on the desktop/browser app 
  • Go to your server’s Server Settings
  • Select Overview in the Community section
  • Press on Join Discord's Admins Server

Note: The only way to join the Discord Admins Server is on the desktop/browser app. This is unavailable on the mobile app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What can I find in the Discord Admins server?

A: The Discord Admins server primarily focuses on engaging, managing, protecting, and growing thriving communities on Discord.

Here you can find chatter about best practices, safety, apps, monetization, and more. Members will also receive updates on new features or updates directly from Discord. We also use the community as a way to gather feedback on some features and ask members to provide us with feedback.

Q: Where can I find the rules for the server?

A: Current rules can be found in the #rules channel. Be sure to read them completely!

However, Discord reserves the right to act on misbehavior and violations not explicitly listed in our rules. At all times, use common sense and good judgment for anything you do on the server. Moderation actions by Staff or Moderators will ensure that we continue to promote healthy communities and assume that all Admins will be acting with positive intent.

Q: I'm in the server, but I can't see any channels! What should I do?

A: You must complete our Community Onboarding to access channels in the server. If you are still struggling with this, you can message our Modmail bot and ask a moderator for assistance.

Q: Manage Server is a pretty high permission to give out, why is it locked behind this?

A: We want to ensure that we are reaching server owners, admins and moderators with this community. As such, this will require us to ensure we are reaching an audience that has this permission or capability in order to create a welcoming and fun community for all.

Q: Is the server only in English?

A: A majority of the categories are in English, but not strictly limited to being an English language community. We have dedicated spaces for French and German users, with additional forum posts for other languages. We may expand this in the future to include more languages.

Q: I was banned or removed from Discord Admins, can I appeal?

A: Since the server works on a warning system before removals, we provide users with time and explanation on how to avoid removal from our server. If a user does hit the threshold for warnings, or is removed from the server, we will not offer appeals to rejoin the server.
