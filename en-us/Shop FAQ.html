Welcome to the Shop

From avatar decorations to profile effects, our new Shop has a variety of items you can buy and collect to customize your profile! Items purchased from the Shop are yours to keep and apply whenever you want. Now you can add your favorite styles to your profile for any occasion!  

Nitro members can enjoy early access to the Shop. Stay tuned as we slowly roll out this feature. To learn more about Nitro, check out this article.

What will I find in the Shop?

Once you enter the Shop, you will be able to preview and purchase available avatar decorations and profile effects! 

Selections available for purchase in the Shop will change periodically and may be available for a limited time. However, any purchased items are yours to keep and can be used at any time.

You can learn more about customizing your profile by heading over to our Custom Profiles article here. 

How do I find the Shop?

At this time, you can only access the Shop on the desktop app or your browser.

Head over to the Shop to start collecting avatar decorations and profile effects! 

1. Navigate to your User Settings > Profiles. 

2. Once there, you will see the banner to the Shop where you can select Go to Shop. You can also select Change Effect or Change Decoration and press the Shop icon.  

3. Once directed to the Shop, you can preview and buy avatar decorations and profile effects to add to your collection! 

Head over to the Avatar Decorations article and the Profile Effects article to find steps on how to apply your collection and customize your profile!
