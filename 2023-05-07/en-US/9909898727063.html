1 Month of Crunchyroll Mega Fan for Discord Nitro Users - Promo FAQ

Starting from November 2nd, 2022 (9:00AM PST) to January 2, 2023 (11:59PM PST), Discord Nitro subscribers can redeem a 1 month trial of Crunchyroll Mega Fan. Read more below about the promotion below.

TL;DR

Offer Details: 1 month trial of Crunchyroll Mega Fan for Discord Nitro subscribers

Who is Eligible:
To claim through Discord, you must be a current 9.99 Nitro subscriber.

To redeem in Crunchyroll, this offer is only for new, first-time Crunchyroll subscribers.

If you have subscribed to or previously activated a trial for any Crunchyroll Membership subscription type (Fan or Mega Fan), you are ineligible for this promotion.

This promotion is only available in certain regions. If you receive an "Unknown Gift Code" error during the claim/redemption process, you are not eligible to redeem this promotion. You can learn more at this Help Center article.

Requirements:

Claim through Discord Gift Inventory from November 2nd, 2022 (9:00AM PST) to December 2nd, 2022 (11:59PM PST), and redeem in your Crunchyroll account by January 2nd, 2023 (11:59PM PST).

Subscription:

After the 1 month free trial period, your Crunchyroll Mega Fan subscription will convert to a monthly or yearly recurring subscription, and Crunchyroll will bill your payment method unless you cancel the subscription.

How do I claim the Crunchyroll Mega Fan trial in Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the Crunchyroll Mega Fan code in the Gift Inventory from November 2nd, 2022 (9:00AM PST) to December 2nd, 2022 (11:59PM PST).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

This promotion is only available in certain regions. If you receive an "Unknown Gift Code" error during the claim/redemption process, you are not eligible to redeem this promotion. You can learn more at this Help Center article.

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active subscriber to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro subscriber).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro subscriber, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by December 2nd, 2022 (11:59PM PST).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after December 2nd, 2022 (11:59PM PST).

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic or Nitro Classic subscriber, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

You can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select the Gift Inventory tab.

2) Click on the Claim button for the Crunchyroll Mega Fan Gift

Once you press Claim on the Crunchyroll Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion. 

If you want to redeem the promotion now, you can then press the next confirmation button to be redirected to the Crunchyroll website on a new browser window to start the redemption process. 

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before December 2nd, 2022 (11:59PM PST).

How do I redeem the Crunchyroll Mega Fan Trial on my Crunchyroll Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your Crunchyroll account.

Eligibility for Redeeming the Crunchyroll Mega Fan Trial

To redeem the 1 month Crunchyroll Mega Fan trial code, this offer is only for new, first-time Crunchyroll Mega Fan subscribers.

If you have subscribed to or previously activated a trial for any Crunchyroll Membership subscription type (Fan or Mega Fan), you are ineligible for this promotion.

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your Crunchyroll account meets the promotion requirements, reach out to the Crunchyroll support team to review your Crunchyroll account further: https://help.crunchyroll.com/hc/en-us
