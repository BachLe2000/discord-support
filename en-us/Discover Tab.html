Discover Tab

The Discover tab is a central place for finding things to do on Discord. Here, users can discover servers, Quests, and much more. 

To navigate to the Discover tab, select the Discover icon [] at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app.

This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore the category pages.

Servers

Servers in Discord are virtual spaces where communities gather to chat, share content, and interact. You can learn more about Server Discovery here.

In the Discover Tab, you will find discoverable Community Servers along with their categories.

To take a closer look at these communities, select the Servers tab within the Discover tab.

Servers are sorted into relevant categories and order of popularity based on the server’s member count.

In Servers, you can browse various server categories like gaming, music, entertainment, and more by selecting from the category bar located at the top of the Servers page.

When you’re ready, press the server you would like to preview! 

Quests

Quests are a way for players to discover games and earn rewards for playing them on Discord. Whether you are playing or streaming your favorite game on Discord, you now have a chance to get rewards!
To explore available Discord Quests, select the Quest tab within the Discover list.

In Quests, you can view recent and current Discord Quests from the last 30 days. While here, press Accept Quest for active Quests to begin.

You can also see past Quests that you’ve completed and earned rewards for by selecting Claimed Quests within the category bar located at the top of the Quest page.

Start a Quest today and enjoy the rewards! You can learn more about Discord Quests here. 

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why was my server recently removed from Discover?

A: If your server was recently removed from the Discover tab, it may be due to a violation of Discord’s Discovery Guidelines. Please review the Discord Server Discovery Guidelines and ensure your server complies with them.
