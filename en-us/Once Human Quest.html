Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Once Human
Quest Name: Winter Survival Quest

Offer Details: Stream 15 minutes of Once Human and receive a Winter Survival Pack!

Availability Period: The Winter Survival Quest can be accepted on Discord from October 25, 2024 until October 31, 2024 (11:59PM UTC). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward. Reward gift codes expire December 31, 2024 (0:00 UTC). 

Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

To fulfill the Quest requirement, Once Human must be played on PC and streamed through the Discord desktop app with at least one friend actively watching the stream. 

In order to successfully complete the Winter Survival Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must live stream their Once Human gameplay to a friend via a Discord voice channel for a minimum duration of 15 minutes on the desktop app. 

1. Before you can begin the Quest, launch the game.

2. Then, head to your Discord desktop app and navigate to a direct message (DM), group chat, or a  server of your choice.

3. Once there, start a voice call or join a voice channel and select the Screen Share [] icon located in the voice status panel. Be sure to select the individual application for the game before you Go Live. 

Streaming to a Friend in a Discord Voice Channel

NOTE: You must select the game application to stream. Streaming your device’s screen will not progress your Quest forward. Once you stream, you can check your progress on the Quest bar or via Quest Home (Discovery > Quests) to ensure progress is being made on the Quest.  

4. When you’re ready to stream your gameplay, press Go Live. You can learn more about streaming your game here. 

Redemption Instructions

1. Once you have completed the Quest by streaming 15 minutes of Once Human, you will receive a reward code in Quest Home (Discovery > Quests). For detailed instructions on how to claim your reward code, please refer to our Discord Quests FAQ.

Once you select Claim Reward, the reward code will also be sent to the email address associated with your Discord account.

2. To redeem your code in-game, first complete the tutorial. 

3. Then, press F3 or ESC to open the event interface.

4. In the event interface, press X or select the Redeem Code button in the bottom right corner.

5. A pop-up window will appear. Enter your code and press REDEEM to claim your reward.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices. 

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows. 

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
