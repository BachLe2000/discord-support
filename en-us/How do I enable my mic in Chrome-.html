If you previously disabled Discord's access to your mic in Chrome, you'll need to enable the permissions in your Google Chrome settings in order to use it once again.

Follow these steps below to allow Discord access to your mic in the Chrome browser. 

Step 1: Head into your Chrome Settings

Select the ellipsis on the browser toolbar and choose Settings.

Then press on the Privacy and security section in the left-hand panel, and then select Site Settings.

Step 2: Remove the Blocked Access

Choose the Microphone option and find Discord in the Block section.

Press on the trashcan symbol to remove the block in access. 

Step 3: Join Voice and Press Allow to Enable Access

In the Discord browser client in Chrome, join a voice channel and press the Allow option when prompted by Chrome.

And that’s it! You should be all set to join in on the fun!
