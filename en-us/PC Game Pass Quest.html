Promotion Terms

Quest Video: PC Game Pass: We Got You
Quest Name: PC Player Quest

Offer Details: Watch the PC Game Pass: We Got You video in Discord to get a 14-day PC Game Pass trial! 

Availability Period: The PC Player Quest can be accepted on Discord desktop app or browser client from November 29, 2024 until December 11, 2024 (8:00AM PT). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.

Other Terms: The PC Game Pass trial is only available to new PC Game Pass members. 

Available in United States, Canada, Mexica, Brazil, United Kingdom, France, Germany, Australia, Japan, and Korea.  

A valid payment method is required, and there is a limit of one reward redemption per account. For details about the Microsoft Services Agreement, Game Pass terms, and system requirements, visit: xbox.com/subscriptionterms.
The reward codes are not “stackable” to previously redeemed trials. 

For information about Discord Quests and current active quests, visit the Discord Quest Help Center.

How to Complete the Quest

To fulfill the Quest requirement, the PC Game Pass: We Got You video must be watched through the Discord desktop app or browser client. 

In order to successfully complete the PC Player Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must watch the video. 

1. Navigate to Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or hover over the Quest bar to find the PC Player Quest.

PC Player Quest Bar and in Quest Home

2. Press Start Video Quest to launch the pop-up video player and watch the trailer. 

How to Start PC Player Quest via Quest Bar

NOTE: To play the video, you must use the Discord desktop app or browser client. The video will pause if you switch away from the Discord window. Don't worry if you can't finish the video in one sitting—your Quest progress is saved, so you can return to it later.

Redemption Instructions

Once you have finished watching the PC Game Pass: We Got You video, you'll be eligible to earn the 14-day PC Game Pass trial. You can participate in this Quest until December 11, 2024 (8:00AM PT).

You can claim your reward code through the Quest bar, video player, or Quest home:  

Through the Quest Bar or Video Player

To claim your PC Game Pass trial, simply press the Claim Reward button in either the Quest bar or the pop-up video player.  

 

“Claim Reward” Button: Quest Bar (Left) vs. Pop-up Video Player (Right)

Through the Quest Home

1. To claim your reward through Quest Home, head over to the Discover tab by selecting the Discover icon []  at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app. 

2. This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here.

3. While here, select Claim Reward for the PC Player Quest. 

“Claim Reward” Button: Quest Home

Once you select Claim Reward, the reward code will also be sent to the email address associated with your Discord account. To redeem your 14-day PC Game Pass trial code visit microsoft.com/redeem by December 31, 2024 (7:59 PM UTC). 

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.

Q: I'm having trouble redeeming this code to my Microsoft account.

A: The PC Game Pass reward code is only available to new PC Game Pass members. Additionally, the reward codes are not “stackable” to previously redeemed trials. Therefore, if you do not meet this eligibility criteria, the code will not work.

However, this code can be gifted to a friend!

On the other hand, if you have meet the eligibility criteria and the code still does not work, contact the Xbox Support team directly to review this further through this link: https://support.xbox.com/contact-us 

Be sure to include the full details of the error, and screenshots to the Xbox Support team!

Q: I’m having trouble with my Microsoft account!

A: Please contact the Xbox support team at: https://support.xbox.com/contact-us
