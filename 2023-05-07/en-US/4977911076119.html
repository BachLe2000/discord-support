WHAT IS LAND-IO?

In Land-io, players claim territory by moving around, making trails, and enclosing plots of land to gain them as their own.

The goal is to have the most land claimed when game time runs out, but watch out for other players! If someone runs into your trail, you'll lose all the land you've collected."

The player with the most land at the end of the 5 minutes wins the game.

HOW TO START A LAND-IO GAME?

Note: ⚠️ Before starting any Discord activity, please make sure to turn on Hardware Acceleration to improve game performance. Go to Discord User Settings > Advanced.

To start a Land-io game, follow these steps below!  

  • Join a voice channel
    • Once joined, you should see the Activity Launcher (it looks like a rocket ship) in the voice channel controls.
  • Click the Activity Launcher to open the “Activity Shelf"
  • Then select "Land-io”

  • Once you've authorized Land-io to access your account, you can begin playing.

Note: ⚠️ Public game may include players from outside of your servers. By default your username and avatar will be anonymized, assigning a character image and name to display in game.
- You can choose to turn off anonymization to show username and avatar to all players in the game (including those outside your server that are in the game.)
- Switching to Private game will only include the players from inside the same Server and voice channel that you are playing in.

Note: ⚠️ You can only use voice, video, or text chat with those inside your same server and voice channel, regardless of being in a public or private game.
