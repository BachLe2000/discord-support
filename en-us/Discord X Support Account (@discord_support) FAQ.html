Our X Posts

Our X posts help keep Discord users informed! If you follow our feed, you’ll get updates on:

  • Clarifying information for general Discord feature information
  • Known issues and bugs
  • Ongoing outages
  • Tips for improving your Discord experience

If you want to stay on top of the latest Discord news, we highly encourage you to give us a follow!

Issues @discord_support can assist with

To receive help, you can either DM us or tag us in a post. In your message, please provide a brief description of the problem you’re running into and attach a screenshot of any error messages you’re receiving!

Although this list is not exhaustive, here are a few examples of issues that @discord_support can assist with:

  • Basic troubleshooting
  • Connection issues/outages
  • Email verification issues
  • General feedback
  • Launch updates
  • Questions related to features or promotions

Make sure to also regularly check our feed for any updates regarding outages, launches, known issues, and updates.

Issues @discord_support cannot assist with

NOTE: Contacting us on X does not replace submitting a ticket through our support portal, nor does the Discord social team directly handle any previously submitted support tickets.

While we are able to assist with a number of issues, some inquiries still lie outside of the @discord_support account scope of support.

Examples of issues @discord_Support cannot assist with:

  • 2FA removal or lost account access
  • Developer support
  • Disabled and banned accounts
  • Hacked accounts
  • Phone verification issues
  • Refunds or subscription cancellations
  • Server ownership transfer
  • Terms of service related inquiries

How to Submit a Request to Discord Support

To contact the Discord support team, send a request through: http://dis.gd/contact

If you have already submitted a ticket to our support team through our support portal, please be patient as they are working as quickly as possible to get to your request! 

If you have any updates or questions regarding a support ticket, make sure to drop it directly into your pre-existing ticket thread to avoid any confusion with those that are handling your ticket.
