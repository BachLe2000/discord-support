How to Customize Lofi

1. To change the sound effects and their volumes, look for suggested soundscapes by tapping on the white circles around the scene to increase or decrease the volume of an individual soundscape. Otherwise, tap the slider icon in the media controls for a list of soundscapes to adjust. Changing the soundscapes and their volume will change it for all users in the same Lofi Activity.

2. To adjust the activity volume itself, use the global volume slider in the lower right corner. This will only impact the volume for the person adjusting their volume and not all other listeners in the same Activity.

3. Users can select different scenes using the Scenes button in the UI Menu. Use the left and right buttons to scroll through the options. Changing a scene will change it for all users in the same Lofi Activity.

4. Users can start, pause, or go to the next music track using the media controls at the bottom of the screen. View local time and date by tapping on the clock in the bottom-left corner. If the scene allows, scenes can adjust from day to night using the toggle also in the bottom-left corner. Changing, pausing, and starting the music track will do the same for all players in the same Lofi Activity.

5. Check out the Settings menu for options and other display options in the Lofi Activity. Anything changed in these settings will only be updated for the individual user and not shared by others.

Q: How do I view/delete my Activities on Discord data? What data is tracked?

A: You can view data for Discord-developed Activities like Lofi through the Discord Privacy and Safety settings. To enhance the gameplay experience, Discord Activities access and temporarily store the following user data once a user joins the Activity:

  • Discord ID
  • Avatar
  • Username

For Activities played in a single session, user data is stored so long as the Activity remains active, even if the user leaves the Activity. Once all users leave the Activity and it is determined the Activity has ended, the user data is deleted and no longer retained.

Note: Deleting your data will remove all of your progression across these Discord-developed activities.
