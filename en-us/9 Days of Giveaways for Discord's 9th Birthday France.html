What’s Happening on Discord’s 9th Birthday?

In honor of our 9th birthday, we're celebrating with 9 days of giveaways that make gaming with your friends on Discord way more fun. Starting on Monday, May 13th follow along on our social channels for a chance to win!

FR Discord’s 9th Birthday Celebration Dates

See full terms and conditions in the Master Sweepstakes Rules here: https://support.discord.com/hc/fr/articles/16000885744791. 

By entering, you agree to these Master Sweepstakes Terms.

Day 1: Custom Joystick Giveaway for Discord’s 9th Birthday

  • Rules: The French Discord’s 9th Birthday Sweepstakes is governed by the Discord Sweepstakes Terms, listed below (“Official Rules”). Discord will run one sweepstakes per platform as described below, with winner(s) per platform. Eligibility and Entry timing are consistent across each Sweepstakes.
  • Additional Eligibility: In addition to the additional eligibility requirements described in the Official Rules, entrants must be thirteen (13) years of age or older (with legal guardian approval if they are under the age of majority) and a legal resident of France.
  • Entry Term: 10:00 AM PST/7:00 PM CEST on Monday May 13, 2024 - 10:00 AM PST/7:00 PM CEST on Monday May 20, 2024
  • Twitter/X Sweepstakes:
    • How to Enter: Follow the Discord_fr Twitter/X account and like and retweet the official Discord_fr Giveaway post. 
    • Prize: One (1) Winner ($255)
      • One (1) custom joystick ($255)
    • Selection and Notification of Winners: Winners will be notified via DM through the account they used to enter the Giveaway and have one (1) week to respond in order to claim their prize.
