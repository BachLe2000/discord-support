Discord X GLSEN

This year we have partnered with GLSEN - an organization committed to impacting K-12 education on a national scale by affirming learning environments for LGBTQ+ youth. But as well as activating supportive educators, we believe in centering and uplifting student-led movements, which have powered initiatives like the Day of Silence, Ally Week, and more.

This partnership is part of a longer-lasting commitment with the organization. We understand that social justice and a focus on diversity and inclusion should not end once the celebration stops.

June is a great kickoff month, but supporting the community and LGBTQI+ initiatives should be a focus year-round.

So what's happening?

Join Discord for this year's Pride celebrations as we partner with GLSEN and spread some love in the form of super cute pins and stickers that are already up and running on our Tiltify page!

All proceeds from this Tiltify campaign will go directly to GLSEN! Our goal this year is to raise over 100K for GLSEN through our Tiltify campaign and we are really excited to bring some joy, delight, and color to everyone supporting this year.  

Click on this link here to head over to the Tiltify campaign and help support the LGBTQI+ community!

Donation Structure

This year we have decided to add a little more fun and excitement to our campaign by introducing blind bags into the mix.

We hope that this creates excitement and social sharing beyond the donation activation. We encourage everyone to share, collect and trade stickers and pins from this campaign.

$25 - Pride Pin Blind Bag

Like opening your summer birthday presents, relive the excitement and mystery of “what’s in the box??” with one of our silver enamel Pride Pin Blind Bags.

We’ve made an equal amount of each pin, so you’ll always have a ¼ chance to receive one of our four-pin designs. (Blind bag contains one pin and 6 stickers. All pins are silver cast with soft enamel, and each sticker is die-cut vinyl)

$30 - Pride 2021 Complete Sticker Set 

Show off your pride throughout the year, on anything you’d like, with this set of super-cute, super-amazing stickers.

This set includes every sticker you may encounter in Pride Pin Blind Bag bundles. (All stickers vary in size, and are die-cut vinyl)

$100 - Glitter Gold Pins & Stickers Set

Shine bright with this special gold enamel and glitter resin pin set, an exclusive upgrade for those looking to collect ‘em all. Also includes our full Pride 2021 sticker lineup. 

We have upgraded these pins with gold enamel, and some extra spicy glitter enamel for all the super collectors out there. (All pins are gold cast with soft enamel, and each sticker is die-cut vinyl)

Eligibility for Rewards

Selecting and making payment for any donation while the campaign is active from June 1st June 30th will make you eligible for selected rewards through the campaign portal. 

Please be sure to fill out the following information on Tiltify:

  • Full Name
  • Street Number and Name
  • City
  • State/Province
  • Postal/Zip Code
  • Country
  • Email
  • Phone Number

The above information will only be used to fulfill your incentive reward. (Email and phone number will be used to supply you tracking information once we ship your reward.)

NOTE: 
- Please be sure that you also select your rewards as you make your way through the Tiltify experience
- We can't send you your goodies unless you select a reward :) :)

Shipping and Fulfillment

As we move through the month of June, we will ship out rewards every Monday for the following weeks donations.

Please keep an eye out on your inbox (and spam folder) for tracking information to follow along. 

Once the campaign closes, we will continue to fulfill donation orders, and plan to complete processing of all orders no later than July 31st (one month after the campaign closes). 

Please contact https://dis.gd/contact before July 31st with any issues, and we will do our best to help solve them. Please understand that Discord will not be responsible for any damaged or lost shipments.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Will these rewards be shipped internationally as well?
    • A: Yup- these rewards also ship internationally too! 
  • Q: Why haven't I received a redemption link to provide my shipping info, when I've made a donation and I'm eligible for a reward! 
    • A: All people eligible for the reward will be emailed a redemption link to provide shipping info at a later date. Sadly we don't have an exact ETA though!
  • Q: What are the pins and stickers made of?
    • A: Each sticker is die-cut vinyl, and ranges from 1.2” to 3” in size. The pin sets are cast in silver enamel (25$) or gold enamel (100$) and are filled with soft enamel. We have added some fancy fun glitter enamel to the gold set to help everyone feel their glitter realness.
  • Q: Can I choose what pin I receive with the 25$ blind bag donation?
    • A: No. Each 25$ donation has an equally random chance to receive each of the silver enamel and sticker sets. 
  • Q: What do I do if I made a donation and haven't received my reward or it arrives broken or damaged?
    • A: Please contact https://dis.gd/contact no later than July 31st and we will do our best to help sort out any damaged items, or missing shipments.
      • If you're contacting us after July 31st, please understand that we may not be able to help resolve your issue.
    • Make sure to include your original email, the confirmation number, and a description of the issue so we can best help with the solution :)
