Promotion Terms

Quest Video: Season 2: Void Chaser Main Trailer
Quest Name: The First Descendant Quest

Offer Details: Watch the Season 2: Void Chaser Main Trailer in Discord and unlock a Discord Collection Bundle!  

Availability Period: The First Descendant Quest can be accepted on Discord desktop app or browser client from December 6, 2024 until December 13, 2024 (6:00PM UTC). 

Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.

Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center. 

How to Complete the Quest

To fulfill the Quest requirement, the Season 2: Void Chaser Main Trailer must be watched through the Discord desktop app or browser client. 

In order to successfully complete The First Descendant Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must watch the trailer. 

1. Navigate to Quest Home (Discover [] > Quests) or hover over the Quest bar to find The First Descendant Quest.

The First Descendant Quest Bar and in Quest Home

2. Press Start Video Quest to launch the pop-up video player and watch the trailer. 

How to Start The First Descendant Quest via Quest Bar

NOTE: To play the video, you must use the Discord desktop app or browser client. The video will pause if you switch away from the Discord window. Don't worry if you can't finish the trailer in one sitting—your Quest progress is saved, so you can return to it later.

Redemption Instructions

Once you have finished watching the Season 2: Void Chaser Main Trailer, you'll be eligible to earn the Discord Collection Bundle. You can participate in this Quest until December 13, 2024 (6:00PM UTC) and claim your reward by December 31, 2024.

You claim your coupon code through the Quest bar, video player, or Quest Home:

Through the Quest Bar or Video Player

To claim your Discord Collection Bundle coupon code, simply press the Claim Reward button in either the Quest bar or the pop-up video player.  

  

“Claim Reward” Button: Quest Bar (Left) vs. Pop-up Video Player (Right)

Through the Quest Home

1. To claim your bundle through Quest Home, head over to the Discover tab by selecting the Discover icon []  at the bottom of the server list on the left side of the app. 

2. This will direct you to the Discover page where you can explore available Discord Quests, by selecting the Quest tab within the Discover list. You can learn more about Discover here.

3. While here, select Claim Reward for The First Descendant Quest. 

“Claim Reward” Button: Quest Home

Once you select Claim Reward, the reward code will also be sent to the email address associated with your Discord account. To receive the rewards, follow these steps in The First Descendant game.
To redeem in-game PC:
1. Access the game > Options > Account > Enter in the Enter Coupon option.
2. Enter the coupon code and press Confirm.
3. Access the mailbox in Albion to receive the item.
To redeem on the Coupons page PC & Consoles:
1. Enter The First Descendant’s Coupon page.
2. Enter your member code and coupon number to redeem the item.
3. Access the mailbox in Albion to receive the item after starting the game. The member code can be copied from the Game Menu > Options > Account > Member Code.
*The rewards will be available until December 31st at 23:59 (PT).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account. 

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.
Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions. 

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests. 

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests. 

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discover > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
