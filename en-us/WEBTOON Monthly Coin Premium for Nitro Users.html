What is WEBTOON Coin Premium?

WEBTOON is a global storytelling platform where a vibrant global community of creators and users discover, create and share incredible stories. WEBTOON’S Monthly Coin Premium allows users to unlock exclusive pricing and enjoy episodes for less. 

How do I claim the WEBTOON’s Monthly Coin Premium in Discord?

As long as you're a Discord Nitro member, you'll be able to claim the WEBTOON’s Monthly Coin Premium code in the Gift Inventory from December 5, 2024 (11:00AM PST) to December 25, 2024 (11:59PM PST).

Eligibility for the Claiming this Promotion in Discord

In order to claim this code, you'll need to be an active member to Discord Nitro, (and you can be either a monthly or yearly Nitro member).

  • If you are an active Discord Nitro member, the code must be claimed in the Gift Inventory by December 25, 2024 (11:59PM PST).
    • Otherwise, the code will not be available to claim after December 25, 2024 (11:59PM PST).

Unfortunately, if you are a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting member, you'll need to subscribe to Nitro in order to claim this promotion.

Step By Step Claim Instructions 

1) Log into Your Discord account and head to the Gift Inventory tab. 

If you’re subscribed to Discord Nitro, start by logging into your Discord account. 

You may notice a notification bar at the top of your browser/desktop app, and you can press the "Take me there" button in the notification bar to be redirected to your Gift Inventory. 

For both the desktop/browser app and mobile app, you can also directly head to your User Settings, and then select Gift Inventory.

2) Click on the Claim button for the WEBTOON Monthly Coin Premium Gift

Once you press Claim on the WEBTOON Monthly Coin Premium Gift in the Gift Inventory and you'll then see a new pop-up with your 12 character code for this promotion.

If you want to redeem your code later, you can press the Maybe Later button, and then redeem the code before December 25, 2024 (11:59PM PST).

How do I redeem the WEBTOON Monthly Coin Premium Trial on my WEBTOON Account?

Once you have claimed the code in the Gift Inventory, you'll now need to officially redeem this code on your WEBTOON account (or share the link with a friend).

Eligibility for Redeeming the WEBTOON Trial

You will need a WEBTOON account without an active Monthly Coin Premium to be able to redeem the trial.

Note: If you're running into any issues when attempting to redeem this promotion, and you believe that your WEBTOON account meets the promotion requirements, you can reach out to WEBTOON’s support team here or to their support email: webtoonsupport@webtoon.com

How do I share my Code with a Friend?

If you're unable to claim this code on your own WEBTOON account, you'll be able to share this code with a friend to use instead.

To share the code, you can copy the provided link from your Gift Inventory (in your User Settings in the Discord app) which you should receive after tapping on Redeem. Then you can share the link to a friend in a Direct Message.
