What is a Bug?

Bugs are unintended behavior of the Discord app that diminish a user's experience. Think of the app crashing unexpectedly, or the "Add Friends to DM" button not letting you add your friends into a Group DM when you haven't reached the limit!

How can I help?

When issues pop up that negatively impact Discord users, we want to be able to understand and fix it as soon as possible. Any user can help us by reporting the bugs! Bug reports should be submitted with a ticket to the Bug form. Be sure to fill out each category, as each is important. The most helpful bug reports include:

  • A concise description of the bug, generally one sentence
  • Simple step by step direction on how to make the bug occur
  • Explanation of what you believe the behavior should be
  • What is actually happening
  • What version of Discord this occurs on
  • What device the bug occurs on

There are some bug reports that are not currently supported. We ask that you check out this Unsupported Reports article before submitting a bug report, to familiarize yourself with what topics are unsupported.

Additionally, if you're experiencing a bug related to Accessibility aka (A11Y) these issues can also be submitted via our Bug Form.

What is Discord Testers?

Discord Testers, or DTesters, is an invite only official Discord server for adept volunteer Bug Testers to help identify, reproduce and provide high quality bug reports to our engineering team. Periodically, new testers will be added and more information will be provided here as these opportunities arise.

Discord Tester FAQ

How can I report bugs if I am not a part of the Discord Testers server?

Bug reports should be submitted with a ticket to the Bug form. Be sure to fill out each category, as each is important.

Are members of the Discord Testers server required to have previous experience?

Members of the Discord Testers server are expected to have submitted quality bug reports prior to joining the server.

Are DTesters required to sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA)? Why?

DTesters may be asked to test features that have not yet been released. Due to the sensitive nature, an NDA is required to be signed. If an invitation is extended to you, you are welcome to review the details of the NDA so you can decide whether to sign or not.

What is the time and submission requirement for a Discord Tester?

DTesters are volunteers, and as such, we want to encourage them to find balance between DTesters activities and personal lives. There is currently no minimum activity requirement, but complete inactivity for a long period of time may result in removal.

What is the minimum age requirement for DTesters?

Discord users must be 13 years of age. If a Discord Tester is between 13 and 18 years of age, a parent or guardian will need to sign the NDA as well as the tester.
