Partnership Nitro Perks

As a gesture of love to our Discord Partners, we've given anyone with a Discord Partner badge all of Nitro's perks as well! These include:

  • Usage of an animated GIF avatar
  • Ability to use custom emotes across servers and DMs
  • Increased file upload cap from 8mb to 100mb
  • Access to animated emojis
  • Super-charged screen share up to 1080p 
  • Choose any available discriminator for your username
  • Two free Server Boosts to buff the community of your choosing
  • Access to Activities on Discord
