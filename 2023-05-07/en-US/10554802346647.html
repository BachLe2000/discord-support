6. Selection and Notification of Winner: On or before Monday, December 12th, 2022, a panel of judges selected at Discord’s discretion and based in the U.S. will score each Submission based on the following criteria (“Judging Criteria”):

Judging Criteria  

1)     Accuracy (⅓);

2)     Expression and posing  (⅓); and

3)     Set design and background (⅓).

The five (5) winners (three (3) winners for Most Creative, one (1) winner for Best Traditional, and one (1) winner for Best Digital) will be determined based on an assessment of each Submission against the Judging Criteria by a panel of three (3) Discord employees on Discord’s Community team and Art School team.  In the unlikely event of a tie, a member of Discord’s Brand/Experiential team will cast the deciding vote on the winning Submission.  The selection of the winners is at the sole discretion of the judges and the judges’ decision is final.

Each potential winner will be notified via Discord ID on or about Monday, December 12th, 2022. Only one alternate winner will be selected for the prize.
