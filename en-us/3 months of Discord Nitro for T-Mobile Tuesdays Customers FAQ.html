Starting from December 12, 2023 (5:00am EST) to December 18, 2023 (11:59am EST), T-Mobile and Metro by T-Mobile customers can claim 3 months of Discord Nitro in the T-Mobile Tuesdays app. Learn below about how you can redeem Discord Nitro.

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: 3 months of Discord Nitro for T-Mobile Tuesdays customers

Who is Eligible:

To redeem in Discord, you must be a first time Nitro member. 

Users who have never had an active Nitro membership on their Discord accounts are eligible to redeem this Nitro promotion.

Users who have had a Discord Nitro membership at any point on their accounts and have had a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic membership in the past 12 months are ineligible to redeem this promotion.

If a user has had a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting membership active on their account and they’ve never had a Nitro membership, redeeming this promotion will override that membership.

We will not be able to refund any leftover membership time from those, and the user will be forfeiting the remainder of the existing membership when activating the 3 month Nitro trial.

Users who have already claimed a gift from this promotion are not eligible to claim further gifts from this promotion.

Requirements:

Claim through the T-Mobile Tuesdays mobile app from December 12, 2023 (5:00am EST) to December 18, 2023 (11:59pm EST) and redeem to Discord account by January 11, 2024 (11:59pm EST).

Membership:

After the 3 month trial period, your Nitro membership will convert to a monthly recurring membership, and Discord will bill your payment method for per month until you cancel it. You can cancel at any time in your Discord User Settings.

How do I redeem the Nitro Trial on my Discord Account? 

Once you have claimed the offer within the T-Mobile Tuesdays Mobile app, you'll now need to officially redeem this promo link on your Discord account!

Eligibility for Redeeming the Nitro Trial

To redeem this promotion link to your Discord account, you must be a first time Nitro member. 

Users who have never had an active Nitro membership on their Discord accounts are eligible to redeem this Nitro promotion.

Users who have had a Discord Nitro membership at any point on their accounts and have had a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic membership in the past 12 months are ineligible to redeem this promotion.

If a user has had a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting membership active on their account and they’ve never had a Nitro membership, redeeming this promotion will override that membership.

Users who have already redeemed the T-Mobile Tuesdays offer cannot redeem it again.

Step by Step Redeem Instructions

If you’re redeeming this offer on an iOS device, you will subscribe to Discord Nitro through the Apple App Store. If you are redeeming this offer on an Android device, you can follow the steps below!

If you're eligible and ready to redeem this Nitro subscription on your own Discord account, you can follow these steps below to redeem the promo!

NOTE:
- Before redeeming Nitro, make sure you are logged in your own Discord account.
- If you accidentally redeemed the Nitro promo link on an account that is not yours, we are unable to provide you with another promo link.

Step 1 - Logging into Discord

  •  If you're not already logged into Discord in your browser, you will then be automatically redirected to either:

Register for a New Account

If you don't have an account, you can directly register a new Discord account

Login to your Account

If you already have a Discord account, you can login to your account using your email and password or using QR code login

Step 2 - Claiming your promotion gift

  • Once you have logged in to your account, you will see a message asking you to claim your promotion gift. 
  • The account you are currently logged in as will receive this gift. The gift cannot be switched to a different user, so be sure you are on the account you want to claim the gift for. If you want to change to a different account, press on the Switch Accounts.
  • Press on Next when you’re ready to redeem your gift on your chosen account.

Note:

- If you're logged into the incorrect Discord account, press the "Wrong account" button to login to the other account! 

 Step 5 - Redeeming your promotion gift

  • Review the details of the promotion then press on Next. Please note: if you switch to a different account at this step, you will not be able to claim and redeem the promotion on the account you switched to.

 

  • Make sure your payment information is accurate. If your Discord account does not have your payment information, please fill out your payment details. You will not be charged until the trial ends and the membership renews.
  • When you've filled out all the necessary information and you're ready to redeem this promotion, press the confirmation button.

Step 3 - Membership Now Activated

After accepting the promo to your account, you'll now be automatically redirected to your Subscriptions tab in your User Settings to see your newly activated Nitro membership on your account.

What if I am an Active Nitro Basic or Server Boosting Subscriber? 

Just to clarify - if you have had a Discord Nitro membership at any point on your account and have had a Nitro Classic or Nitro Basic membership in the past 12 months, you are ineligible to redeem this promotion.

So long as you have had a Nitro Basic, Nitro Classic, or Server Boosting membership active on your account and you’ve never had a Nitro membership, you will be able to redeem this promotion and replace it with the 3 months Nitro membership.

HOWEVER, please note: By choosing to activate this 3 month trial, you will lose any features associated with your current memberships, and **you will not receive a refund for any remaining value on your current membership**.

At the end of the promotion period, you'll be converted to a paying Nitro membership per month unless you cancel your membership.

Payment Info and Subscription Questions

Q: Why do I need to submit my payment info to redeem Nitro?

A: Because Nitro is a membership plan, at the end of your 3 month trial, we will start your first billing cycle so you can continue to enjoy Nitro on Discord.

However, you can cancel the Nitro membership at any time before the end of the 3 months.

Q: Why do I see a temporary hold/charge on my account when this is supposed to be a free promotion?

A: Just to clarify, this Nitro promotion is free, however, there will be a temporary hold placed on the payment method upon activating the promotion. This is to help Discord confirm the payment method works as expected and to prevent the use of fraudulent payment methods. This charge will appear in your Discord transaction history, and it may appear as a hold on your payment method.

We immediately reverse the temporary hold, and you’ll see that reflected in your Discord transaction history. Your bank may take longer to remove the hold from your payment method.

Note: Banks will usually remove the temporary hold from your account within a few business days.

If your Discord transaction history does not show the hold released after 5 business days, please let us know at https://dis.gd/contact, and our support team will be able to investigate this issue further.

Potential Errors and Other Issues

Q: I see a "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo.

A: If you're seeing an "Oh no! It looks like you have Nitro already" error, that means that the Discord account you're trying to redeem this promotion on currently has a Nitro membership so it is ineligible for this Nitro promotion.

Please make sure you're logged into the account that you want to redeem this promotion on.

Q: I see a "Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub!" error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo.

A: If you're seeing an “Sorry, looks like you’ve already had a Nitro sub” error, that means that you've previously subscribed to Nitro on your Discord account and you won't be able to redeem this 3 month perk.

If you have never been Nitro member, please submit a ticket through our support system: https://dis.gd/contact and our support team will be able to investigate further.

Be sure to include your full Discord ID and email address tied to your Discord account.

Q: I see a "This gift has already been claimed" error when I try to claim my Nitro promo.

A: If you are seeing a "This gift has been claimed already" error, that means that the Nitro promo has already been used and cannot be used again. You may need to double check if you've claimed this promo on another Discord account.

Unfortunately, once the promo has been claimed, the promo cannot be re-enabled, and the Nitro cannot be transferred from one account to another.

Q: I see a "Wait a Second...Looks like this code didn't work." error when I try to redeem my Nitro promo.

A: If you're seeing an “Wait a Second...Looks like this code didn't work.” error, that means that the promo is invalid.

Please note that our support team is unable to intervene if you are given an invalid promo and we will not be able to provide a new promo.

Q: I see a “Looks like something went wrong” error when I click on “Next”.

A: There are several reasons why you may be seeing this error:

  • You are ineligible for this promotion
  • The promotion is invalid
  • The link is invalid

Please note that the Discord support team is not able to assist if you run into this error when attempting to redeem. See the list above for possible reasons why your account may be ineligible or otherwise unable to redeem this promotion.

Q: I see a "Sorry, This Payment Source Cannot Be Used for Redemption" error when trying to redeem my promo!

A: If you're seeing a "Sorry, This Payment Source Cannot Be Used for Redemption" error, that means that the payment method you've selected to cover the temporary $1 hold cannot be used for that purpose. 

Please keep in mind that prepaid payment methods, such as Gcash, cannot be used to cover temporary holds due to the nature of how prepaid payment methods function.

For more information on temporary holds, please check out this article!

Q: I see a “Looks like your email isn’t verified. Make sure to verify your email before claiming this gift.”

A: If you are seeing a “Looks like your email isn’t verified, make sure to verify your email before claiming this gift” error, you are trying to redeem this Nitro promotion on a Discord account with an email that hasn’t been verified yet.

You can check out this support article on how to resend the email verification for your Discord account. If you need help setting up your Discord account, you can read our Getting Started article.
