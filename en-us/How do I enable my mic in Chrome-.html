If you previously disallowed Discord access to your mic in Chrome, you will need to enable it in your Google Chrome settings in order to use it once again.

Follow these steps to allow Discord access to your mic in the Chrome browser: 

1. Select the ellipsis on the browser toolbar and choose Settings. Press on the Privacy and security section in the left-hand panel, then select Site Settings.

2. Choose the Microphone option and find Discord in the Block section and select the trashcan to delete it.

3. In the Discord browser client in Chrome, join a voice channel and press Allow when prompted by Chrome.

And that’s it! You should be all set to join in on the fun! 

For more information, check out Chrome's official support page here: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2693767
