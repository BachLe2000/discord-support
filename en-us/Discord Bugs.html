What is a Bug?

Bugs are unintended behavior of the Discord app that diminish a user's experience. Think of the app crashing unexpectedly, or the "Add Friends to DM" button not letting you add your friends into a Group DM when you haven't reached the limit!

How do I Report a Bug?

When issues pop up that negatively impact Discord users, we want to be able to understand and fix it as soon as possible. Any user can help us by reporting the bugs! Bug reports should be submitted with a ticket to the Bug Reporting form. Be sure to fill out each category, as each is important. The most helpful bug reports include:

  • A concise description of the bug, generally one sentenceÂ Â 
  • Simple step by step direction on how to make the bug occur
  • Explanation of what you believe the behavior should be
  • What is actually happening
  • What version of Discord this occurs on
  • What device the bug occurs on

There are some bug reports that are not currently supported.Â  We ask that you check out this Unsupported Reports article before submitting a bug report, to familiarize yourself with what topics are unsupported.

Additionally, if you're experiencing a bug related to Accessibility aka (A11Y) these issues can also be submitted via our Bug Reporting form.

What is Discord Testers?

Discord Testers (also known as DTesters) is an official, invite only Discord server for adept volunteer bug testers. Our goal is to identify, reproduce, and provide high-quality bug reports. While there is no minimum activity expectation, inactive members may be removed. We intentionally keep our population small, but we periodically add new members.

We're thrilled to announce that we're getting ready to welcome a few new members to the Discord Testers server. If you're interested, keep reading!

Currently we are considering individuals who:

  • Are interested in submitting quality bug reports
  • Are 18 years or older
  • Have strong written English communication skills
  • Have a collaborative spirit
  • Are willing and able to sign a legally binding non-disclosure agreement
  • Demonstrate strong proficiency in the Consideration Quiz

Only a small percentage of applicants will have the opportunity to join. New participants will be selected based on their performance on the quiz, as well as the needs of the server. We will carefully consider each applicant, so the selection process may take some time. We appreciate your patience!
