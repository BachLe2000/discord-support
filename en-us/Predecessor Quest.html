Promotion Terms

Quest Game: Predecessor
Quest Name: Predecessor Skin Quest
Offer Details: Play 15 minutes of Predecessor to receive a Lumin Rampage Skin gift code. Reward gift codes expire October 3, 2024.
Who is Eligible: Available to players on PC where both Discord and Predecessor are available.
Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions.
Availability Period: The Predecessor Skin Quest can be accepted on Discord from August 27, 2024 until September 3, 2024 (11:59PM UTC).
Quest requirements must be completed during this period to receive the reward.
Other Terms: Limit of one reward redemption per account.
For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

How to Complete the Quest

In order to successfully complete the Predecessor Skin Quest, players who meet the eligibility criteria must play Predecessor on PC.

Redemption Instructions

Option 1
1. Once you have completed the Quest by playing 15 minutes of Predecessor, you will receive a gift code in Quest Home (Discovery > Quests). For detailed instructions on how to redeem your gift code, please refer to our Discord Quests FAQ.

2. Then, head over to the Predecessor in-game store by selecting STORE.
3. Navigate to the REDEEM subtab and enter the redemption gift code to receive the Lumin Rampage Skin.

Note: If the Quest is not completed by September 3, 2024, the Quest will no longer be available within Quest Home (Discovery > Quests).

Option 2
You can also claim your Lumin Rampage Skin gift code by pressing the Claim Reward button within the Quest bar.

Once you select Claim Reward, the gift code will also be sent to the email address associated with your Discord account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on how many Quests I can redeem on my Discord account?

A: Eligible users can redeem one of each of the available Quest campaigns on their Discord account.

For more information on Discord Quests and to view the currently active quests, visit our Discord Quest Help Center.

Q: Is this feature/promotion available in my region?

A: Some Quests will not be available in all regions based on terms and conditions. If you are in an ineligible region for a Quest, you will not be able to complete it.

Additionally, Discord’s premium services are restricted in some regions.

Q: Why am I unable to see Quests on the Discord mobile app?

A: This feature is only available on the desktop and browser app.

Q: I have concerns or questions regarding privacy and Quests.

A: ​​We encourage you to review Discord’s Privacy Policy for further details about how we use your information. You can head over to our Data Used for In-Game Rewards article, to learn more about how we use data for Quests.

Q: Why am I unable to complete Quest while using Linux?

A: Currently, Quest rewards are unable to be earned on Linux devices.

However, our team is looking into a resolution for this issue so that earning Quest rewards is possible on Linux in the future. For now, we recommend completing the requirement on a different operating system, like Windows or MacOS.

Our support team does not have an exact ETA for when a solution will be available, and won't be able to provide any status updates on this specific issue.

Q: I no longer see a Quest. Where did it go?

A: Quests are typically only available for a limited amount of time. If you no longer see a Quest in the Quest Home (Discovery > Quests) or in-app notifications about the quest, the period to complete and claim the reward for the Quest is over.
